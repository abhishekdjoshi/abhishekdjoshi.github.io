Ketu Gayatri Mantra
केतु गायत्री मंत्र


This is the lyrics of Ketu Gayatri Mantra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Ketu Gayatri Mantra in English. The Ketu Gayatri Mantra is read to please Lord Ketu.




English

Om Gada Hastaya Vidmahe,
Amrute Shaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥

Om Padma Putraya Vidmahe,
Amrute Shaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥

Om Chitra Varnaya Vidhmahe,
Sarpa Roopaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥

Om Ashwa Dhwajaya Vidmahe,
Shula Hastaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥

Om Kem Ketuve Namah. ॥

Om Sram Srim Sah Ketuve Namah. ॥

Om Ketum Krunvan Ketuve,
Pesho Marya Apesh Se,
Sumush Adbhiraja Yathah. ॥

Palash Pushp Samkasham,
Taaraka Graham Astakam,
Roudram Roudra Atmakam Ghoram,
Tum Ketum Pranamam Yaham. ॥

Devanagari Script

ऊं गदाहस्ताय विद्मिहे, अमृतेशाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो: केतु: प्रचोदयात ॥

ऊँ पद्म पुत्राय विद्महे, अमृतेशाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो केतु प्रचोदयात् ॥

ऊं चित्रवर्णाय विद्महे, सरपुरोपाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो: केतु: प्रचोदयात ॥

ऊँ अश्वा ध्वाजाय विद्महे, शूला हुताया धीमहि ।
तन्नो: केतु: प्रचोदयात ॥

ऊं कें केतवे नम: ॥

ऊँ स्त्रां स्त्रीं स्त्रौं स: केतवे नम: ॥

ऊँ केतुं कृण्वन्नकेतवे पेशो मर्या अपेश से ।
सुमुषद्भिरजायथा: ॥

पलाशपुष्पसंकाशं तारकाग्रहमस्तकम् ।
रौद्रं रौद्रात्मकं घोरं तं केतुं प्रणमाम्यहम् ॥

Meaning in English

Om Gada Hastaya Vidmahe,
Amrute Shaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him who has a gada (a kind of weapon, which is mace with stone head) in his hand, he, who can give amrit, a sweet nectar for vitality and longevity. Lord Ketu give me higher intellect, and Lord Ketu, illuminate my mind and understanding.

Om Padma Putraya Vidmahe,
Amrute Shaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him who is son of Goddess Padma, and, he, who can give amrit, a sweet nectar for vitality and longevity. Lord Ketu give me higher intellect, and Lord Ketu, illuminate my mind and understanding.

Om Chitra Varnaya Vidhmahe,
Sarpa Roopaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him who can only be described through a magnificent painting and drawing, he, who is like snake. Lord Ketu give me higher intellect, and Lord Ketu, illuminate my mind and understanding.

Om Ashwa Dhwajaya Vidmahe,
Shula Hastaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Ketu-hu Pracho Dayaat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him who has a horse in his flag, he, who has a trident in his hand. Lord Ketu give me higher intellect, and Lord Ketu, illuminate my mind and understanding.

Om Kem Ketuve Namah. ॥
Om, I bow down to Lord Ketu.

Om Sram Srim Sah Ketuve Namah. ॥
Om, I bow down to Lord Ketu

Om Ketum Krunvan Ketuve,
Pesho Marya Apesh Se,
Sumush Adbhiraja Yathah. ॥
Om, I bow down to Lord Ketu. I pray to you Lord Ketu not to trouble me a lot in life, I will do all I can to please you. Please give me your blessings.

Palash Pushp Samkasham,
Taaraka Graham Astakam.
Roudram Roudra Atmakam Ghoram,
Tum Ketum Pranamam Yaham. ॥
I bow down to Lord Ketu, who has appearance like a palaasha flower, who makes the star-eyed constellation a sight to behold.
