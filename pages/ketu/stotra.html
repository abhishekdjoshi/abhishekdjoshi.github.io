Ketu Stotra
केतु स्तोत्र


English

Ketu-hu Kaalah Kaliyata,
Dhumra Ketur Vivarnak.
Lok Ketur Maha Ketu-hu,
Sarva Ketur Bhaya Pradah. ॥1॥

Roudro Rudra Priyo Rudhrah,
Krur Karma Sugandhrak.
Phalash Dhoom Sankash Aschitra,
Yagyo Pavit Dhrik. ॥2॥

Tara Gana Vimardo Cha,
Jaimi Neyo Grihadhipah.
Pancha Vinshati Namani,
Keturyah Satatam Pathet. ॥3॥

Tasya Nashyanti,
Badhasch Sarvah Ketu Prasadatah.
Dhana Dhanya Pashunam Cha,
Bhaved Vridwirn Sanshayah. ॥4॥





Devanagari Script

केतु: काल: कलयिता धूम्रकेतुर्विवर्णक: ।
लोककेतुर्महाकेतु: सर्वकेतुर्भयप्रद: ॥1॥

रौद्रो रूद्रप्रियो रूद्र: क्रूरकर्मा सुगन्ध्रक् ।
फलाशधूमसंकाशश्चित्रयज्ञोपवीतधृक् ॥2॥

तारागणविमर्दो च जैमिनेयो ग्रहाधिप: ।
पंचविंशति नामानि केतुर्य: सततं पठेत् ॥3॥

तस्य नश्यंति बाधाश्चसर्वा: केतुप्रसादत: ।
धनधान्यपशूनां च भवेद् व्रद्विर्नसंशय: ॥4॥





Meaning in English

Ketu-hu Kaalah Kaliyata,
Dhumra Ketur Vivarnak.
Lok Ketur Maha Ketu-hu,
Sarva Ketur Bhaya Pradah. ॥1॥
Ketu, he who causes death and he who makes time, smoky headed one. One who is multi coloured, who is head of people, a great head. One who has only head, one who torments the Sun.

Roudro Rudra Priyo Rudhrah,
Krur Karma Sugandhrak.
Phalash Dhoom Sankash Aschitra,
Yagyo Pavit Dhrik. ॥2॥
He who is angry, he who likes Lord Shiva, he who is fierce. He who does cruel deeds, and he who is seen by pleasant smell. One who resembles the smoke of husk, one who is seen wearing sacred thread.

Tara Gana Vimardo Cha,
Jaimi Neyo Grihadhipah.
Pancha Vinshati Namani,
Keturyah Satatam Pathet. ॥3॥
One who troubles star groups, one who belongs to the clan of Jaimini. One who is the lord of all planets, one who is Lord Ganesha. One who is the lord of obstacles, one who destroys disease to poison. One who has forsaken everything, one who is wise. And one who makes pilgrimages to sacred places happen. If these twenty five names of Ketu are read then all the troubles of that person will come to an end.

Tasya Nashyanti,
Badhasch Sarvah Ketu Prasadatah.
Dhana Dhanya Pashunam Cha,
Bhaved Vridwirn Sanshayah. ॥4॥
Due to the grace of Ketu all his troubles will come to an end, and without doubt his wealth, cereals and animals will increase.
