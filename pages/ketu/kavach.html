Ketu Kavach
केतु कवच


This is the lyrics of Ketu Kavach Stotra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Ketu Kavach Stotra in English. The Ketu Kavach Stotra or Stotram is read to please Lord Ketu.




English

Asya Shri Ketu Kavach Stotram Mantrasya,
Triyambak Rishi-hi.
Anushtup Chandah, Ketur Devata,
Kam Bijam, Namah Shakti-hi.
Ketu Riti Kilkam,
Ketu Prityartham Jape Viniyogah.

Kethum Karala Vadanam,
Chithra Varnam Kireetinam,
Pranamami Sada Kethum,
Dwajaakaaram Graheswaram. ॥1॥

Chithra Varna Sira Pathu,
Bhalam Dhoomra Samudhyuthi,
Pathu Nethre Pingalaksha,
Shruti May Raktha Lochana. ॥2॥

Granam Pathu Swarnabha,
Schibukam Simhikasutha,
Pathum Cha Kandam May Kethu,
Skandou Pathu Grahadhipa. ॥3॥

Hasthou Pathu Sura Shreshta,
Kukshim Pathu Maha Graha,
Simhasana Katim Pathu,
Madhyam Pathu Mahasura. ॥4॥

Ooru Pathu Maha Shirsho,
Januni May Athi Kopana,
Pathu Padhou Cha May,
Kroora Sarvangam Nara Pingala. ॥5॥

Ya Idham Kavacham Divyam,
Sarva Roga Vinasanam,
Sarva Shathru Vinasam Cha,
Dharanad Vijayi Bhaved. ॥6॥

॥ Iti Brahmanda Purane,
Kethu Kavacham Sampurnam. ॥

Devanagari Script

अस्य श्रीकेतुकवचस्तोत्रमंत्रस्य त्र्यंबक ऋषिः ।
अनुष्टप् छन्दः । केतुर्देवता । कं बीजं । नमः शक्तिः ।
केतुरिति कीलकम् । केतुप्रीत्यर्थं जपे विनियोगः ॥

केतुं करालवदनं चित्रवर्णं किरीटिनम् ।
प्रणमामि सदा केतुं ध्वजाकारं ग्रहेश्वरम् ॥1॥

चित्रवर्णः शिरः पातु भालं धूम्रसमद्युतिः ।
पातु नेत्रे पिङ्गलाक्षः श्रुती मे रक्तलोचनः ॥2॥

घ्राणं पातु सुवर्णाभश्चिबुकं सिंहिकासुतः ।
पातु कण्ठं च मे केतुः स्कन्धौ पातु ग्रहाधिपः ॥3॥

हस्तौ पातु सुरश्रेष्ठः कुक्षिं पातु महाग्रहः ।
सिंहासनः कटिं पातु मध्यं पातु महासुरः ॥4॥

ऊरू पातु महाशीर्षो जानुनी मेऽतिकोपनः ।
पातु पादौ च मे क्रूरः सर्वाङ्गं नरपिङ्गलः ॥5॥

य इदं कवचं दिव्यं सर्वरोगविनाशनम् ।
सर्वशत्रुविनाशं च धारणाद्विजयी भवेत् ॥6॥

॥ इति श्रीब्रह्माण्डपुराणे केतुकवचं सम्पूर्णम् ॥

Meaning in English

Kethum Karala Vadanam,
Chithra Varnam Kireetinam,
Pranamami Sada Kethum,
Dwajaakaaram Graheswaram. ॥1॥
I always salute Ketu, who has face filled with horror, who is of multiple colors, has a crown, and who is shaped like a flag and is the lord of planets.

Chithra Varna Sira Pathu,
Bhalam Dhoomra Samudhyuthi,
Pathu Nethre Pingalaksha,
Shruti May Raktha Lochana. ॥2॥
Let the multicolored one protect my head, let my hair be protected by he who produces smoke, let my eyes bye protected by one with reddish brown eye, and let what I hear be protected by he who has red blood eyes.

Granam Pathu Swarnabha,
Schibukam Simhikasutha,
Pathum Cha Kandam May Kethu,
Skandou Pathu Grahadhipa. ॥3॥
Let my smell be protected by him who shines like gold, let my chin be protected by the son of Goddess Simhika, let my neck and feet be protected by Ketu, And let the lord of planets protect my shoulder.

Hasthou Pathu Sura Shreshta,
Kukshim Pathu Maha Graha,
Simhasana Katim Pathu,
Madhyam Pathu Mahasura. ॥4॥
Let my arms be protected by the chief of devas, let the great planet protect my belly, let my hip be protected by he who sits on the throne, and let the great asura protect my middle.

Ooru Pathu Maha Shirsho,
Januni May Athi Kopana,
Pathu Padhou Cha May,
Kroora Sarvangam Nara Pingala. ॥5॥
Let my thighs be protected by the great head, let my knees be protected by him with great anger, let my feet be protected by the cruel one, and let all my parts of body be protected by the reddish brown God.

Ya Idham Kavacham Divyam,
Sarva Roga Vinasanam,
Sarva Shathru Vinasam Cha,
Dharanad Vijayi Bhaved. ॥6॥
This holy armour destroys all diseases, destroys all enemies and once worn will make you victorious.

॥ Iti Brahmanda Purane,
Kethu Kavacham Sampurnam. ॥
Thus ends the armour of Ketu from Brahmanda Purana.
