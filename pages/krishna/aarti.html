Meaning in English

Aarti Kunj Bihari Ki,
Shri Girdhar Krishna Murari Ki.
Gale Mein Baijanti Mala,
Bajave Murali Madhur Bala.
We sing this aarti to Lord Krishna (Kunj Bihari), who is Giridhara who roams in the gardens, who has a garland from Lord Vishnu around his neck, and who as a child plays a melodious flute.

Shravan Mein Kundal Jhalakala,
Nand Ke Anand Nandlala.
Gagan Sam Ang Kanti Kali,
Radhika Chamak Rahi Aali.
You are the one who has shining hoops in his ears, and is a dear one of Nanda, and one who is a cloud of happiness and the moon for the village of Vraja, and a dear to Radhika.

Latan Mein Thadhe Banamali
Bhramar Si Alak, Kasturi Tilak,
Chandra Si Jhalak
Lalit Chavi Shyama Pyari Ki.
You are the one who has a body like clouds and a dark complexion, Who has a shining friend, and who has dark locks of hair, a wild turmeric mark on his forehead, and whose glance is like the moon, and the one who is dear one of Shyama.

Shri Girdhar Krishna Murari Ki.

Kanakmay Mor Mukut Bilse,
Devata Darsan Ko Tarse
Gagan So Suman Raasi Barse.
You are the one who has a gold crown of peacock feathers, who the demi-gods flock to get a glimpse of, and the one who is showered with flowers from the sky.

Baje Murchang, Madhur Mridang,
Gwaalin Sang.
Atual Rati Gop Kumaari Ki.
You are the one for who the harp and drums play when you are with the cow maids, you are the one who has immeasurable charm, and you are the who is the child of Gopa.

Shri Girdhar Krishna Murari Ki.

Jahaan Te Pragat Bhayi Ganga,

Smaran Te Hot Moh Bhanga.
You are the one whose feet Ganga wants to touch and flow on, the same river Ganga which washes away all the dirt from within us and the same river Ganga which destroys desire.

It's the same rive Ganga that is matted in Lord Shiva’s hair. Ganga is eager to touch you feet and glow where you feet lands.

The flowers are blooming on the banks of the Yamuna. The flutes are playing in Vrindavan. There are cows, milkmen and milkmaids everywhere.

The moon is smiling its moonlight. The shackles of life are destroyed upon hearing the songs of Shri Krishna and you are the one who destroys the grief of the weak.
