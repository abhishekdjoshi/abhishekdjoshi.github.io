Krishna Nandkumar Ashtakam
श्री कृष्ण नन्दकुमार अष्टकम


This is the lyrics of Krishna Nandkumar Ashtakam in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Krishna Nandkumar Ashtakam in English. The Krishna Nandkumar Ashtakam is read to please Lord Krishna.




English

Sundara Gopaalam Ura Vana Maalam Nayana Vishaalam Duhkha Haram,
Vrndaavana Chandram Aananda Kandam Parama Anandam Dharanni Dharam,
Vallabha Ghanashyaamam Purnna Kaamam Atya Abhiramam Priti Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥1॥

Sundara Varija Vadanam Nirjita Madanam Ananda Sadanam Mukuta Dharam,
Gunja Kriti Haaram Vipina Vihaaram Paramodaram Chira Haram,
Vallabha Patta Piitam Krita Upavitam Kara Navanitam Vibudha Varam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥2॥

Shobhita Mukha Dhulam Yamunaa Kulam Nipata Atulam Sukhada Taram,
Mukha Mandita Rennum Charita Dhenum Vadita Venum Madhura Suram,
Vallabham Ati Vimalam Shubha Pada Kamalam Nakha Ruchi Amalam Timira Haram,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥3॥

Shira Mukuta Sudesham Kunchita Kesham Natavara Vesham Kama Varam,
Maayaa Krita Manujam Haladhara Anujam Pratihata Danujam Bhaara Haram,
Vallabha Vraja Paalam Subhaga Suchalam Hitam Anukalam Bhava Varam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥4॥

Indivara Bhasam Prakata Surasam Kusuma Vikasam Vamshi Dharam,
Hrita Manmatha Manam Rupa Nidhaanam Krita Kala Gaanam Chita Haram,
Vallabha Mridu Hasam Kunja Nivasam Vividha Vilasam Keli Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥5॥

Ati Para Pravinam Palita Dinam Bhakta Adhinam Karma Karam,
Mohanam Ati Dhiram Phani Bala Viram Hata Para Viram Tarala Taram,
Vallabha Vraja Ramanam Varija Vadanam Haladhara Shamanam Shaila Dharam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥6॥

Jaladhara Dyuti Angam Lalita Tribhangam Bahu Krita Rangam Rasika Varam,
Gokula Parivaaram Madana Akaram Kunja Viharam Guddha Taram,
Valabha Vraja Chandram Subhaga Suchandam Krita Anandam Bhranti Haram,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥7॥

Vandita Yuga Charanam Pavana Karanam Jagad Uddharanam Vimala Dharam,
Kaaliya Shira Gamanam Krita Phani Namanam Ghatita Yamanam Mridula Taram,
Valabha Duhkha Haranam Nirmala Charanam Asharana Sharanam Mukti Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥8॥

Meaning in English

Sundara Gopaalam Ura Vana Maalam Nayana Vishaalam Duhkha Haram,
Vrndaavana Chandram Aananda Kandam Parama Anandam Dharanni Dharam,
Vallabha Ghanashyaamam Purnna Kaamam Atya Abhiramam Priti Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥1॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, who is the beautiful Gopala of Vrindavana, with vanamala, a garland of forest flowers, hanging on His chest and with large eyes captivating everyone. Nandkumar whose very form removes the sufferings of His devotees, who is the bright moon of Vrindavana, and a source of immense joy. Nandkumar is the embodiment of supreme bliss Himself and bears the earth within Him. The most beloved Ghanashyama, one who is dark coloured, who is self-satisfied by being absorbed within His own divine nature, and extremely pleasing, inspiring love and affection from all. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Sundara Varija Vadanam Nirjita Madanam Ananda Sadanam Mukuta Dharam,
Gunja Kriti Haaram Vipina Vihaaram Paramodaram Chira Haram,
Vallabha Patta Piitam Krita Upavitam Kara Navanitam Vibudha Varam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥2॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, who has a beautiful face like the lotus flower which subdues the pride of even Madana, the god of love, in beauty, who is an abode of joy, and who wears a crown on His head, who wears a garland made of gunja berries and wanders in the groves with the gopis. Nandkumar is extremely generous and also extremely playful, stealing the Clothes of the Gopis. Nandkumar is beloved of all and loves to wear yellow garments. Nandkumar wears the sacred thread, who stole butter during childhood and held it in His hand and who is the supreme God. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Shobhita Mukha Dhulam Yamunaa Kulam Nipata Atulam Sukhada Taram,
Mukha Mandita Rennum Charita Dhenum Vadita Venum Madhura Suram,
Vallabham Ati Vimalam Shubha Pada Kamalam Nakha Ruchi Amalam Timira Haram,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥3॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, whose face is adorned with the dust of the shore of Yamuna river in Vrindavana, such a matchless adornment of dust gives great joy to the devotees. Nandkumar, who with the adornment of dust on His face, wanders about with the cows in Vrindavana playing melodious tunes in His flute. Nandkumar is the beloved of the devotees and stainless pure divinity, whose lotus feet is a source of auspicious blessings, and the splendour of whose pure toe-nails remove the darkness within the heart of devotees. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Shira Mukuta Sudesham Kunchita Kesham Natavara Vesham Kama Varam,
Maayaa Krita Manujam Haladhara Anujam Pratihata Danujam Bhaara Haram,
Vallabha Vraja Paalam Subhaga Suchalam Hitam Anukalam Bhava Varam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥4॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, who has a crown well-decorating His head, from which curly locks flow down. Nandkumar, who has assumed the dress of a dancing actor and the beauty of that form excels the God of love. Nandkumar has assumed a human form through His power of Maya, and became the younger brother of Haladhara (Balaram). Nandkumar checked the demons and removed the weight of sins from the earth. Nandkumar is the beloved protector of Vraja, bringing good fortune to the people and guiding them well. Nandkumar is always a benefactor of everyone and feels deeply for His devotees. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Indivara Bhasam Prakata Surasam Kusuma Vikasam Vamshi Dharam,
Hrita Manmatha Manam Rupa Nidhaanam Krita Kala Gaanam Chita Haram,
Vallabha Mridu Hasam Kunja Nivasam Vividha Vilasam Keli Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥5॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, who shine like the blossom of a blue lotus and manifests His divine plays like the blossom of the flowers, holding the divine flute in His hand. Nandkumar takes away the pride of Manmatha through His repository of beauty, and playing melodious music with His flute, steals the Heart of all. Nandkumar is extremely dear to all and has a gentle smile, who dwells in the grooves of Vrindavana and plays various divine plays with the Gopis. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Ati Para Pravinam Palita Dinam Bhakta Adhinam Karma Karam,
Mohanam Ati Dhiram Phani Bala Viram Hata Para Viram Tarala Taram,
Vallabha Vraja Ramanam Varija Vadanam Haladhara Shamanam Shaila Dharam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaaram Sarva Sukhasaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥6॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, who is very skillful and wise and protects the distressed, who is under the control of His devotees and performs His duties in a steady manner. Nandkumar is very captivating as well as composed, and who with His elder brother Balavira (i.e. Balarama), the incarnation of serpent Shesha, killed mighty enemies, and who is also extremely Playful. Nandkumar is very dear to the Gopis of Vraja and delights them with His lotus-like face. Nandkumar pacifies the anger of Haladhara (i.e. Balarama) and lifted the Govardhana mountain. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Jaladhara Dyuti Angam Lalita Tribhangam Bahu Krita Rangam Rasika Varam,
Gokula Parivaaram Madana Akaram Kunja Viharam Guddha Taram,
Valabha Vraja Chandram Subhaga Suchandam Krita Anandam Bhranti Haram,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥7॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, whose body has the splendour of rain and clouds and bends in three-parts in playful attitude, who performed many plays and is a great rasika, one Who delight in Rasa or love plays. Nandkumar treats the entire Gokula as a great family, and with his beautiful form which is like Madana, the god of Love, moves about in the grooves of Vraja performing His divine plays. Nandkumar is also very Mysterious. Nandkumar is the beloved moon of Vraja, bringing good fortune to all, and with His alluring form He gives great Joy to all, taking away the delusion and monotony from life. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.

Vandita Yuga Charanam Pavana Karanam Jagad Uddharanam Vimala Dharam,
Kaaliya Shira Gamanam Krita Phani Namanam Ghatita Yamanam Mridula Taram,
Valabha Duhkha Haranam Nirmala Charanam Asharana Sharanam Mukti Karam,
Bhaja Nanda Kumaram Sarva Sukhasaaram Tattva Vicharam Brahma Param. ॥8॥
Salutations to Sri Nandakumar, whose pair of feet is extolled by the devotees, that pair of feet which make everything pure and uplift the world, that pair of feet which has embodied purity itself. Nandkumar moving about over the head of Serpent Kaliya, bent its hood and danced over them, after destroying its deadly bind with his soft and tender body. Nandkumar is the beloved of the devotees and takes away their sorrows, and His pure holy feet gives refuge to those who are without refuge, and finally grants them liberation. Worship that Nandakumar, the essence of all joy, by reflecting on His divine essence, which is, of the nature of supreme Brahman.
