Lakshmi Chalisa
लक्ष्मी चालीस


This is the lyrics of Lakshmi Chalisa in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Lakshmi Chalisa in English. The Lakshmi Chalisa is read to please Goddess Lakshmi.




English

॥ Doha ॥
Maatu Lakshmi Kari Kripa, Hridaya Mein Vaas
Manokamna Siddha Kari, Paruvahu Meri Aas ॥1॥

॥ Sortha ॥
Yahi Mor Ardas, Hath Jod Vinati Karun
Sab Vidhi Karau Suvas, Jai Janani Jagadambika ॥2॥

॥ Chaupai ॥
Sindu Suta Main Sumirau Tohi
Gyan Buddhi Vidya Do Mohi ॥3॥

Tum Samaan Nahi Koi Upkari
Sab Vidhi Puravahu Aas Hamari ॥4॥

Sabaki Tum Hi Ho Avalamba ॥5॥

Tum Hi Ho Sab Ghat Ghat Vaasi
Vinati Yahi Hamari Khaasi ॥6॥

Kripa Karau Jaga Janani Bhavani ॥8॥

Sudhi Lijai Aparadh Bisari ॥9॥

Kripa Drishti Chitavavo Mam Ori
Jaga Janani Vinati Sun Mori ॥10॥

Sankat Haro Hamari Mata ॥11॥

Kshirasindhu Jab Vishnu Mathayo

Shanka Dil Mein Kabhi Na Lavai ॥27॥

Devanagari Script

॥ दोहा ॥
मातु लक्ष्मी करि कृपा, करो हृदय में वास ।
मनोकामना सिद्ध करि, परुवहु मेरी आस ॥1॥

॥ सोरठा ॥
यही मोर अरदास, हाथ जोड़ विनती करुं ।
सब विधि करौ सुवास, जय जननि जगदम्बिका ॥2॥

॥ चौपाई ॥
सिन्धु सुता मैं सुमिरौ तोही ।
ज्ञान बुद्धि विद्या दो मोही ॥3॥

तुम समान नहिं कोई उपकारी ।
सब विधि पुरवहु आस हमारी ॥4॥

जय जय जगत जननि जगदम्बा ।
सबकी तुम ही हो अवलम्बा ॥5॥

तुम ही हो सब घट घट वासी ।
विनती यही हमारी खासी ॥6॥

जगजननी जय सिन्धु कुमारी ।
दीनन की तुम हो हितकारी ॥7॥

विनवौं नित्य तुमहिं महारानी ।
कृपा करौ जग जननि भवानी ॥8॥

केहि विधि स्तुति करौं तिहारी ।
सुधि लीजै अपराध बिसारी ॥9॥

कृपा दृष्टि चितववो मम ओरी ।
जगजननी विनती सुन मोरी ॥10॥

ज्ञान बुद्धि जय सुख की दाता ।
संकट हरो हमारी माता ॥11॥

क्षीरसिन्धु जब विष्णु मथायो ।
चौदह रत्न सिन्धु में पायो ॥12॥

चौदह रत्न में तुम सुखरासी ।
सेवा कियो प्रभु बनि दासी ॥13॥

जब जब जन्म जहां प्रभु लीन्हा ।
रुप बदल तहं सेवा कीन्हा ॥14॥

स्वयं विष्णु जब नर तनु धारा ।
लीन्हेउ अवधपुरी अवतारा ॥15॥

तब तुम प्रगट जनकपुर माहीं ।
सेवा कियो हृदय पुलकाहीं ॥16॥

अपनाया तोहि अन्तर्यामी ।
विश्व विदित त्रिभुवन की स्वामी ॥17॥

तुम सम प्रबल शक्ति नहीं आनी ।
कहं लौ महिमा कहौं बखानी ॥18॥

मन क्रम वचन करै सेवकाई ।
मन इच्छित वांछित फल पाई ॥19॥

तजि छल कपट और चतुराई ।
पूजहिं विविध भांति मनलाई ॥20॥

और हाल मैं कहौं बुझाई ।
जो यह पाठ करै मन लाई ॥21॥

ताको कोई कष्ट नोई ।
मन इच्छित पावै फल सोई ॥22॥

त्राहि त्राहि जय दुःख निवारिणि ।
त्रिविध ताप भव बंधन हारिणी ॥23॥

जो चालीसा पढ़ै पढ़ावै ।
ध्यान लगाकर सुनै सुनावै ॥24॥

ताकौ कोई न रोग सतावै ।
पुत्र आदि धन सम्पत्ति पावै ॥25॥

पुत्रहीन अरु सम्पति हीना ।
अन्ध बधिर कोढ़ी अति दीना ॥26॥

विप्र बोलाय कै पाठ करावै ।
शंका दिल में कभी न लावै ॥27॥

पाठ करावै दिन चालीसा ।
ता पर कृपा करैं गौरीसा ॥28॥

सुख सम्पत्ति बहुत सी पावै ।
कमी नहीं काहू की आवै ॥29॥

बारह मास करै जो पूजा ।
तेहि सम धन्य और नहिं दूजा ॥30॥

प्रतिदिन पाठ करै मन माही ।
उन सम कोइ जग में कहुं नाहीं ॥31॥

बहुविधि क्या मैं करौं बड़ाई ।
लेय परीक्षा ध्यान लगाई ॥32॥

करि विश्वास करै व्रत नेमा ।
होय सिद्ध उपजै उर प्रेमा ॥33॥

जय जय जय लक्ष्मी भवानी ।
सब में व्यापित हो गुण खानी ॥34॥

तुम्हरो तेज प्रबल जग माहीं ।
तुम सम कोउ दयालु कहुं नाहिं ॥35॥

मोहि अनाथ की सुधि अब लीजै ।
संकट काटि भक्ति मोहि दीजै ॥36॥

भूल चूक करि क्षमा हमारी ।
दर्शन दजै दशा निहारी ॥37॥

बिन दर्शन व्याकुल अधिकारी ।
तुमहि अछत दुःख सहते भारी ॥38॥

नहिं मोहिं ज्ञान बुद्धि है तन में ।
सब जानत हो अपने मन में ॥39॥

रुप चतुर्भुज करके धारण ।
कष्ट मोर अब करहु निवारण ॥40॥

केहि प्रकार मैं करौं बड़ाई ।
ज्ञान बुद्धि मोहि नहिं अधिकाई ॥41॥

॥ दोहा ॥
त्राहि त्राहि दुख हारिणी, हरो वेगि सब त्रास ।
जयति जयति जय लक्ष्मी, करो शत्रु को नाश ॥42॥

रामदास धरि ध्यान नित, विनय करत कर जोर ।
मातु लक्ष्मी दास पर, करहु दया की कोर ॥43॥





Meaning in English

Maatu Lakshmi Kari Kripa, Hridaya Mein Vaas
Manokamna Siddha Kari, Paruvahu Meri Aas ॥1॥
Oh divine mother Lakshmi bestow your grace upon me. Reside in my heart and pacify the many urges and desires that are in my mind. This is my humble prayer.

Yahi Mor Ardas, Hath Jod Vinati Karun
Sab Vidhi Karau Suvas, Jai Janani Jagadambika ॥2॥
Sindu Suta Main Sumirau Tohi
Gyan Buddhi Vidya Do Mohi ॥3॥
Tum Samaan Nahi Koi Upkari
Sab Vidhi Puravahu Aas Hamari ॥4॥
Oh Eternal being of all existence, born from the ocean of your thought, I beseech you. Fill me with the grandeur of intelligence, knowledge and wisdom that is you. Anointing us with your divine shakti (energy), you grant us many gifts. Within the three worlds there is none as charitable as you, It is only you Devi that can fulfill all desires.

Jai Jai Jagat Janani Jagadamba
Sabaki Tum Hi Ho Avalamba ॥5॥
Tum Hi Ho Sab Ghat Ghat Vaasi
Vinati Yahi Hamari Khaasi ॥6॥
Victory, Victory to you, the Supreme Self, for you are above all material cause. Mere words cannot fully describe the depth of your infinite love for your creation. Oh Eternal Energy you reside in the many expressions of your devotees, this is our humble prayer.

Jagajanani Jai Sindhu Kumari
Dinan ki Tum Ho Hitakari ॥7॥
Vinavaun Nitya Tumahin Maharani
Kripa Karau Jaga Janani Bhavani ॥8॥
Oh all pervading truth, you have taken form out the ocean at your will. Bestowing gifts to the mankind that are deserving. It is you who sanctify and deliver the poor. Oh Self Sustaining reality, protect us within the portals of your grace. This is our humble prayer to you in utmost supplication, Oh Devi.

Kehi Vidhi Stuti Karaun Tihari
Sudhi Lijai Aparadh Bisari ॥9॥
Kripa Drishti Chitavavo Mam Ori
Jaga Janani Vinati Sun Mori ॥10॥
In what way can I extol Thee glory? With a repentant heart I confess that I know not of how to sing your praises. Though I sing of your prayers, my human nature remains. Forgive me of my faults, through your compassionate eyes nurture our existence. Devi take us into your protective care and heed our cries, you are the source of our salvation.

Gyan Buddhi Jai Sukh Ki Data
Sankat Haro Hamari Mata ॥11॥
Kshirasindhu Jab Vishnu Mathayo
Chaudah Ratna Sindhu Mein Payo ॥12॥
The virtues of knowledge, wisdom, victory and happiness are bestowed upon those of steadfast faith and devotion and to whom surrender totally to you. Please remove the many faults that plague us. You were revealed as the self, when Shri Vishnu ordered the churning of the milky ocean amongst fourteen jewels.

Chaudah Ratna Mein Tum Sukharasi
Seva Kiyo Prabu Bani Dasi ॥13॥
Jab Jab Janma Jahan Prabhu Linha
Roop Badal Tahan Seva Kinha ॥14॥
Of those fourteen jewels you are the prized gem of them all. Revealing in the oneness of existence, you joyously became a servant of God. From time to time you take birth as the Lord, you also embody your self in human form as his eternal companion and selflessly serves him.

Svayan Vishnu Jab Nar Tanu Dhara
Linheu Avadhapuri Avatara ॥15॥
Tab Tum Pragat Janakapur Mahin
Seva Kiyo Hridaya Pulakahin ॥16॥
Shree Vishnu took the form of a man and he appeared as the Avatar of Shree Rama in Ayodhya. It is at that very moment you manifested yourself in Janakpur to serve him and fill the hearts of your devotees with immense joy as they rejoiced in the play of consciousness.

Apanaya Tohi Antaryami
Vishva Vidit Tribhuvan Ki Swami ॥17॥
Tum Sam Prabal Shakti Nahin Aani
Kahan Lau Mahima Kahaun Bakhani ॥18॥
For he is the lord of the three worlds and is the knower of the hearts of all, he joyously embraced you knowing you to be no different to him. Your divine power cannot be estimated by any nor can your grace be described in its grandeur.

Man Kram Vachan Karai Sevakai
Man Ichchhita Vanchhit Phal Pai ॥19॥
Taji Chhal Kapat Aur Chaturai
Pujahin Vividh Bhanti Man Lai ॥20॥
Through thought, word and deed I worship you in total surrender that may the fruit of my surrender be fulfilled. To be absorbed in your grace I must cast aside guile, deceit ad dishonesty, constantly worshipping you with utmost devotion.

Aur Haal Main Kahaun Bujhai
Jo Yah Paath Karai Man Lai ॥21॥
Tako Koi Kashta Noi
Man Ichchhita Pavai Phal Soi ॥22॥
How can one glorify you ? Through steadfast surrender is the only way I will truly understand of your grace. It is only then there will be no difficulty and the fruits of our surrender will be fulfilled.

Trahi Trahi Jai Dukh Nivarini
Trividh Taap Bhav Bandhan Harini ॥23॥
Jo Chalisa Pade Padave
Dhyana Lagakar Sunai Sunavai ॥24॥
Save me! Save me! For you are the remover of all pains and the destroyer of all fears and three kinds of difficulties. Those who read and listen of your praises and encourage others to rejoice in your praise suffer no difficulties.

Takau Koi Na Rog Satavai
Putra Aadi Dhan Sampatti Pavai ॥25॥
Putrahin Aru Sampatti Hina
Andh Badhir Kodhi Ati Dina ॥26॥
Through steadfast devotion they will be blessed with children, wealth and happiness amongst all. Those who bear no children, who are sad, the blind, the deaf, the poor and low caste.

Vipra Bolaya Kai Paath Karavai
Shanka Dil Mein Kabhi Na Lavai ॥27॥
Paath Karavai Din Chalisa
Ta Par Kripa Karain Gaurisa ॥28॥
They shall engage with the learnt and in the full richness of devotion with all doubts casted away from their hearts shall worship you. Praises of you should be read and gloried with reverence and total surrender. Through the anointing of your shakti all will be blessed with your grace.

Sukh Sampatti Bahut Si Pavai
Kami Nahin Kaahu Ki Avai ॥29॥
Barah Maas Karai Jo Puja
Tehi Sam Dhanya Aur Nahin Duja ॥30॥
Resplendent health and immense happiness will flourish in abundance. None will suffer of any kind of unhappiness or sorrow. Wealth both spiritually and materially of those unwavering in their devotion and surrender to you will be multiplied unfold.

Pratidin Paath Karai Man Mahin
Un Sam Koi Jag Mein Kahun Naahin ॥31॥
Bahuvidhi Kya Mai Karaun Badai
Leya Pariksha Dhyana Lagai ॥32॥
Those who sing and glorify you each day will be of unparallel glory and will enjoy of your incomparable grace. I surrender to you and meditate upon your divine presence in all that I embark on in diverse ways each day.

Kari Vishvas Karai Vrat Nema
Hoy Siddha Upajai Ur Prema ॥33॥
Jai Jai Jai Lakshmi Bhavani
Sab Mein Vyapita Ho Gun Khaani ॥34॥
Those who worship you in thought, word and deed receive many gifts from you and experience tremendous love through your grace. Your love fill all of creation. Glory, glory, glory be unto you resplendent Lakshmi Devi, for you are the storehouse of knowledge. May your anointing grace manifest within your devotees.

Tumharo Tej Prabal Jag Mahin
Tum Sam Kou Dayalu Kahun Naahin ॥35॥
Mohi Anath Ki Sudhi Ab Lijai
Sankat Kaati Bhakti Mohi Dijai ॥36॥
Your glory is indescribable in this world. There is none that is so kind and merciful to sinners as you Devi. In your protective care take this orphan who needs a mother. Remove all of our difficulties and grant us constant devotion to you.

Bhul Chuk Kari Kshama Hamari
Darshan Dajai Dasha Nihari ॥37॥
Bin Darshan Vyakul Adhikari
Tumahi Achhat Dukh Sahate Bhaari ॥38॥
Forgive us of our mistakes, deliver us from the ocean of suffering which binds us. May your divine grace be showered upon us. I beg to dwell in your grace for eternity. Only through your divine blessing will all our difficulties vanish.

Nahin Mohin Gyan Buddhi Hai Tan Mein
Sab Janat Ho Apane Man Mein ॥39॥
Roop Chaturbhuja Karake Dharan
Kashta Mor Ab Karahu Nivaran ॥40॥
Kehi Prakar Main Karaun Badai
Gyan Buddhi Mohin Nahin Adhikai ॥41॥
Devi you know how ignorant I am in the mind, bless my eyes with your divine form. Destory all my pains and suffering. Lacking in knowledge and intelligence of worshiping thee, I am ignorant to understanding your true greatness.

Trahi Trahi Dukh Harini,Haro Vegi Sab Tras
Jayati Jayati Jai Lakshmi,Karo Shatru Ko Naash ॥42॥
Deliver me, Deliver me, Oh remover of all difficulties. Destroy all the evils that torment me. Glory Glory to you Ma Lakshmi, Destroy the multitudes of enemies that plague me in evil thoughts that beseech my mind.

Ramdas Dhari Dhyan Nit,Vinay Karat Kar Jor
Maatu Lakshmi Daas Par,Karahu Daya Ki Kor ॥43॥
Daily through my unwavering heart filled of faith and love I surrender and meditate upon you. Oh Lakshmi, the eternal self of all existence, bestow your grace on your devotee.
