Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra
महालक्ष्मि अष्टकं स्तोत्र


This is the lyrics of Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra or Stotram in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra or Stotram in English. The Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra or Stotram is read to please Goddess Lakshmi.




English

Namastheasthu Mahamaaye, Sri Pithe Sura-Poojite,
Shankha Chakra Ghadha-Hasthe, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥1॥

Namasthe Garudaarudhe, Kolaasura Bhayankari,
Sarva Paapa Hare Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥2॥

Sarvaghne Sarva-Varade, Sarva-Dhushta Bhayankari,
Sarva Dhukka Haree Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥3॥

Siddhi Buddhi-Pradhe Devi, Bukhti Mukti Pradhaayini,
Mantra Moorte Sadhaa Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥4॥

Aadhyantha-Rahite Devi, Aadhya Shakti Maheshwari,
Yogaghne Yoga-Sambhoote, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥5॥

Sthoola Sookshma Mahaa Raudre, Maha Shakti Mahodare,
Mahaa Paapa Haree Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥6॥

Padma-asana Sthithe Devi, Para-Brahma Swaroopini,
Parameshi Jagan-maataah, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥7॥

Shveta-Ambara-Dhare Devi, Naana Alankara Bhooshite,
Jagat-Sthithe Jagan-Maataah, Maha Lakshmi Namosthuthe ॥8॥

Mahalakshmya Ashtakam Stotram, Yah Pathe Bhakti Maan-arah,
Sarva Siddhi Mavaapnoti Rajyam, Prapnoti Sarvada ॥9॥

Ek-Kaale Pathe Nityam, Maha Paap Vinaashnam,
Dwikalam Yah Pathe Nityam, Dhana Dhanya Samanvit-ah ॥10॥

Trikalam Yah Pathe Nityam, Mahashatru Vinaashnam,
Mahalakshmi Bharve Nityam, Prasanna Varada Shubha ॥11॥

॥Iti Shri Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra॥





Devanagari Script

नमस्तेस्तु महामाये श्रीपीठे सुरपूजिते ।
शङ्खचक्रगदाहस्ते महालक्ष्मि नमोऽस्तुते ॥1॥

नमस्ते गरुडारूढे कोलासुरभयंकरि ।
सर्वपापहरे देवि महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥2॥

सर्वज्ञे सर्ववरदे सर्वदुष्टभयंकरि ।
सर्वदुःखहरे देवि महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥3॥

सिद्धिबुद्धिप्रदे देवि भुक्तिमुक्तिप्रदायिनि ।
मन्त्रमूर्ते सदा देवि महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥4॥

आद्यन्तरहिते देवि आद्यशक्तिमहेश्वरि ।
योगजे योगसम्भूते महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥5॥

स्थूलसूक्ष्ममहारौद्रे महाशक्तिमहोदरे ।
महापापहरे देवि महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥6॥<

पद्मासनस्थिते देवि परब्रह्मस्वरूपिणि ।
परमेशि जगन्मातर्महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥7॥

श्वेताम्बरधरे देवि नानालङ्कारभूषिते ।
जगत्स्थिते जगन्मातर्महालक्ष्मि नमोस्तुते ॥8॥

महालक्ष्म्यष्टकं स्तोत्रं यः पठेद्भक्तिमान्नरः ।
सर्वसिद्धिमवाप्नोति राज्यं प्राप्नोति सर्वदा ॥9॥

एककाले पठेन्नित्यं महापापविनाशनम् ।
द्विकालं यः पठेन्नित्यं धनधान्यसमन्वितः ॥10॥

त्रिकालं यः पठेन्नित्यं महाशत्रुविनाशनम् ।
महालक्ष्मिर्भवेन्नित्यं प्रसन्ना वरदा शुभा ॥11॥

॥इति श्री महालक्ष्मि अष्टकं स्तोत्र॥





Meaning in English

Namastheasthu Mahamaaye, Sri Pithe Sura-Poojite,
Shankha Chakra Ghadha-Hasthe, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥1॥
Salutations to you O Great Maya, one who resides in Sri Chakra and is adored by the Gods, one who holds the conch-shell, the discus and the mace, I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Namasthe Garudaarudhe, Kolaasura Bhayankari,
Sarva Paapa Hare Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥2॥
Salutations to the one who is riding the eagle garuda (Lord Vishnu’s vehicle). To the one, who destroyed the demon Kola Asura. To the one, who can destroy all sins caused by the mind, body and the soul. I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Sarvaghne Sarva-Varade, Sarva-Dhushta Bhayankari,
Sarva Dhukka Haree Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥3॥
To the one, who is aware of everything in the creation, one who can fulfill all desires, one who is the destroyer of all evils, one who removes all miseries. I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Siddhi Buddhi-Pradhe Devi, Bukhti Mukti Pradhaayini,
Mantra Moorte Sadhaa Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥4॥
To the one who bestows success and intelligence. To the one who gives liberation or moksha (salvation). To the one who is an embodiment of all mantras, I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Aadhyantha-Rahite Devi, Aadhya Shakti Maheshwari,
Yogaghne Yoga-Sambhoote, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥5॥
To the one who has no beginning or end, to one who the primordial energy of this cosmic creation. To one who is the divine fire born of all yoga, to the one who is in the minds of the all yogis, I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Sthoola Sookshma Mahaa Raudre, Maha Shakti Mahodare,
Mahaa Paapa Haree Devi, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥6॥
To the one who is the gross and subtle manifestation of Lord Rudra, to that ultimate strength who holds the entire creation within you, to the one who is the greatest energy of the cosmos and to the one who can eliminate all the hurdles of the progress. I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Padma-asana Sthithe Devi, Para-Brahma Swaroopini,
Parameshi Jagan-maataah, Maha Lakshmi Namostuthe ॥7॥
To the one who is seated in the yogic lotus posture, to the one can assume the form of supreme Brahma, to the one who is the supreme and universal mother of the entire creation, I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Shveta-Ambara-Dhare Devi, Naana Alankara Bhooshite,
Jagat-Sthithe Jagan-Maataah, Maha Lakshmi Namosthuthe ॥8॥
One who is clad in pure white clothes (symbol of purity, sanctity and truth), one who is adorned with many ornamentation, one who is the universal divine mother of the entire cosmic genesis of all time and space, and the mother of all creation, I worship you, Sri Mahalakshmi.

Mahalakshmya Ashtakam Stotram, Yah Pathe Bhakti Maan-arah,
Sarva Siddhi Mavaapnoti Rajyam, Prapnoti Sarvada ॥9॥
The devotee who chants this verse of eight stanzas to Goddess Lakshmi gains all success and gains sovereignty at all times.

Ek-Kaale Pathe Nityam, Maha Paap Vinaashnam,
Dwikalam Yah Pathe Nityam, Dhana Dhanya Samanvit-ah ॥10॥
He who always recites once daily, gains destruction of great sin; he who recites twice daily, always is endowed with wealth and food.

Trikalam Yah Pathe Nityam, Mahashatru Vinaashnam,
Mahalakshmi Bharve Nityam, Prasanna Varada Shubha ॥11॥
He who recites it three times daily always gains destruction of great enemies, and Goddess Lakshmi the pure giver of boons, would be always pleased with the person.

॥Iti Shri Mahalakshmi Ashtakam Stotra॥
Here ends Sri Mahalakshmi Ashtakam
