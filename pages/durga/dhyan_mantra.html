Durga Dhyan Mantras
दुर्गा देवी ध्यान मंत्र


This is the lyrics of Durga Dhyan Mantras in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the Durga Dhyan Mantras meaning in English. The Durga Dhyan Mantras is read to please Goddess Durga.




English

Om Jataa Jut Samaa Yukta,
Amardhendu Krit Lakshnam,
Lochan Yatra Sanyuktam Pdmendu,
Sadya Shan Naam.

Ripavah Sankshayam Yaanti,
Kalyaanam Chop Padyate,
Nandate Cha Kulam Punsaam,
Maha Atmayam Mam Srinu Yanamam.

Om Sarvabaadhaa Vinirmukto,
Dhan Dhaanyah Sutaanvitah,
Manushyo Matprasaaden Bhavishyati,
Na Sanshayah Om.

Shanti Karmani,
Swapna Darshane,
Grah Pidasu Chograsu,
Maha Atmayam Srinu Yanamam.

Baal Grah Bhibhutanaam,
Balanam Shanti Karakam,
Sanghat Bhede Cha Nrinam,
Maetri Karan Mutmam.

Sharnagat Deenart,
Paritran Parayaney,
Servasyarti Harey Devi,
Narayani Namostutey.

Sarva Swaroopey Sarweshy,
Sarwa Shakti Samanvitey,
Bhayebhyah Trahi No,
Devi Durge Devi Namostutey.

Rogan Shoshan Pahan Situsht Arushtatu,
Kamaan Saklaan Bhistaan,
Twam Ashritanaam Na Vipanaranam,
Tvama Shritahya Shraytam Prayaanti.

Sarva Badha Prashmanan,
Trilokya Syakhileshwari,
Evamevmev Twaya Karyam Sma,
Dweri Vinashnam.

Sarwa Baadha Vinirumk To,
Dhan Dhanya Sutaan Vitah.

Manushyo Mat Prasaaden,
Bhavishyati Na Sansha Yah.

Dehi Saubhagyam Arogyam,
Dehi Devi Param Sukham.

Rupam Dehi Jayam Dehi,
Yasho Dehi Dwisho Jahi.

Jyanti Mangala Kaali Bhadra,
Kaali Kapalini Durga Kshama,
Shiva Dhatri Swaha,
Swadha Namo Stutey.





Devanagari Script

ॐ जटा जूट समायुक्तमर्धेंन्दु कृत लक्षणाम ।
लोचनत्रय संयुक्तां पद्मेन्दुसद्यशाननाम ॥

रिपव: संक्षयम् यान्ति कल्याणम चोपपद्यते ।
नन्दते च कुलम पुंसाम माहात्म्यम मम श्रृणुयान्मम ॥

ॐ सर्वाबाधा विनिर्मुक्तो, धन धान्यः सुतान्वितः ।
मनुष्यो मत्प्रसादेन भविष्यति न संशयः ॐ ॥

शान्तिकर्मणि सर्वत्र तथा दु:स्वप्नदर्शने ।
ग्रहपीडासु चोग्रासु माहात्म्यं श्रृणुयान्मम ॥

बालग्रहभिभूतानां बालानां शांतिकारकं ।
सङ्घातभेदे च नृणाम मैत्रीकरणमुतमम ॥

शरणागत दीनार्तपरित्राण परायणे ।
सर्वस्यातिहरे देवि नारायण नमोस्तुते ॥

सर्वस्वरूपे सर्वेशे सर्वेशक्तिसमन्विते ।
भयेभ्यस्त्राहि नो देवि दुर्गे देवि नमोऽस्तु ते ॥

रोगनशेषानपहंसि तुष्टा ।
रुष्टा तु कामान् सकलानभीष्टान् ॥

त्वामाश्रितानां न विपन्नराणां ।
त्वमाश्रिता हृयश्रयतां प्रयान्ति ॥

सर्वाबाधा प्रशमनं त्रैलोक्यस्याखिलेश्वरि ।
एवमेव त्वया कार्यमस्मद्दैरिविनाशनम् ॥

सर्वाबाधा विर्निर्मुक्तो धनधान्यसुतान्वित: ।
मनुष्यो मत्प्रसादेन भविष्यति न संशय: ॥

जयन्ती मङ्गला काली भद्रकाली कपालिनी ।
दुर्गा शिवा क्षमा धात्री स्वाहा स्वधा नमोऽस्तु ते ॥





Meaning in English

Om Jataa Jut Samaa Yukta,
Amardhendu Krit Lakshnam,
Lochan Yatra Sanyuktam Pdmendu,
Sadya Shan Naam.
O' Mother! I pray to you. Help me to achieve better concentration in my work and in anything I do. If I lack concentration while studying, help me increase my concentration. Adorations to you the Divine Mother!

Ripavah Sankshayam Yaanti,
Kalyaanam Chop Padyate,
Nandate Cha Kulam Punsaam,
Maha Atmayam Mam Srinu Yanamam.
O' Mother! I pray to you. Provide me protection against my enemies, and help me get rid of enemies and from the people who are jealous of me. Help me in achieving welfare and bliss in my life. I will chant this mantra regularly, help me with my well-being. Adorations to you the Divine Mother!

Om Sarvabaadhaa Vinirmukto,
Dhan Dhaanyah Sutaanvitah,
Manushyo Matprasaaden Bhavishyati,
Na Sanshayah Om.
O' Mother! I pray to you. Provide me with relief from any kind of problem and misery in my life. Help me in acquiring wealth, and create prosperity in my life. Adorations to you the Divine Mother!

Shanti Karmani,
Swapna Darshane,
Grah Pidasu Chograsu,
Maha Atmayam Srinu Yanamam.
O' Mother! I pray to you. Help me to achieve peace in every difficult stage of my life. Help me when I suffer from nightmares and grant me relief everytime I have nightmares. Help me get rid of every kind of negative thoughts and fearful dreams. Adorations to you the Divine Mother!

Baal Grah Bhibhutanaam,
Balanam Shanti Karakam,
Sanghat Bhede Cha Nrinam,
Maetri Karan Mutmam.
O' Mother! I pray to you. Protect my child from spectral problems and negative souls. O' Mother, lay your mercy on my child. Help eliminate any negative energy that is disturbing my child. Adorations to you the Divine Mother!

Sharnagat Deenart,
Paritran Parayaney,
Servasyarti Harey Devi,
Narayani Namostutey.

Sarva Swaroopey Sarweshy,
Sarwa Shakti Samanvitey,
Bhayebhyah Trahi No,
Devi Durge Devi Namostutey.

Rogan Shoshan Pahan Situsht Arushtatu,
Kamaan Saklaan Bhistaan,
Twam Ashritanaam Na Vipanaranam,
Tvama Shritahya Shraytam Prayaanti.

Sarva Badha Prashmanan,
Trilokya Syakhileshwari,
Evamevmev Twaya Karyam Sma,
Dweri Vinashnam.

Sarwa Baadha Vinirumk To,
Dhan Dhanya Sutaan Vitah.

Manushyo Mat Prasaaden,
Bhavishyati Na Sansha Yah.

Dehi Saubhagyam Arogyam,
Dehi Devi Param Sukham.

Rupam Dehi Jayam Dehi,
Yasho Dehi Dwisho Jahi.

Jyanti Mangala Kaali Bhadra,
Kaali Kapalini Durga Kshama,
Shiva Dhatri Swaha,
Swadha Namo Stutey.

You who are perpetually endeavouring to protect the weak and the poor and remove their misery. Oh Narayani, I pray to you.

Oh Goddess Durga, please protect us from all kinds of fear. Oh omnipotent Durga, I pray to you.

Oh Goddess, when you are pleased, remove all ailments and when you are angry, destroy everything that a person desires for. However, those who come to you for sanctuary never have to confront any casastrophy. Instead, such people secure enough merit to provide s helter to others.

Whoever listens to the story of the Goddess during the great Puja that is organised in the winters succeeds in overcoming all obstacles and is blessed wealth and progeny.

Oh Goddess, bless me with good fortune, good health, good looks, success and fame. Oh Vaishnavi, you are the very basis for the world. You have mesmerized the World. When you are pleased with some one you ensure his salvation from the cycle of life and death.

Oh Goddess, you who are known by the names of Mangala, Kali, Bhadra Kali, Kapalinee, Durge, Kshama, Shivaa, Dhatri, Swahaa, Swadha, I pray to you.
