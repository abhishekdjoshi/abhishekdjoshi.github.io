Durga Chalisa | दुर्गा चालीसा


English

Namo Namo Durge Sukh Karani
Namo Namo Ambe Dukh Harani ॥1॥

Nirakar Hay Jyoti Tumhari
Tihon Lok Phaili Ujiyari ॥2॥

Shashi Lalaat Mukh Maha Vishala
Netra Laal Bhru-kuti Vik-rala ॥3॥

Darash Karath Jan Ati Sukh Paave॥4॥

Tum-hi Aadi Sundari Baala ॥6॥

Devanagari Script

नमो नमो दुर्गे सुख करनी ।
नमो नमो अम्बे दुःख हरनी ॥1॥

निराकार है ज्योति तुम्हारी ।
तिहूँ लोक फैली उजियारी ॥2॥

शशि ललाट मुख महाविशाला ।
नेत्र लाल भृकुटि विकराला ॥3॥

रूप मातु को अधिक सुहावे ।
दरश करत जन अति सुख पावे ॥4॥

तुम संसार शक्ति लय कीना ।
पालन हेतु अन्न धन दीना ॥5॥

अन्नपूर्णा हुई जग पाला ।
तुम ही आदि सुन्दरी बाला ॥6॥

प्रलयकाल सब नाशन हारी ।
तुम गौरी शिवशंकर प्यारी ॥7॥

शिव योगी तुम्हरे गुण गावें ।
ब्रह्मा विष्णु तुम्हें नित ध्यावें ॥8॥

रूप सरस्वती को तुम धारा ।
दे सुबुद्धि ऋषि-मुनिन उबारा ॥9॥

धरा रूप नरसिंह को अम्बा ।
प्रगट भईं फाड़कर खम्बा ॥10॥

रक्षा कर प्रह्लाद बचायो ।
हिरण्याक्ष को स्वर्ग पठायो ॥11॥

लक्ष्मी रूप धरो जग माहीं ।
श्री नारायण अंग समाहीं ॥12॥

क्षीरसिन्धु में करत विलासा ।
दयासिन्धु दीजै मन आसा ॥13॥

हिंगलाज में तुम्हीं भवानी ।
महिमा अमित न जात बखानी ॥14॥

मातंगी अरु धूमावति माता ।
भुवनेश्वरी बगला सुख दाता ॥15॥

श्री भैरव तारा जग तारिणी ।
छिन्न भाल भव दुःख निवारिणी ॥16॥

केहरि वाहन सोह भवानी ।
लांगुर वीर चलत अगवानी ॥17॥

कर में खप्पर-खड्ग विराजै ।
जाको देख काल डर भाजे ॥18॥

सोहै अस्त्र और त्रिशूला ।
जाते उठत शत्रु हिय शूला ॥19॥

नगर कोटि में तुम्हीं विराजत ।
तिहुंलोक में डंका बाजत ॥20॥

शुम्भ निशुम्भ दानव तुम मारे ।
रक्तबीज शंखन संहारे ॥21॥

महिषासुर नृप अति अभिमानी ।
जेहि अघ भार मही अकुलानी ॥22॥

रूप कराल कालिका धारा ।
सेन सहित तुम तिहि संहारा ॥23॥

परी गाढ़ सन्तन पर जब-जब ।
भई सहाय मातु तुम तब तब ॥24॥

अमरपुरी अरु बासव लोका ।
तब महिमा सब रहें अशोका ॥25॥

ज्वाला में है ज्योति तुम्हारी ।
तुम्हें सदा पूजें नर-नारी ॥26॥

प्रेम भक्ति से जो यश गावै ।
दुःख दारिद्र निकट नहिं आवें ॥27॥

ध्यावे तुम्हें जो नर मन लाई ।
जन्म-मरण ताकौ छुटि जाई ॥28॥

जोगी सुर मुनि कहत पुकारी ।
योग न हो बिन शक्ति तुम्हारी ॥29॥

शंकर आचारज तप कीनो ।
काम अरु क्रोध जीति सब लीनो ॥30॥

निशिदिन ध्यान धरो शंकर को ।
काहु काल नहिं सुमिरो तुमको ॥31॥

शक्ति रूप को मरम न पायो ।
शक्ति गई तब मन पछितायो ॥32॥

शरणागत हुई कीर्ति बखानी ।
जय जय जय जगदम्ब भवानी ॥33॥

भई प्रसन्न आदि जगदम्बा ।
दई शक्ति नहिं कीन विलम्बा ॥34॥

मोको मातु कष्ट अति घेरो ।
तुम बिन कौन हरै दुःख मेरो ॥35॥

आशा तृष्णा निपट सतावे ।
मोह मदादिक सब विनशावै ॥36॥

शत्रु नाश कीजै महारानी ।
सुमिरौं इकचित तुम्हें भवानी ॥37॥

करो कृपा हे मातु दयाला ।
ऋद्धि-सिद्धि दे करहु निहाला ॥38॥

जब लगि जियउं दया फल पाऊं ।
तुम्हरो यश मैं सदा सुनाऊं ॥39॥

दुर्गा चालीसा जो नित गावै ।
सब सुख भोग परमपद पावै ॥40॥

देवीदास शरण निज जानी ।
करहु कृपा जगदम्ब भवानी ॥41॥





Meaning in English

Namo Namo Durge Sukh Karani
Namo Namo Ambe Dukh Harani ॥1॥
I salute you, O Mother Durga, you bestow happiness to all. I salute you, O Mother Amba, you end all miseries.

Nirakar Hay Jyoti Tumhari
Tihon Lok Phaili Ujiyari ॥2॥
Your radiance is limitless. And you illumine all the three worlds (earth, heaven and the nether world).

Shashi Lalaat Mukh Maha Vishala
Netra Laal Bhru-kuti Vik-rala ॥3॥
Your forehead is shining like the moon and your face is very big. And your eyes are red with a frightening frown.

Roop Matu Ko Adhik Suhave
Darash Karath Jan Ati Sukh Paave॥4॥
Your look is enchanting, the very sight of which gives joy to all.

Tum Sansar Shakti Lay Kina
Paalan Hetu Annh Dhan Deenha ॥5॥
In you lie all the powers of the world. And you nurture the world with food and wealth.

Annapurna Huyi Jagh Paala
Tum-hi Aadi Sundari Baala ॥6॥
You as mother Annapoorna nurture the whole world. And you are the primordial form of a young girl of extreme beauty.

Pralay Kaal Sab Naashan Haari
Tum Gauri Shiv Shankar Pyaari ॥7॥
You destroy everything at the time of Pralaya (the great dissolution). You are mother Gouri, the beloved consort of Lord Shankara.

Shiv Yogi Tumhare Gunn Gaavein
Bramha Vishnu Tumhein Nit Dhyavein ॥8॥
Your praises are sung by Lord Shiva and the yogis. And on you Brahma and Vishnu always meditate.

Rup Saraswati Ko Tum Dhara
Dey Subudhi Rishi Munin Ubara ॥9॥
You assume the form of Mother Saraswati, and grant wisdom to the sages and thus ensure their Welfare.

Dhara Roop Narsimha Ko Amba
Pragat Bhayin Phadkar Khamba ॥10॥
You assumed the form of Lord Narasimha, and appeared breaking the pillar to save Prahlada.

Raksha Kar Prahlad Bachayo
Hiranya-aksh Ko Swarg Pathayo ॥11॥
You saved Prahlada, and sent Hiranyakashyap to heaven as he was killed by you.

Lakshmi Roop Dharo Jag Maahi
Shri Narayan Ang Samahi ॥12॥
You assumed the form of Mother Lakshmi, and repose by the side of Shree Narayana.

Kshir Sindhu Mein Karath Vilasa
Daya Sindhu Deejai Mann Aasa ॥13॥
You dwell in the ocean of milk with Lord Vishnu, and you are the ocean of compassion. Please fulfill my wishes.

Hing Laaj Mein Tumhi Bhavani
Mahima Amit Nn Jaat Bakhani ॥14॥
You are Mother Bhavani in Hingalaja, your glories are limitless and cannot be described.

Maa-tangi Aru Dhumavati Maata
Bhuvaneshwari Bagala Sukh Daata ॥15॥
You are Matangi and Dhoomavati Mata. And you as Mother Bhuvaneshwari and Mother Bagalamukhi bestow happiness to all.

Shri Bhairav Taara Jag Taarini
Chhin Bhaal Bhav Dukh Nivarini॥16॥
You are Mother Bhairavi and Tara Devi, the redeemer of the world. And you as Mother Chhinnamasta, remove the sorrows of the world.

Ke Hari Vahan Soh Bhavani
Laangur Veer Chalath Ag-vani ॥17॥
You reposing gracefully upon the lion as Mother Bhavani, is welcomed by the brave Hanuman moving forward.

Kar Mein Khappar Khadag Viraj-ai
Jaako Dekh Kaal Dar Bhaje ॥18॥
You appear with skull and sword in hands, seeing Which even time flees in panic.

Sohai Astra Aur Trishula
Jaate Uthath Shatru Hiya Shoola ॥19॥
Seeing you, well armed and with trident in hand, the Heart of the enemies tremble with fear.

Nagar Koti Mein Tumhi Virajat
Tihon Lok Mein Dunka Baajat ॥20॥
You repose in Nagarkot Kangra. And make the three worlds shudder with the might of your glory.

Shumbh Nishumbh Daanav Tum Maare
Rakth Beej Shankhan Sam-hare ॥21॥
You killed the demons Shumbha and Nishumbha, and also the many Rakta Bija Demons.

Mahisha-sur Nrup Ati Abhimani
Jehi Agha Bhaar Mahi Akulani ॥22॥
Mahishasura the king was very proud, and filled the earth with the weight of his many sins.

Roop Karaal Kaalika Dhara
Sen Sahit Tum Tihi Sam-hara॥23॥
You assumed the ferocious form of Mother Kali, and killed Mahishasura and his army.

Pari Gaadh Santan Par Jab Jab
Bhai Sahai Maatu Tum Tab Tab ॥24॥
Whenever the saints were in trouble, Mother, you came to their rescue.

Amarpuri Aru Baasav Loka
Tab Mahima Sab Rahein Ashoka ॥25॥
Amarpuri and other lokas, remain sorrowless because of your grace.

Jwala Mein Hai Jyoti Tumhari
Tumhein Sadaa Pujein Nar Naari ॥26॥
Your light resides in jwalamukhi. All men and women always worship you.

Prem Bhakti Say Joh Yash Gaavai
Dukh Daridra Nikat Nahi Aavain ॥27॥
Whoever sings your praises with love and devotion, sorrow and poverty will not come near him.

Dhyave Tumhe Joh Nar Mann Layee
Janm Maran Taakou Chuthi Jaayi॥28॥
Those who meditate on you deeply, will be freed from the cycles of birth and death.

Jogi Sur Muni Kahat Pukari
Yog Na Ho Bin Shati Tumhari ॥29॥
Yogis, Devas and Sages say that, without your power there cannot be yoga, a union with the divine.

Shankar Acharaj Tap Keeno
Kaam Aru Krodh Jeeti Sab Lino ॥30॥
Shankar Acharya did penance, and won over desire and anger.

Nishidhin Dhyan Dharo Shankar Ko
Kaahu Kaal Nahi Sumiro Tumko ॥31॥
He always meditated on Lord Shiva, and never thought of you.

Shakti Roop Ko Maram Na Payo
Shakti Gayi Tab Man Pachi-tayo ॥32॥
He did not realize the glory of Shakti, when his powers waned, he started repenting.

Sharna-gat Huyi Kirti Bakhani
Jaya Jaya Jaya Jagdamb Bhavani ॥33॥
Then he sought your refuge and sung your praises. Victory, victory, victory to Mother Bhavani, the mother of the universe.

Bhai Prasan Aadhi Jagdamba
Dayi Shakti Nahi Keen Vilamba ॥34॥
This pleased the Adi Jagadamba, the primeval mother of the world, and she gave back his powers without delay.

Moko Maatu Kasht Athi Ghero
Tum Bin Kaun Harai Dukh Mero ॥35॥
I am surrounded by grave distress, O Mother, apart from You, who can remove my sorrows?

Asha Trishna Nipat Sataave
Moh Madaadik Sab Vinasha-vai ॥36॥
Desires and longings always torture me, and passions and lust torment me.

Shatru Naash Keejai Maharani
Sumiraun Ekchit Tumhein Bhavani ॥37॥
O Great Queen, please destroy my enemies, I meditate on you with one-pointed devotion.

Karo Krupa Hey Maatu Dayaala
Ridhi-Sidhi De Karahu Nihaala॥38॥
Bestow your grace on me, O kind Mother. And give me spiritual wealth and power.

Jab Lagi Jiya-un Daya Phal Paa-un
Tumharo Yash Main Saada Suna-un ॥39॥
As long as I live, may I be a recipient of your mercy, and let me always sing your glories to others.

Durga Chalisa Joh Nith Gaavai
Sab Sukh Bhog Param-pad Paavai ॥40॥
Those who sing the Durga Chalisa everyday, will get worldly prosperity and finally attain the supreme feet of the Mother.

Devi Daas Sharan Nij Jaani
Karahu Krupa Jagdamb Bhavani॥41॥
Knowing that Devidas has taken your refuge, please bestow your grace, O Mother Jagadamba Bhavani.
