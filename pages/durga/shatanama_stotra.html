Eshwar Uvaach

Sarvasur Vinasha Ch Sarvadanav Ghatini.
Sarva Shastramayi Satya Sarvastra Dharini Tatha ॥ 11 ॥

Anek Shastra Hasta Ch Anekastrasya Dharini.

Devanagari Script

ईश्वर उवाच

शतनाम प्रवक्ष्यामि शृणुष्व कमलानने ।
यस्य प्रसादमात्रेण दुर्गा प्रीता भवेत् सती ॥ 1 ॥

ॐ सती साध्वी भवप्रीता भवानी भवमोचनी ।
आर्या दुर्गा जया चाद्या त्रिनेत्रा शूलधारिणी ॥ 2 ॥

पिनाकधारिणी चित्रा चण्डघण्टा महातपाः ।
मनो बुद्धिरहंकारा चित्तरूपा चिता चितिः ॥ 3 ॥

सर्वमन्त्रमयी सत्ता सत्यानन्द स्वरूपिणी ।
अनन्ता भाविनी भाव्या भव्याभव्या सदागतिः ॥ 4 ॥

शाम्भवी देवमाता च चिन्ता रत्नप्रिया सदा ।
सर्वविद्या दक्षकन्या दक्षयज्ञविनाशिनी ॥ 5 ॥

अपर्णानेकवर्णा च पाटला पाटलावती ।
पट्टाम्बर परीधाना कलमञ्जीररञ्जिनी ॥ 6 ॥

अमेयविक्रमा क्रुरा सुन्दरी सुरसुन्दरी ।
वनदुर्गा च मातङ्गी मतङ्गमुनिपूजिता ॥ 7 ॥

ब्राह्मी माहेश्वरी चैन्द्री कौमारी वैष्णवी तथा ।
चामुण्डा चैव वाराही लक्ष्मीश्च पुरुषाकृतिः ॥ 8 ॥

विमलोत्कर्षिणी ज्ञाना क्रिया नित्या च बुद्धिदा ।
बहुला बहुलप्रेमा सर्ववाहन वाहना ॥ 9 ॥

निशुम्भशुम्भहननी महिषासुरमर्दिनी ।
मधुकैटभहन्त्री च चण्डमुण्डविनाशिनी ॥ 10 ॥

सर्वासुरविनाशा च सर्वदानवघातिनी ।
सर्वशास्त्रमयी सत्या सर्वास्त्रधारिणी तथा ॥ 11 ॥

अनेकशस्त्रहस्ता च अनेकास्त्रस्य धारिणी ।
कुमारी चैककन्या च कैशोरी युवती यतिः ॥ 12 ॥

अप्रौढा चैव प्रौढा च वृद्धमाता बलप्रदा ।
महोदरी मुक्तकेशी घोररूपा महाबला ॥ 13 ॥

अग्निज्वाला रौद्रमुखी कालरात्रिस्तपस्विनी ।
नारायणी भद्रकाली विष्णुमाया जलोदरी ॥ 14 ॥

शिवदूती कराली च अनन्ता परमेश्वरी ।
कात्यायनी च सावित्री प्रत्यक्षा ब्रह्मवादिनी ॥ 15 ॥

य इदं प्रपठेन्नित्यं दुर्गानामशताष्टकम् ।
नासाध्यं विद्यते देवि त्रिषु लोकेषु पार्वति ॥ 16 ॥

धनं धान्यं सुतं जायां हयं हस्तिनमेव च ।
चतुर्वर्गं तथा चान्ते लभेन्मुक्तिं च शाश्वतीम् ॥ 17 ॥

कुमारीं पूजयित्वा तु ध्यात्वा देवीं सुरेश्वरीम् ।
पूजयेत् परया भक्त्या पठेन्नामशताष्टकम् ॥ 18 ॥

तस्य सिद्धिर्भवेद् देवि सर्वैः सुरवरैरपि ।
राजानो दासतां यान्ति राज्यश्रियमवाप्नुयात् ॥ 19 ॥

गोरोचनालक्तककुङ्कुमेव सिन्धूरकर्पूरमधुत्रयेण ।
विलिख्य यन्त्रं विधिना विधिज्ञो भवेत् सदा धारयते पुरारिः ॥ 20 ॥

भौमावास्यानिशामग्रे चन्द्रे शतभिषां गते ।
विलिख्य प्रपठेत् स्तोत्रं स भवेत् संपदां पदम् ॥ 21 ॥

॥ इति श्रीविश्वसारतन्त्रे दुर्गाष्टोत्तरशतनामस्तोत्रं समाप्तम् ॥





Meaning in English

Eshwar Uvaach
Lord Shiva speaks

Shatanaam Pravakshyami Shrunushv Kamlaane.
Yasya Prasad Maatrnay Durga Prita Bhavet Sati ॥ 1 ॥
O Parvati, the one who has a lotus-like face! I am telling you those one hundred and eight names, by the means of which Durga or Sati can be pleased.

Om Sati Saadhvi Bhava-prita Bhavani Bhava-mochani.
Arya Durga Jaya Chadya Trinetra Shool-dharini ॥ 2 ॥
Sati, the daughter of Dakṣa; Sadhvi, the Sanguine; Bhavaprita, loved by the universe; Bhavani, the abode of the universe; Bhavamocani, the absolver of the universe; Arya; Durga; Jaya; Adya, the beginning reality; Trinetra, having three-eyes; Suladhariṇi, holding a monodent.

Pinak-dharini Chitra Chand-ghanta Maha-tapah.
Mano Budhir Ahankara Chita Rupa Chita Chitihi ॥ 3 ॥
Pinakadhariṇi, who holds the trident of Siva; Citra; Chandghanta, having mighty bells; Mahatapa, with severe penance; Manas, mind; Buddhi, wisdom; Ahankara, pride; Cittarupa, thought state; Cita, death bed; Citi, the thinking mind.

Sarva Mantra-mayi Sata Satyanand Swarupini.
Ananta Bhavini Bhavya Bhavya-bhavya Sada-gatihi ॥ 4 ॥
Sarvamantramayi, possessing all the instruments of thought; Satta, above all; Satyanandasvarupini, eternal bliss; Ananta, infinite or beyond measure; Bhavini, beautiful woman; Bhavya, future; Bhavya, with splendor; Abhavya, improper or fear causing; Sadagati, always bestowing Moksha.

Shambhavi Devamata Ch Chinta Ratnapriya Sada.
Sarva-vidya Daksha-kanya Dakshyagya Vinashini ॥ 5 ॥
Sambhavi, consort of Sambhu; Devamata; Cinta, thoughts; Ratnapriya, adorned or loved by jewels; Sarvavidya, abode of knowledge; Dakshakanya, that is Sati, daughter of Daksha; Dakshaya vinashini, destroyer of the sacrifice of Daksha.

Aparna Nekvarna Ch Paatla Paatlavati.
Patambar Paridhana Kalam-ajjir-rajjini ॥ 6 ॥
Aparna; Anekavarna, having many complexions, for example: Kali, Gauri; Patala, red in color; Patalavati, wearing a red color apparel; Pattambara paridhana, wearing a dress made of leather; Kalamanjiraranjini, wearing a melodious anklet.

Ameya Vikrama Krura Sundari Sur-sundari.
Vana Durga Ch Maatangi Matang-muni Poojita ॥ 7 ॥
Ameya, immeasurable; Vikrama, fierce; Krura, cruel on demons; Sundari; Surasundari; Vanadurga; Matangi; Matangamunipujita, prayed by Sage Matanga.

Brahmi Maaheswari Chaindri Kaumari Vaishnavi Tatha.
Chamunda Chaiv Varahi Lakshmisch Purusha Krutihi ॥ 8 ॥
Brahmi; MaheSvari; Caindri; Kaumarc;Vaishnavi; Chamunda; Varahi; Lakshmi; Purusha kriti, taking the form of a man.

Vimalotakrshini Gyana Kriya Nitya Ch Budhida.
Bahula Bahula Prema Sarva-vahana Vahana ॥ 9 ॥
Vimalotkarshini, providing joy; Jnana; Kriya; Nitya, eternal one; Buddhida, bestower of wisdom; Bahula, numerous in forms; Bahulaprema, generously benevolent; Sarvavahanavahana, sits or rides all vehicles.

Nishumbh Shumbhanani Mahishasur-mardini.
Madhukaitbahaantri Ch Chandmund Vinashini ॥ 10 ॥
Slayer of Sumbha and Nishumbha, Mahishasura, Madhu and Kaitabha, and Chanda and Munda.

Sarvasur Vinasha Ch Sarvadanav Ghatini.
Sarva Shastramayi Satya Sarvastra Dharini Tatha ॥ 11 ॥
Sarvasuravinasha, destroyer of all demons; Sarvadanavaghatini, causes injury to all the demons; SarvaSastramayi, deft in all theories; Satya; Sarvastradharini, possessor of all the missile weapons.

Anek Shastra Hasta Ch Anekastrasya Dharini.
Kumari Chaik-kanya Ch Kaishori Yuvati Yatihi ॥ 12 ॥
Anekashastrahasta, possessor of many hand weapons; Anekastrasya Dharini, possessor of many missile weapons; Kumari; Ekakanya; Kaisori; Yuvati; Yati.

Aproduha Chaiv Proudha Ch Vradha Mata Balaprada.
Mahodari Mukta Keshi Ghor-rupa Mahabala ॥ 13 ॥
Apraudha, who never gets old; Praudha, who is old; Vriddhamata, old mother; Balaprada, bestower of strength; Mahodari, gigantic abdomen which stores the universe; Mukta kesha, having open tresses; Ghorarupa, having a fierce outlook; Mahabala, having immense strength.

Agnijwala Raudra-mukhi Kaalratri-stapasvini.
Narayani Bhadrakaali Vishnumaya Jalodari ॥ 14 ॥
Agnijvala, poignant like fire; Raudramukhi, having a fierce face like universe destroying Rudra; Kalaratri; Tapasvini; Narayani; Bhadrakali; Vishnumaya; Jalodari, abode of the ethereal universe.

Shivduti Karaali Ch Ananta Parameshwari.
Katyayani Ch Savitri Pratyksha Bramhavadini ॥ 15 ॥
Sivaduti; Karali, fierce; Ananta, immeasurable; Parameshwari; Katyayani; Savitri; Pratyaksha; Brahmavadini.

Yah Ind Prapataynityam Durganam Shatashtakam.
Nasadhyam Vidyate Devi Trishu Lokeshu Parvati ॥ 16 ॥
Those who daily read these 108 names from Durga Stotram, find nothing impossible in the three worlds.

Dhanam Dhanyam Sutam Jayam Hayam Hastinmev Ch.
Chaturvargam Tatha Chantey Labhenmuktim Ch Shashwatim ॥ 17 ॥
They receive benefits like wealth, luxury, offspring and lineage, elephants, four things - dharma, artha, kaam, moksha, and in the end are liberated eternally.

Kumarim Pujyitva Tu Dhyatva Devim Sureshvarim.
Pujyet Paraya Bhaktaya Pathenam Shatashtakam ॥ 18 ॥
Having prayed and meditated upon Devi, Kumari, and the Goddess of demigods, worship her with devotion using this stotra.

Tasya Sidhirbhaved Devi Sarve-he Survarai-rapi.
Rajano Dasataam Yanti Rajya-shriyam Vapnuyaat ॥ 19 ॥
O Goddess! The king who prays in the above manner and attains perfection, attains his kingdom, and splendor back from everyone including the demigods.

Gorochana Laktak Kumkumev Sindhur Karpur Madhu-trayen.
Vilikhya Yantram Vidhina Vidhigyo Bhavet Sada Dharyate Purari-hi ॥ 20 ॥
Those who write a yantra or diagram of this stotra with gomutra (cow's urine), Alta (a red color pigment), kunkuma, sindura, camphor, and honey, and keep it in hand, becomes similar to Shiva.

Bhouma Vasyani Shamagre Chandre Shata-bhisham Gatey.
Vilikhya Prapathet Stotram Sa Bhavet Sampadam Padam ॥ 21 ॥
The one who writes and recites this eulogy during Bhauma new moon night attains every worldly luxury.

॥ Iti Shri Vishwasar Tanre Durgashtor Shataman Stotram Samaptam ॥
This ends the Durga Ashtottara Shataman Stotra.
