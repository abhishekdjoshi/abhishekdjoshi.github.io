Meaning in English

Jaya Bhagwati Devi Namo Varday
Jaya Paap Vinashini Bahu-falday
Jaya Shumbh Ni-shumbh Kapal-ghare
Pranamami Tu Devi Nararti-hare ||1||
Victory to You O divine goddess, my salutations to you Who is the giver of boons,
Victory to You O Devi, who is the destroyer of sins and bestower of many fruits,
Victory to You O Devi, Who wears the heads of sinful demons Shumbha and Nishumbha after slaying them,
I Bow down to You O Devi, you indeed are the remover of pains of human beings.

Jaya Chandra Divakar Netra-ghare
Jaya Pavak-bhushith Vakra-vare
Jaya Bhair-vadeh Nilini-pare
Jaya Andhak-daitya Vishosh-kare ||2||
Victory to You O Devi, who wears the sun and the moon as her eyes,
Victory to You O Devi, whose beautiful face is adorned by the glow of fire,
Victory to You O Devi, who as the supreme being fused her body with that of Bhairava,
Victory to You O Devi, who dried up the strength of demon Andhaka for his wickedness.

Jaya Mahish-vimardini Shool-kare
Jaya Loka-samast Kapap-hare
Jaya Devi Pita-maha Vishnu-natey
Jaya Bhaskar-shiro Vanate ||3||
Victory to You O Devi, who crushed the demon Mahishasura with trident in hand,
Victory to You O Devi, who destroys the sins in the whole world,
Victory to You O Devi, to whom fatherly Brahma and Sri Vishnu bow down.,
Victory to You O Devi, to whom Sun and the other devas bow down.

Jaya Shanmukh-sayu Dhayi-shanutey
Jaya Sagar-gamini Shambhu-nutey
Jaya Dukh-daridra Vinasha-kare
Jaya Putra-kalatra Vivridh-kare ||4||
Victory to You O Devi, who is praised by Lord Shanmukha adorned with various weapons,
Victory to You O Devi, who is praised by Lord Shiva seeing you flowing towards the ocean for the welfare of mankind,
Victory to You O Devi, who destroys all sorrows and poverty,
Victory to You O Devi, who augments our fortune by providing sons and wives.

Jaya Devi Samast Shareer-ghare
Jaya Naka-vidarshini Dukh-hare
Jaya Vyadhi-vinashini Moksha-kare
Jaya Vachhi-dayani Siddhi-vare ||5||
Victory to You O Devi, who wears all the bodies, remaining as the underlying pure consciousness,
Victory to You O Devi, who bestows the vision of spiritual sky during meditation, thus destroying the root of our sorrows and spiritual ignorance,
Victory to You O Devi, who destroys the diseases, obstructing our spiritual progress, and finally grants liberation from the delusion of samsara,
Victory to You O Devi, who thus grants our wishes and takes us towards our spiritual fulfilment.

Et-davya Sanskritam Stotram
Yah Pate-niyatah Shuchi-hi
Grahe-va Shudh-bhaven
Prita Bhagwati Sada ||6||
This hymn is composed by Sage Vyasa,
Whoever recites this hymn regularly after becoming bodily and mentally clean ,
in his home or anywhere with pure devotion to the goddess,
will please the Bhagavati Devi always.
