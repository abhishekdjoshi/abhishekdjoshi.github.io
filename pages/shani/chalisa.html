Shani Chalisa
शनि चालीसा


This is the lyrics of Shani Chalisa in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the Shani Chalisa meaning in English. The Shani Chalisa is read to please Lord Shani.




English

॥ Doha - Beginning॥
Jai Ganesh Girija Suwan, Mangal Karan Krupaal,
Deenan Ke Dhuk Dhoor Kari, Kheejai Naath Nihaal.

Jai Jai Sri Shanidev Prabhu, Sunahu Vinay Maharaaj,
Karahu Krupa Hey Ravi Thanay, Rakhahu Jan Ki Laaj.

॥ Doha - Four line Prayer ॥
Jayathi Jayathi Shani Dayaala,
Karath Sadha Bhakthan Prathipaala. ॥1॥

Chaari Bhuja, Thanu Shyam Viraajai,
Maathey Rathan Mukut Chavi Chaajai. ॥2॥

Param Vishaal Manohar Bhaala,
Tedi Dhrishti Bhrukuti Vikraala. ॥3॥

Kundal Shravan Chamaacham Chamke,
Hiye Maal Mukthan Mani Dhamkai. ॥4॥

Kar Me Gadha Thrishul Kutaara,
Pal Bich Karai Arihi Samhaara. ॥5॥

Pinghal, Krishno, Chaaya, Nandhan,
Yum, Konasth, Raudra, Dhuk Bhamjan. ॥6॥

Sauri, Mandh Shani, Dhasha Naama,
Bhanu Puthra Poojhin Sab Kaama. ॥7॥

Jaapar Prabu Prasan Havain Jhaahin,
Rakhhun Raav Karai Shan Maahin. ॥8॥

Parvathhu Thrun Hoi Nihaarath,
Thrunhu Ko Parvath Kari Daarath. ॥9॥

Raaj Milath Ban Raamhin Dheenhyo,
Kaikeyihu Ki Mathi Hari Linhiyo. ॥10॥

Banhu Mae Mrug KapatDhikaayi,
Maathu Janki Gayi Churaayi. ॥11॥

Lakhanhin Shakthi Vikal Kari Daara,
Machiga Dhal Mae Haahaakaar. ॥12॥

Raavan Ki Ghathi-Mathi Bauraayi,
Ramchandra Soan Bair Badaayi. ॥13॥

Dhiyo Keet Kari Kanchan Lanka,
Baji Bajarang Beer Ki Danka. ॥14॥

Nrup Vikram Par Thuhin Pagu Dhaara,
Chitra Mayur Nigli Gai Haara. ॥15॥

Haar Naulakka Laagyo Chori,
Haath Pair Darvaayo Thori. ॥16॥

Bhaari Dhasha Nikrusht Dhikaayo,
Thelhin Ghar Kholhu Chalvaayo. ॥17॥

Vinay Raag Dheepak Mah Khinhayo,
Thab Prasann Prabhu Hvai Sukh Dheenhayo. ॥18॥

Harishchandrahun NrupNaari Bhikani,
Aaphun Bharen Dome Gar Paani. ॥19॥

Thai nal par dasha sirani,
Bhunji-Meen Koodh Gayi Paani. ॥20॥

Sri Shankarhin GahyoJab Jaayi,
Paarvathi Ko Sathi Karaayi. ॥21॥

Thanik Vilokath Hi Kari Reesa,
Nabh Udi Gayo Gaurisuth Seesa. ॥22॥

Paandav Par Bhay Dasha Thumhaari,
Bachi Draupadhi Hothi Udhaari. ॥23॥

Kaurav Ke Bi Gathi Mathi Maaryo,
Yudh Mahabharath Kari Daryo. ॥24॥

Ravi Kah Mukh Mahn Dhari Thathkala,
Lekar Koodhi Paryo Paathaala. ॥25॥

Sesh Dhev-Lakhi Vinthi Laayi,
Ravi Ko Mukh Thay Dhiyo Chudaayi. ॥26॥

Vaahan Prabhu Kay Sath Sujana,
Juj Dhigaj Gadharbh Mrugh Swaana. ॥27॥

Jambuk Sinh Aadhi Nakh Dhari,
So Phal Jyothish Kahath Pukari. ॥28॥

Gaj Vaahan Lakshmi Grah Aavain,
Hay Thay Sukh Sampathi Upjaavai. ॥29॥

Gadharbh Haani Karai Bahu Kaaja,
Sinha Sidhkar Raaj Samaja. ॥30॥

Jhambuk Budhi NashtKar Darai,
Mrug Dhe Kasht Praan Samharai. ॥31॥

Jab Aavahi Prabu Svan Savaari,
Choru Aaadhi Hoy Dar Bhaari. ॥32॥

Thaishi Chaari CharanYuh Naama,
Swarn Laoh Chaandhi Aru Thama. ॥33॥

Lauh Charan Par Jab Prabu Aavain,
Daan Jan Sampathi Nashta Karavain. ॥34॥

Samatha ThaamraRajath Shubhkari,
Swarn Sarva Sukh Mangal Bhaari. ॥35॥

Jo Yuh Shani Charithra Nith Gavai,
Kabahu Na Dasha Nikrushta Sathavai. ॥36॥

Adhbuth NathDhikavain Leela,
Karain Shatru Kay Nashi Bhali Deela. ॥37॥

Jo Pundith Suyogya Bulvaayi,
Vidhvath Shani Gruha Shanthi Karayi. ॥38॥

Peepal Jal Shani Diwas Chadavath,
Deep Dhaan Dhai Bahu Sukh Pawath. ॥39॥

Kahath Raam Sundhar Prabu Dhasa,
Shani Sumirath Sukh Hoth Prakasha. ॥40॥

॥ Doha - Conclusion॥
Path Shanishchar Dev Ko, Ki Ho Bhakt Taiyaar,
Karat Path Chalis Din, Ho Bhavasaagar Paar.





Devanagari Script

॥ दोहा ॥
जय गणेश गिरिजा सुवन, मंगल करण कृपाल ।
दीनन के दुःख दूर करि, कीजै नाथ निहाल ॥

जय जय श्री शनिदेव प्रभु, सुनहु विनय महाराज ।
करहु कृपा हे रवि तनय, राखहु जन की लाज ॥

॥ चौपाई ॥
जयति जयति शनिदेव दयाला ।
करत सदा भक्तन प्रतिपाला ॥1॥

चारि भुजा, तनु श्याम विराजै ।
माथे रतन मुकुट छवि छाजै ॥2॥

परम विशाल मनोहर भाला ।
टेढ़ी दृष्टि भृकुटि विकराला ॥3॥

कुण्डल श्रवण चमाचम चमके ।
हिये माल मुक्तन मणि दमके ॥4॥

कर में गदा त्रिशूल कुठारा ।
पल बिच करैं अरिहिं संहारा ॥5॥

पिंगल, कृष्णों, छाया, नन्दन ।
यम, कोणस्थ, रौद्र, दुःख भंजन ॥6॥

सौरी, मन्द, शनि, दशनामा ।
भानु पुत्र पूजहिं सब कामा ॥7॥

जा पर प्रभु प्रसन्न है जाहीं ।
रंकहुं राव करैं क्षण माहीं ॥8॥

पर्वतहू तृण होई निहारत ।
तृणहू को पर्वत करि डारत ॥9॥

राज मिलत वन रामहिं दीन्हो ।
कैकेइहुं की मति हरि लीन्हो ॥10॥

बनहूं में मृग कपट दिखाई ।
मातु जानकी गई चतुराई ॥11॥

लखनहिं शक्ति विकल करिडारा ।
मचिगा दल में हाहाकारा ॥12॥

रावण की गति मति बौराई ।
रामचन्द्र सों बैर बढ़ाई ॥13॥

दियो कीट करि कंचन लंका ।
बजि बजरंग बीर की डंका ॥14॥

नृप विक्रम पर तुहि पगु धारा ।
चित्र मयूर निगलि गै हारा ॥15॥

हार नौलाखा लाग्यो चोरी ।
हाथ पैर डरवायो तोरी ॥16॥

भारी दशा निकृष्ट दिखायो ।
तेलिहिं घर कोल्हू चलवायो ॥17॥

विनय राग दीपक महँ कीन्हों ।
तब प्रसन्न प्रभु हवै सुख दीन्हों ॥18॥

हरिश्चन्द्र नृप नारि बिकानी ।
आपहुं भरे डोम घर पानी ॥19॥

तैसे नल पर दशा सिरानी ।
भूंजी-मीन कूद गई पानी ॥20॥

श्री शंकरहि गहयो जब जाई ।
पार्वती को सती कराई ॥21॥

तनिक विलोकत ही करि रीसा ।
नभ उड़ि गयो गौरिसुत सीसा ॥22॥

पाण्डव पर भै दशा तुम्हारी ।
बची द्रोपदी होति उधारी ॥23॥

कौरव के भी गति मति मारयो ।
युद्ध महाभारत करि डारयो ॥24॥

रवि कहं मुख महं धरि तत्काला ।
लेकर कूदि परयो पाताला ॥25॥

शेष देव-लखि विनती लाई ।
रवि को मुख ते दियो छुड़ई ॥26॥

वाहन प्रभु के सात सुजाना ।
जग दिग्ज गर्दभ मृग स्वाना ॥27॥

जम्बुक सिंह आदि नख धारी ।
सो फल ज्योतिष कहत पुकारी ॥28॥

गज वाहन लक्ष्मी गृह आवैं ।
हय ते सुख सम्पत्ति उपजावै ॥29॥

गर्दभ हानि करै बहु काजा ।
गर्दभ सिंद्धकर राज समाजा ॥30॥

जम्बुक बुद्धि नष्ट कर डारै ।
मृग दे कष्ट प्राण संहारै ॥31॥

जब आवहिं प्रभु स्वान सवारी ।
चोरी आदि होय डर भारी ॥32॥

तैसहि चारि चरण यह नामा ।
स्वर्ण लौह चाँजी अरु तामा ॥33॥

लौह चरण पर जब प्रभु आवैं ।
धन जन सम्पत्ति नष्ट करावै ॥34॥

समता ताम्र रजत शुभकारी ।
स्वर्ण सर्वसुख मंगल कारी ॥35॥

जो यह शनि चरित्र नित गावै ।
कबहुं न दशा निकृष्ट सतावै ॥36॥

अदभुत नाथ दिखावैं लीला ।
करैं शत्रु के नशि बलि ढीला ॥37॥

जो पण्डित सुयोग्य बुलवाई ।
विधिवत शनि ग्रह शांति कराई ॥38॥

पीपल जल शनि दिवस चढ़ावत ।
दीप दान दै बहु सुख पावत ॥39॥

कहत राम सुन्दर प्रभु दासा ।
शनि सुमिरत सुख होत प्रकाशा ॥40॥

॥ दोहा ॥
पाठ शनिश्चर देव को, की हों विमल तैयार ।
करत पाठ चालीस दिन, हो भवसागर पार ॥





Meaning in English

॥ Doha - Beginning॥
Jai Ganesh Girija Suwan, Mangal Karan Krupaal,
Deenan Ke Dhuk Dhoor Kari, Kheejai Naath Nihaal.
Oh Lord Ganesha for whom a mountain is like a flower, please be merciful, and takeaway the troubles of the tortured and raise our consciousness.

Jai Jai Sri Shanidev Prabhu, Sunahu Vinay Maharaaj,
Karahu Krupa Hey Ravi Thanay, Rakhahu Jan Ki Laaj.
Oh Lord Shani you are so victorious and merciful one. Listen to our prayers and protect the purity and righteousness of your devotees.

॥ Doha - Four line Prayer ॥
Jayathi Jayathi Shani Dayaala,
Karath Sadha Bhakthan Prathipaala. ॥1॥
Victory be to the merciful Shani Dev, you are the protector of those who take refuge in you. Oh the four armed one with a beautiful dark skin and a forehead decorated with a pearl studded crown.

Chaari Bhuja, Thanu Shyam Viraajai,
Maathey Rathan Mukut Chavi Chaajai. ॥2॥
Oh the four armed one with a beautiful dark skin and a forehead decorated with a pearl studded crown.

Param Vishaal Manohar Bhaala,
Tedi Dhrishti Bhrukuti Vikraala. ॥3॥
(ou look so smart and possess a bright spear. Thou create a frowning look by looking like a slayer.

Kundal Shravan Chamaacham Chamke,
Hiye Maal Mukthan Mani Dhamkai. ॥4॥
The ear rings and pearl necklace you wear dazzle in the light.

Kar Me Gadha Thrishul Kutaara,
Pal Bich Karai Arihi Samhaara. ॥5॥
You carry in your arms a mace, trident and battle-axe, cutting down the enemies on the move.

Pinghal, Krishno, Chaaya, Nandhan,
Yum, Konasth, Raudra, Dhuk Bhamjan. ॥6॥
Son of Chaya, you are called as Pinglo, Krishna, Yama, Konastha, Raudra and the remover of pain and suffering.

Sauri, Mandh Shani, Dhasha Naama,
Bhanu Puthra Poojhin Sab Kaama. ॥7॥
Sauri, Manda – these ten names that belong to you, Oh son of Lord Surya, you bring fame to them.

Jaapar Prabu Prasan Havain Jhaahin,
Rakhhun Raav Karai Shan Maahin. ॥8॥
When you are pleased or displeased with someone, You can immediately transform him from a beggar to a king or the reverse.

Parvathhu Thrun Hoi Nihaarath,
Thrunhu Ko Parvath Kari Daarath. ॥9॥
At your will, even simple things can become complex and tough. Your blessings can turn the toughest tasks into simple ones.

Raaj Milath Ban Raamhin Dheenhyo,
Kaikeyihu Ki Mathi Hari Linhiyo. ॥10॥
When you chose to twist the wish of Kaikeyi, the wife of Dasaratha, even Rama had to give up his kingdom and leave to the forest in exile.

Banhu Mae Mrug KapatDhikaayi,
Maathu Janki Gayi Churaayi. ॥11॥
In the woods an illusory deer distracted Lord Rama. And as a result Sita, who is an incarnation of mother nature, was kidnapped.

Lakhanhin Shakthi Vikal Kari Daara,
Machiga Dhal Mae Haahaakaar. ॥12॥
Even Lord Rama’s brother Lakshman fainted thus inspiring fear in every member of Lord Rama’s army.

Raavan Ki Ghathi-Mathi Bauraayi,
Ramchandra Soan Bair Badaayi. ॥13॥
Ravana lost touch with his sense and wisdom, And ended up picking a fight against Lord Rama.

Dhiyo Keet Kari Kanchan Lanka,
Baji Bajarang Beer Ki Danka. ॥14॥
But as soon as Lord Hanuman, also known as Bajrang Bali, invaded Ravana’s Lanka, the Golden Lanka was turned into ruins.

Nrup Vikram Par Thuhin Pagu Dhaara,
Chitra Mayur Nigli Gai Haara. ॥15॥
While afflicted by Shani, King Vikramaditya’s necklace was swallowed by a pictured peacock.

Haar Naulakka Laagyo Chori,
Haath Pair Darvaayo Thori. ॥16॥
Allegations of theft befell even Lord Krishna who was badly beaten up.

Bhaari Dhasha Nikrusht Dhikaayo,
Thelhin Ghar Kholhu Chalvaayo. ॥17॥
When life was filled with miseries during your Maha Dasha period, even Lord Krishna had to work in the house of a layman.

Vinay Raag Dheepak Mah Khinhayo,
Thab Prasann Prabhu Hvai Sukh Dheenhayo. ॥18॥
When he prayed with devotion to you, he was blessed with all that he wanted.

Harishchandrahun NrupNaari Bhikani,
Aaphun Bharen Dome Gar Paani. ॥19॥
Even King Harishchandra suffered during your Dasha period, he lost all that he owned and even his wife was sold away.

Thai nal par dasha sirani,
Bhunji-Meen Koodh Gayi Paani. ॥20॥
He was forced to do menial work, at a house of a poor sweeper.

Sri Shankarhin GahyoJab Jaayi,
Paarvathi Ko Sathi Karaayi. ॥21॥
(While you moved through the zodiac sign of Lord Shiva, his wife Parvati had to be burnt alive in fire.

Thanik Vilokath Hi Kari Reesa,
Nabh Udi Gayo Gaurisuth Seesa. ॥22॥
When you looked at the young Lord Ganesha, His head bounced into the sky and was destroyed.

Paandav Par Bhay Dasha Thumhaari,
Bachi Draupadhi Hothi Udhaari. ॥23॥
When the Pandavas were going through your Dasha period, They even lost their wife Draupadi in a bet and were left with no belongings.

Kaurav Ke Bi Gathi Mathi Maaryo,
Yudh Mahabharath Kari Daryo. ॥24॥
Even the Kauravas were not spared and they lost their sense and wisdom, Sparking the great battle of Mahabharata against the Pandavas.

Ravi Kah Mukh Mahn Dhari Thathkala,
Lekar Koodhi Paryo Paathaala. ॥25॥
Oh Shani Dev, you gulped even the sun and escaped into the third world.

Sesh Dhev-Lakhi Vinthi Laayi,
Ravi Ko Mukh Thay Dhiyo Chudaayi. ॥26॥
Only when the gods came and prayed to you, you released the sun from captivity.

Vaahan Prabhu Kay Sath Sujana,
Juj Dhigaj Gadharbh Mrugh Swaana. ॥27॥
Oh Shani Dev, you ride on seven vehicles including an elephant, horse, donkey, deer, dog, jackal and lion.

Jambuk Sinh Aadhi Nakh Dhari,
So Phal Jyothish Kahath Pukari. ॥28॥
All these animals possess terrible nails. Therefore astrologers declare.

Gaj Vaahan Lakshmi Grah Aavain,
Hay Thay Sukh Sampathi Upjaavai. ॥29॥
While riding on an elephant, you bring wealth; while riding on a horse, you bring comfort and wealth.

Gadharbh Haani Karai Bahu Kaaja,
Sinha Sidhkar Raaj Samaja. ॥30॥
when riding on an donkey, you bring losses in several ways, riding on a lion, you bring kingdoms and fame.

Jhambuk Budhi NashtKar Darai,
Mrug Dhe Kasht Praan Samharai. ॥31॥
While riding on a jackal, you take away wisdom; when riding on a deer, you inflict death and pain.

Jab Aavahi Prabu Svan Savaari,
Choru Aaadhi Hoy Dar Bhaari. ॥32॥
When mounted on a dog, you bring accusations of theft thereby making the cursed into beggar.

Thaishi Chaari CharanYuh Naama,
Swarn Laoh Chaandhi Aru Thama. ॥33॥
In a similar manner, you have four different forms of your feet, made of gold,iron, silver and copper.

Lauh Charan Par Jab Prabu Aavain,
Daan Jan Sampathi Nashta Karavain. ॥34॥
When you come home with your iron feet, you destroy all of one’s wealth and belongings.

Samatha ThaamraRajath Shubhkari,
Swarn Sarva Sukh Mangal Bhaari. ॥35॥
Your Copper feet signal that things will be left unharmed, while Silver feet signal many benefits, And brightest of them all, your golden feet bring all types of happiness.

Jo Yuh Shani Charithra Nith Gavai,
Kabahu Na Dasha Nikrushta Sathavai. ॥36॥
Whoever sings your prayers in a manner like this, oh Shani Dev, he is never troubled by your adverse periods.

Adhbuth NathDhikavain Leela,
Karain Shatru Kay Nashi Bhali Deela. ॥37॥
You show your powers before your devotees and kill their enemies or make them helpless.

Jo Pundith Suyogya Bulvaayi,
Vidhvath Shani Gruha Shanthi Karayi. ॥38॥
Learned men and priests resort to the Vedic way of pleasing you with holy pujas and rituals.

Peepal Jal Shani Diwas Chadavath,
Deep Dhaan Dhai Bahu Sukh Pawath. ॥39॥
Since Peepal tree represents Shani, those who water it on saturdays and offer incense sticks to your feet will get all comforts, wealth and health.

Kahath Raam Sundhar Prabu Dhasa,
Shani Sumirath Sukh Hoth Prakasha. ॥40॥
Oh handsome Shani Dev, thus says Lord Rama, your devotee.

॥ Doha - Conclusion॥
Path Shanishchar Dev Ko, Ki Ho Bhakt Taiyaar,
Karat Path Chalis Din, Ho Bhavasaagar Paar.
Those devotees singing Shani chalisa with devotion for forty days will find their way to heaven.
