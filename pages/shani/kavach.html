Shani Kavach
शनि कवच


This is the lyrics of Shani Kavach in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the Shani Kavach meaning in English. The Shani Kavach is read to please Lord Shani.




English

॥ Atha Shri Shani Kavacham ॥
Asya Shri Shanischar Kavach Stotra Mantrasya Kashyap Rishi-hi.
Anushtup Chand-ah. Shanaischro Devata. Shim Shakti-hi.
Shum Kilkam. Shanaischar Prityartham Japey Viniyog-ha.
Nilambaro Nilvapu-hu Kiriti Grudhrasthi Tastrasakaro Dhanushman.
Chaturbhuj-ha Suryasut-ha Prasanna-ha Sada Mam Syadwarad-ha Prashant-ha. ॥ 1 ॥

॥ Bramhovaach ॥
Shrunudhva Mrishya-ha Sarve Shani Pidaharam Mahat,
Kavacham Shanirajasya Saureridmanutamam ॥ 2 ॥

Kavacham Devatavasam Vajra Panjar Samgyakam,
Shanaischar Pritikaram Sarva Soubhagyadayakam ॥ 3 ॥

Om Shri Shanaischar-ha Paatu Bhalam May Surya Nandan-ha,
Netre Chayatmaj-ha Paatu Paatu Karnou Yamanuj-ha ॥ 4 ॥

Nasam Vaivswat-ha Paatu Mukham May Bhaskar-ha Sada,
Snigdh Kantsch May Kantam Bhojou Paatu Mahabhuj-ha ॥ 5 ॥

Skandhau Paatu Shanaischaiv Karaou Paatu Shubhprad-ha,
Vaksh-ha Paatu Yambhrata Kukshim Patvasitastha ॥ 6 ॥

Nabhim Gruhapati-hi Paatu Mand-ha Paatu Katim Tatha,
Uru Mamaantak-ha Paatu Yamo Jaanuyugam Tatha ॥ 7 ॥

Padou Mandagati-hi Paatu Sarvaang Paatu Pipal-ha,
Angopangani Sarvani Rakhshenme Surya Nandan-ha ॥ 8 ॥

Itye Tat Kavacham Divyam Pathet Surya Sutasya Yah,
Na Tasya Jaayate Pida Prito Bhavati Suryaj-ha ॥ 9 ॥

Vyaya Janma Dwitiyastho Mrityusthangatopi Va,
Kalatrastho Gato Vaapi Supritastu Sada Shani-hi ॥ 10 ॥

Ashtmasthe Surya Sutay Vyayey Janma Dwitiyagey,
Kavacham Pathato Nityam Na Pida Jaayate Kwachit ॥ 11 ॥

Itye Tatkavacham Divyam Saure Yar Nirmitam Pura,
Dwadashatam Janmastha Doshana Shayate Sada,
Janma Lagna Sthitanando Shansarvanashyate Prabhu-hu ॥ 11 ॥

॥ Iti Shri Bramhand Purane Bramha Naarad Samvaade Shanaischar Kavacham Sampurnam ॥





Devanagari Script

॥ अथ श्री शनि कवचम् ॥
अस्य श्री शनैश्चरकवचस्तोत्रमंत्रस्य कश्यप ऋषिः ॥
अनुष्टुप् छन्दः ॥ शनैश्चरो देवता ॥ शीं शक्तिः ॥
शूं कीलकम् ॥ शनैश्चरप्रीत्यर्थं जपे विनियोगः ॥
निलांबरो नीलवपुः किरीटी गृध्रस्थितस्त्रासकरो धनुष्मान् ॥
चतुर्भुजः सूर्यसुतः प्रसन्नः सदा मम स्याद्वरदः प्रशान्तः ॥ 1 ॥

॥ ब्रह्मोवाच ॥
श्रुणूध्वमृषयः सर्वे शनिपीडाहरं महत् ।
कवचं शनिराजस्य सौरेरिदमनुत्तमम् ॥ 2 ॥

कवचं देवतावासं वज्रपंजरसंज्ञकम् ।
शनैश्चरप्रीतिकरं सर्वसौभाग्यदायकम् ॥ 3 ॥

ॐ श्रीशनैश्चरः पातु भालं मे सूर्यनंदनः ।
नेत्रे छायात्मजः पातु पातु कर्णौ यमानुजः ॥ 4 ॥

नासां वैवस्वतः पातु मुखं मे भास्करः सदा ।
स्निग्धकंठःश्च मे कंठं भुजौ पातु महाभुजः ॥ 5 ॥

स्कंधौ पातु शनिश्चैव करौ पातु शुभप्रदः ।
वक्षः पातु यमभ्राता कुक्षिं पात्वसितत्सथा ॥ 6 ॥

नाभिं ग्रहपतिः पातु मंदः पातु कटिं तथा ।
ऊरू ममांतकः पातु यमो जानुयुगं तथा ॥ 7 ॥

पादौ मंदगतिः पातु सर्वांगं पातु पिप्पलः ।
अङ्गोपाङ्गानि सर्वाणि रक्षेन्मे सूर्यनंदनः ॥ 8 ॥

इत्येतत्कवचं दिव्यं पठेत्सूर्यसुतस्य यः ।
न तस्य जायते पीडा प्रीतो भवति सूर्यजः ॥ 9 ॥

व्ययजन्मद्वितीयस्थो मृत्युस्थानगतोSपि वा ।
कलत्रस्थो गतो वापि सुप्रीतस्तु सदा शनिः ॥ 10 ॥

अष्टमस्थे सूर्यसुते व्यये जन्मद्वितीयगे ।
कवचं पठतो नित्यं न पीडा जायते क्वचित् ॥ ११ ॥

इत्येतत्कवचं दिव्यं सौरेर्यनिर्मितं पुरा ।
द्वादशाष्टमजन्मस्थदोषान्नाशायते सदा ।
जन्मलग्नास्थितान्दोषान्सर्वान्नाशयते प्रभुः ॥ 11 ॥

॥ इति श्रीब्रह्मांडपुराणे ब्रह्म-नारदसंवादे शनैश्चरकवचं संपूर्णं ॥





Meaning in English

॥ Atha Shri Shani Kavacham ॥
Asya Shri Shanischar Kavach Stotra Mantrasya Kashyap Rishi-hi.
Anushtup Chand-ah. Shanaischro Devata. Shim Shakti-hi.
Shum Kilkam. Shanaischar Prityartham Japey Viniyog-ha.
Shani Kavach is from Brahmananda Purana. Kashyapa is the sage/rishi, Anushtup is chanda, Shanaishchara is god, Shim is shakti, Shooma is Kilakam and it is recited for Lord Shanaishchara.

Nilambaro Nilvapu-hu Kiriti Grudhrasthi Tastrasakaro Dhanushman.
Chaturbhuj-ha Suryasut-ha Prasanna-ha Sada Mam Syadwarad-ha Prashant-ha. ॥ 1 ॥
He has worn blue clothes, his body is also blue and he has a crown on his head shining in blue rays. He is desirous of causing fear to those who are after money. He has four hands, he is son of Surya and he is always happy. He who is calm and quiet, I am requesting blessing for me.

॥ Bramhovaach ॥
Shrunudhva Mrishya-ha Sarve Shani Pidaharam Mahat,
Kavacham Shanirajasya Saureridmanutamam ॥ 2 ॥
Brahma said. O! All rishies listen, originated from Surya, a very auspicious Shani kavach, which makes us free from all type of troubles from Lord Shani.

Kavacham Devatavasam Vajra Panjar Samgyakam,
Shanaischar Pritikaram Sarva Soubhagyadayakam ॥ 3 ॥
This kavach is loved by Lord Shani himself and this kavach is the place where Lord Shani likes to live. It gives us all a type of happiness such as wealth, health and anything we desire and make our body very strong like a weapon.

Om Shri Shanaischar-ha Paatu Bhalam May Surya Nandan-ha,
Netre Chayatmaj-ha Paatu Paatu Karnou Yamanuj-ha ॥ 4 ॥
Om I bow to Lord Shanaichara. Let my forehead be protected by son of Lord Surya. Let my eyes be protected by Chayatmaja, i.e. one who is born from shadow. Let my ears be protected by Yamanuja, who is younger brother of God Yama.

Nasam Vaivswat-ha Paatu Mukham May Bhaskar-ha Sada,
Snigdh Kantsch May Kantam Bhojou Paatu Mahabhuj-ha ॥ 5 ॥
Let my nose be protected by Vaivaswata. Let my mouth be protected by Bhaskara, and let my throat be protected by Snigdha Kanth. Let my arms be protected by Mahabhuja.

Skandhau Paatu Shanaischaiv Karaou Paatu Shubhprad-ha,
Vaksh-ha Paatu Yambhrata Kukshim Patvasitastha ॥ 6 ॥
Let my shoulders be protected by Lord Shani and my hands be protected by Shubhaprada. Let my breast be protected by Yamabhrata. Let my kukshi be protected by Asita, one who is unbounded.

Nabhim Gruhapati-hi Paatu Mand-ha Paatu Katim Tatha,
Uru Mamaantak-ha Paatu Yamo Jaanuyugam Tatha ॥ 7 ॥
Let my navel be protected by Grahapati, and let my loins be protected by Manda. Let my chest be protected by Antaka, and let my knees be protected by Yama.

Padou Mandagati-hi Paatu Sarvaang Paatu Pipal-ha,
Angopangani Sarvani Rakhshenme Surya Nandan-ha ॥ 8 ॥
Let my feet be protected by Mandagati, and let all other parts of my body be protected by Pipala. Let my middle part of the body, and let other parts of the body be protected by the one who is son of Lord Surya.

Itye Tat Kavacham Divyam Pathet Surya Sutasya Yah,
Na Tasya Jaayate Pida Prito Bhavati Suryaj-ha ॥ 9 ॥
Whosoever recites this auspicious kavach of Lord Shani, son of Lord Surya, will be free from any troubles by Lord Shani.

Vyaya Janma Dwitiyastho Mrityusthangatopi Va,
Kalatrastho Gato Vaapi Supritastu Sada Shani-hi ॥ 10 ॥
Even if in any horoscope Lord Shani happens to be in twelfth house, first house, second house, eighth house or in seventh house, it will give good results on reciting this Kavach every day. Otherwise in the above described houses Shani becomes unfavorable and bad results are seen.

Ashtmasthe Surya Sutay Vyayey Janma Dwitiyagey,
Kavacham Pathato Nityam Na Pida Jaayate Kwachit ॥ 11 ॥
Eighth house, twelfth house, first house, second house - all these are considered very bad houses for Lord Shani and very bad results are seen and experienced. However anybody having Lord Shani in the above houses in his horoscope if recited this Shani Kavach daily becomes free from all troubles.

Itye Tatkavacham Divyam Saure Yar Nirmitam Pura,
Dwadashatam Janmastha Doshana Shayate Sada,
Janma Lagna Sthitanando Shansarvanashyate Prabhu-hu ॥ 11 ॥
This is a very auspicious and spiritual Shani Kavach, which is very ancient creation. This removes all the bad effects of Lord Shani when it occupies twelefth, eighth or first house in the horoscope, at the time of the birth, which are evil houses for Lord Shani. Lord Shani always destroys all evil effects arising from the time of birth.

॥ Iti Shri Bramhand Purane Bramha Naarad Samvaade Shanaischar Kavacham Sampurnam ॥
Here the Shani Kavach is completed which is from Brahmananda Purana and discussed by Lord Brahama with Narada Rishi.
