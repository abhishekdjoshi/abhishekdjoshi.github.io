Shani Gayatri Mantra
शनि गायत्री मंत्र


This is the lyrics of Shani Gayatri Mantra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Shani Gayatri Mantra in English. The Shani Gayatri Mantra is read to please Lord Shani.




English

Om Shiro Rupaaya Vidmahe Mrityu Rupaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Souri-hi Prachodayat. ॥

Om Shanaischraaya Vidmahe Chhaya Putraaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Mand-ha Prachodayat. ॥

Om Kaakadhwajaaya Vidmahae Khadga Hastaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Mand-ha Prachodayaat. ॥

Nilanjana Samabhasam Raviputram Yamagrajam,
Chaya Martanda Sambhutam Tam Namami Shanaischaram. ॥





Devanagari Script

ॐ शिरोरुपाय विद्मिहे मृत्युरुपाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो: सौरि: प्रचोदयात ॥

ॐ शनैश्चराय विदमहे छायापुत्राय धीमहि ।
तन्नो मंद: प्रचोदयात ॥

ॐ काकध्वजाय विद्महे खड्गहस्ताय धीमहि ।
तन्नो मन्दः प्रचोदयात ॥

नीलांजनसमाभासं रविपुत्रं यमाग्रजम्‌ ।
छायामार्तण्ड सम्भूतं तं नमामि शनैश्चरम्‌ ॥





Meaning in English

Om Shiro Rupaaya Vidmahe Mrityu Rupaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno-ho Souri-hi Prachodayat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him, who is shaped like a headband and who can sometimes come in the form of death, and let Lord Shani illuminate my mind.

Om Shanaischraaya Vidmahe Chhaya Putraaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Mand-ha Prachodayat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him, who is Lord Shani and who is son of shadown of Lord Sun, and let Lord Shani illuminate my mind.

Om Kaakadhwajaaya Vidmahae Khadga Hastaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Mand-ha Prachodayaat. ॥
Om, let me meditate to him, who has crow in his flag and who has a sword in his hand, togive me higher intellect, and let Lord Shani illuminate my mind.

Nilanjana Samabhasam Raviputram Yamagrajam,
Chaya Martanda Sambhutam Tam Namami Shanaischaram. ॥
Looking like a blue cloud, the son of the Sun, he is the foremost of those who control. He can even put his shadow over the glorious sun. To the Lord Shani, the emblem of control, we bow down in devotion.
