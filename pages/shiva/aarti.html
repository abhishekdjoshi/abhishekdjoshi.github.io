Hansanan Garudasan Vrish-vahan Sajai
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥2॥

Trigun Roop Nirakhta Tribhuvan Jan Mohe
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥3॥

Chadan Mrigmad Sohai Bhale Shashi Dhari
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥4॥

Sankadik Garunadik Bhootadik Sange
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥5॥

Sukhkari Dukhhari Jag Palankari

Devanagari Script

जय शिव ओंकारा, ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ।
ब्रह्मा, विष्णु, सदाशिव, अर्द्धांगी धारा ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥1॥

एकानन चतुरानन पंचानन राजे ।
हंसासन गरूड़ासन वृषवाहन साजे ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥2॥

दो भुज चार चतुर्भुज दसभुज अति सोहे ।
त्रिगुण रूप निरखते त्रिभुवन जन मोहे ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥3॥

अक्षमाला वनमाला मुण्डमाला धारी ।
त्रिपुरारी कंसारी कर माला धारी ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥4॥

श्वेतांबर पीतांबर बाघंबर अंगे ।
सनकादिक गरुणादिक भूतादिक संगे ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥5॥

कर के मध्य कमंडलु चक्र त्रिशूलधारी ।
सुखकारी दुखहारी जगपालन कारी ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥6॥

ब्रह्मा विष्णु सदाशिव जानत अविवेका ।
प्रणवाक्षर में शोभित ये तीनों एका ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥7॥

त्रिगुणस्वामी जी की आरति जो कोइ नर गावे ।
कहत शिवानंद स्वामी सुख संपति पावे ।
ॐ जय शिव ओंकारा ॥8॥





Meaning in English

Jai Shiv Omkara, Om Jai Shiv Omkara
Brahma Vishnu Sadashiv Ardhagni Dhara
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥1॥
Glory to you, O Shiva! Glory to you, O Omkaara! May Brahma, Vishnu and the assembly of other gods, including the great Lord Shiva, relieve me of my afflictions.

Ekanan Chaturanan Panchanan Rajai
Hansanan Garudasan Vrish-vahan Sajai
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥2॥
Being the Absolute, True being, Consciousness and Bliss, you play the roles of all the three Gods - Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva. As Vishnu, you have but one face, as Brahma four and as Shiva five. They gladden the sight of all who behold them. As Brahma you prefer the back of the swan for your seat, as Vishnu you like to ensconce yourself on the back of Garuda (A large mythological eagle - like bird believed to be the vehicle of Lord Vishnu) and as Shiva you make the sacred bull your conveyance; all these stand ready. O Great Lord, pray rid me of my afflictions.

Do Bhuj Char Chaturbhuj Das Bhuj Ati Sohe
Trigun Roop Nirakhta Tribhuvan Jan Mohe
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥3॥
As Brahma, you possess two arms, as Vishnu four and as Shiva (Dashabaahu) ten, all of which look matchlessly lovely. No sooner do the inhabitants of the three spheres behold you than they are all enchanted. O great Lord Omkaara, pray rid me of my afflictions.

Akshaymala Vanmala Mundmala Dhari
Chadan Mrigmad Sohai Bhale Shashi Dhari
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥4॥
You are, O great Lord Omkaara, wearing a garland of Rudraaksha, another of forest flowers the third of skulls; your forehead, glistening in the moonlight which it holds, is smeared with sandal-paste and musk. Pray rid me of my afflictions.

Shvetambar Pitambar Baghambar Ange
Sankadik Garunadik Bhootadik Sange
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥5॥
O great Lord Omkaara, your body is attired in white and yellow silken clothes and in tiger skin, while in your company are troops of goblins, gods like Brahma and divine seers like Sanaka. Pray rid me of my afflictions.

Kar ke Mashya Kamandalu Chakra Trishooldhari
Sukhkari Dukhhari Jag Palankari
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥6॥
O great Lord Omkaara, you hold akamandalu (the mendicants water-jar) in one of your hands and in another a trident; you bring joy to all, destroy all distress and sustain the whole world. May you rid me of all my afflictions.

Brahma Vishnu Sadashiv Jaanat Aviveka
Pranvaakshar Mein Shobhit Yah Tinon Eka
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥7॥
The ignorant (unwise and stupid) know Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva as three individual gods, but they are all indistinguishably fused into a single mystic syllable ‘OM’. Pray rid me of my afflictions.

Trigun Swami ji Ki Aarti Jo Koi Nar Gave
Kahat Shivanand Swami Manvanchhit Phal Pave
Om Jai Shiv Omkara ॥8॥
Says Swami Shivananda, “He who recites this Arti to the Lord of the three gunas-sattva, rajas and tamas - attains fulfillment of his heart’s desire”. O great Lord Omkaara, may you rid me of my afflictions.
