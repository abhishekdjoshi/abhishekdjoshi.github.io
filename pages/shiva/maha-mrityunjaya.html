Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra | महा मृत्युंजय मंत्र


English

Om Triyambakam Yajamahe
Sungandhim Pushti Vardhanam
Urvarukamiv Bandhnanan
Mrutyor Mukshiya Ma Mrutat



Devanagari Script

ॐ त्र्यम्बकम् यजामहे
सुगन्धिम् पुष्टिवर्धनम् |
उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान्
मृत्योर्मुक्षीय मा मृतात् ||



Meaning in English

Om Triyambakam Yajamahe
Sungandhim Pushti Vardhanam
Urvarukamiv Bandhnanan
Mrutyor Mukshiya Ma Mrutat

We worship the three-eyed one, Lord Siva, who is fragrant and who nourishes all beings; may He liberate me from death, for the sake of immortality, even as the fruit is severed from its bondage.
