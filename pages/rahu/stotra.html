Rahu Stotram / Rahu Stotra
राहु स्तोत्रम् / राहु स्तोत्र


This is the lyrics of Rahu Stotra or Stotram in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Rahu Stotra or Stotram in English. The Rahu Stotra or Stotram is read to please Lord Rahu.




English

Asya Shri Rahu Stotrasya Vamdev Rishi-hi. Gayatri Chand-ha. Rahurdevata.
Rahu Prityartham Jape Viniyog-ha.

Rahur Dhanavamantri Cha Simhika Chitha Nandana,
Ardhakaya-ha Sada Krodhi Chandradithya Vimardhan-ha. ॥ 1 ॥

Roudro Rudra Priyo Daitya-ha Swar Bhanurbhanu Mitida-ha,
Graha Raja Sudhapayi Rakatithya Bhilashuka-ha. ॥ 2 ॥

Kala Drushti Kala Roopa Sri Kanta Hridayashraya,
Vidhunthudha Saimhikeyo Ghora Roopo Mahabala-ha. ॥ 3 ॥

Graha Pida Karo Damshtri Raktha Nethro Mahodhara,
Panchavimsathi Namani Sthuthwa Rahum Sada Nara. ॥ 4 ॥

Ya Paden Mahathi Pida Thasya Nasyathi Kevalam,
Aarogyam Putham Athulam Sriyam Dhanyam Pasum Sthadha. ॥ 5 ॥

Dadhathi Rahu Sthasmai Thu Ya Padeth Stotra Muthamam,
Sathatham Padathe Yasthu Jeeveth Varsha Satham Nara. ॥ 6 ॥

Iti Shri Skand Purane Rahu Stotram Sampoornam.





Devanagari Script

अस्य श्रीराहुस्तोत्रस्य वामदेव ऋषिः । गायत्री छन्दः । राहुर्देवता ।
राहुप्रीत्यर्थं जपे विनियोगः ॥

राहुर्दानव मन्त्री च सिंहिकाचित्तनन्दनः ।
अर्धकायः सदाक्रोधी चन्द्रादित्यविमर्दनः ॥ 1 ॥

रौद्रो रुद्रप्रियो दैत्यः स्वर्भानुर्भानुमीतिदः ।
ग्रहराजः सुधापायी राकातिथ्यभिलाषुकः ॥ 2 ॥

कालदृष्टिः कालरुपः श्रीकष्ठह्रदयाश्रयः ।
विधुंतुदः सैंहिकेयो घोररुपो महाबलः ॥ 3 ॥

ग्रहपीडाकरो द्रंष्टी रक्तनेत्रो महोदरः ।
पञ्चविंशति नामानि स्मृत्वा राहुं सदा नरः ॥ 4 ॥

यः पठेन्महती पीडा तस्य नश्यति केवलम् ।
विरोग्यं पुत्रमतुलां श्रियं धान्यं पशूंस्तथा ॥ 5 ॥

ददाति राहुस्तस्मै यः पठते स्तोत्रमुत्तमम् ।
सततं पठते यस्तु जीवेद्वर्षशतं नरः ॥ 6 ॥

इति श्रीस्कन्दपुराणे राहुस्तोत्रं संपूर्णम् ॥





Meaning in English

Asya Shri Rahu Stotrasya Vamdev Rishi-hi. Gayatri Chand-ha. Rahurdevata.
Rahu Prityartham Jape Viniyog-ha.
This is the beginning of the Lord Rahu Stotra. This is an appropriate form to express your admiration to Lord Rahu.

Rahur Dhanavamantri Cha Simhika Chitha Nandana,
Ardhakaya-ha Sada Krodhi Chandradithya Vimardhan-ha. ॥ 1 ॥
Rahu, minister of rakshasa, one who makes Simhika happy. One who is half bodied and who is always angry, and who is tormentor who troubles Sun and Moon.

Roudro Rudra Priyo Daitya-ha Swar Bhanurbhanu Mitida-ha,
Graha Raja Sudhapayi Rakatithya Bhilashuka-ha. ॥ 2 ॥
Angry one, devotee of Rudhra and one who is near the Sun and one who terrifies the sun. One who is king of planets, one who got nectar, and one who desires the moon and the sun.

Kala Drushti Kala Roopa Sri Kanta Hridayashraya,
Vidhunthudha Saimhikeyo Ghora Roopo Mahabala-ha. ॥ 3 ॥
One who has death inflicting sight and one who likes death. One who lives in the heart of Shiva and one who made moon dim or lose its shine. One who is the son of Simhika and one who has terrifying form and is also very strong.

Graha Pida Karo Damshtri Raktha Nethro Mahodhara,
Panchavimsathi Namani Sthuthwa Rahum Sada Nara. ॥ 4 ॥
One who torments planets, one who has big teeth and one who has red eyes and one who has a big paunch. One should recite this twenty five names and pray to Rahu.

Ya Paden Mahathi Pida Thasya Nasyathi Kevalam,
Aarogyam Putham Athulam Sriyam Dhanyam Pasum Sthadha. ॥ 5 ॥
If a man recites these twenty five names and prays to Rahu, his big tormenting troubles vanish immediately, and he gets health, incomparable sons, wealth and cereals and animals.

Dadhathi Rahu Sthasmai Thu Ya Padeth Stotra Muthamam,
Sathatham Padathe Yasthu Jeeveth Varsha Satham Nara. ॥ 6 ॥
That person, who reads this, would also be given other things by Lord Rahu. The man who reads this regularly would live for one hundred years.

Iti Shri Skand Purane Rahu Stotram Sampoornam.
This completes the Lord Rahu's Stotram.
