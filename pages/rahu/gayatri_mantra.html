Rahu Gayatri Mantra
राहु गायत्री मंत्र


This is the lyrics of Rahu Gayatri Mantra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Rahu Gayatri Mantra in English. The Rahu Gayatri Mantra is read to please Lord Rahu.




English

Om Shiro Rupaay Vidhmhe Amrateshaay Dhimahi,
Tanno Rahu: Prachodyat. ॥

Om Naakadhwajaaya Vidmahae Padma Hastaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Raahu: Prachodayaat. ॥

Om Neelvarnaay Vidhmahe Sainhikaay Dhimahi,
Tanno Rahu Prachodyat. ॥

Om Ardhkaayam Mahaviryam Chandra Aditya Vmardanam,
Simhika Garbha Sambhootam Tam Rahum Pranamamyaham. ॥

Om Bhraam Bhrim Bhraum Sah Rahve Namah. ॥
Om Aim Hrim Rahve Namah. ॥





Devanagari Script

ॐ शिरो-रूपाय विदमहे अमृतेशाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो राहुः प्रचोदयात् ॥

ॐ नाकाध्वजाय विद्महे पद्माहस्ताय धीमहि ।
तन्नो राहू: प्रचोदयात ॥

ॐ नीलवर्णाय विद्महे सैंहिकेयाय धीमहि ।
तन्नो राहुः प्रचोदयात् ॥

ॐ अर्धकायं महावीर्यं चन्द्रादित्यविमर्दनम् ।
सिंहिकागर्भसम्भूतं तं राहुं प्रणमाम्यहम् ॥

ॐ भ्रां भ्रीं भ्रौं सः राहवे नमः ॥
ऊँ ऎं ह्रीं राहवे नम: ॥





Meaning in English

Om Shiro Rupaay Vidhmhe Amrateshaay Dhimahi,
Tanno Rahu: Prachodyat. ॥
Om, I meditate to Lord Rahu, who is shaped like a headband and who is an eternal one, i.e. one who has consumed amrit. Let Lord Rahu kindle my intellect and illuminate it.

Om Naakadhwajaaya Vidmahae Padma Hastaaya Dhimahi,
Tanno Raahu: Prachodayaat. ॥
Om, I meditate to Lord Rahu, who has a flag bearing snake's image and holding a lotus in his hands. Let Lord Rahu kindle my intellect and illuminate it.

Om Neelvarnaay Vidhmahe Sainhikaay Dhimahi,
Tanno Rahu Prachodyat. ॥
Om, I meditate to Lord Rahu, who is blue colored and who is an eternal one. Let Lord Rahu kindle my intellect and illuminate it.

Om Ardhkaayam Mahaviryam Chandra Aditya Vmardanam,
Simhika Garbha Sambhootam Tam Rahum Pranamamyaham. ॥
Om, I bow down to Lord Rahu who has only half body, but possesses the power to subdue even the sun and the moon. He was born from the womb of Simhika.

Om Bhraam Bhrim Bhraum Sah Rahve Namah. ॥
Om Aim Hrim Rahve Namah. ॥
Om, I bow to Lord Rahu with all my might.
Om, I bow to Lord Rahu.
