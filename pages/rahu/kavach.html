Rahu Kavach
राहु कवच


This is the lyrics of Rahu Kavach in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Rahu Kavach in English. The Rahu Kavach is read to please Lord Rahu.




English

Asya Shri Rahu Kavach Stotra Mantrasya Chandrama Rishihi.
Anushtup Chandha Ram Bijam Namaha Shaktihi.
Swaha Kilakam Rahu Prithyartham Jape Viniyogaha.

Pranamami Sada Rahum Shoorpakaram Kiritinam,
Sainikeyam Karalasyam Lokanam Bhayapradam. ॥ 1 ॥

Nilambaraha Shiraha Patu Lalaatam Lokvanditaha,
Chakshushi Paatu Me Rahuhu Shrotre Tvardha Shariravaan. ॥ 2 ॥

Naasikaam Me Dhoomrvarnaha Shoolapaanirmukham Mam,
Givaam Me Sinhika Soonu Kantham Me Kathinaagrikaha. ॥ 3 ॥

Bhujamgesho Bhujou Paatu Nilamaalya Ambarha Karou,
Paatu Vaksha Sthalam Mantri Paatu Kukshi Vidhuntudaha. ॥ 4 ॥

Katim Me Vikataha Paatu Urum Me Soor Poojitaha,
Swar Bhaanur Jaanuni Paatu Janghe Me Paatu Janghyaha. ॥ 5 ॥

Gulfou Grahapatihi Paatu Paadou Me Bhishana Krutihi,
Sarvaani Angaani Me Paatu Nilash Chandan Bhushanaha. ॥ 6 ॥

Rahor Idam Kavach Amrudhidavastudam Yo,
Haktta Pathatya Nudinam Niyataha Shuchihi San,
Praapnoti Kirtim Atulaam Shrimruddhimaayu,
Raarogyam Aatmavijayam Cha Hi Tat Prasaadaat. ॥ 7 ॥

॥ Iti Shri Maahaabhaarate Dhrutarashtra Sanjay Samvaade Dronparvani Raahu Kavacham Sampoornam ॥





Devanagari Script

अथ राहुकवचम्
अस्य श्रीराहुकवचस्तोत्रमंत्रस्य चंद्रमा ऋषिः ॥
अनुष्टुप छन्दः । रां बीजं । नमः शक्तिः ॥
स्वाहा कीलकम् । राहुप्रीत्यर्थं जपे विनियोगः ॥

प्रणमामि सदा राहुं शूर्पाकारं किरीटिन् ॥
सैन्हिकेयं करालास्यं लोकानाम भयप्रदम् ॥ 1 ॥

निलांबरः शिरः पातु ललाटं लोकवन्दितः ॥
चक्षुषी पातु मे राहुः श्रोत्रे त्वर्धशरीरवान् ॥ 2 ॥

नासिकां मे धूम्रवर्णः शूलपाणिर्मुखं मम ॥
जिव्हां मे सिंहिकासूनुः कंठं मे कठिनांघ्रीकः ॥ 3 ॥

भुजङ्गेशो भुजौ पातु निलमाल्याम्बरः करौ ॥
पातु वक्षःस्थलं मंत्री पातु कुक्षिं विधुंतुदः ॥ 4 ॥

कटिं मे विकटः पातु ऊरु मे सुरपूजितः ॥
स्वर्भानुर्जानुनी पातु जंघे मे पातु जाड्यहा ॥ 5 ॥

गुल्फ़ौ ग्रहपतिः पातु पादौ मे भीषणाकृतिः ॥
सर्वाणि अंगानि मे पातु निलश्चंदनभूषण: ॥ 6 ॥

राहोरिदं कवचमृद्धिदवस्तुदं यो ॥
भक्ता पठत्यनुदिनं नियतः शुचिः सन् ॥
प्राप्नोति कीर्तिमतुलां श्रियमृद्धिमायु ॥
रारोग्यमात्मविजयं च हि तत्प्रसादात् ॥ 7 ॥

॥ इति श्रीमहाभारते धृतराष्ट्रसंजयसंवादे द्रोणपर्वणि राहुकवचं संपूर्णं ॥





Meaning in English

Asya Shri Rahu Kavach Stotra Mantrasya Chandrama Rishihi.
Anushtup Chandha Ram Bijam Namaha Shaktihi.
Swaha Kilakam Rahu Prithyartham Jape Viniyogaha.
Chandrama is the rishi of Rahu Kavacham. Anustup is the Chanda. Ram is the bija. Namaha is the shakti. Svaaha is the kilakam. This Rahu Kavacham is recited for Lord Rahu so that all troubles rising because Lord Rahu is badly placed in the horoscope can be removed.

Pranamami Sada Rahum Shoorpakaram Kiritinam,
Sainikeyam Karalasyam Lokanam Bhayapradam. ॥ 1 ॥
I always bow to Lord Rahu whose crown is like winnowing fan. Lord Rahu is son of Simhika i.e. lioness. He removes troubles of people.

Nilambaraha Shiraha Patu Lalaatam Lokvanditaha,
Chakshushi Paatu Me Rahuhu Shrotre Tvardha Shariravaan. ॥ 2 ॥
Let my head be protected by Nilambar, one who has worn blue cloths. Let my forehead be protected by Lord Rahu, who is called Lokavandita, as people always bow to him. Let my eyes be protected by Lord Rahu. Let my ears be protected by Ardhasharirvan, one who has only body and no head.

Naasikaam Me Dhoomrvarnaha Shoolapaanirmukham Mam,
Givaam Me Sinhika Soonu Kantham Me Kathinaagrikaha. ॥ 3 ॥
Let my nose be protected by Dhumravarna, one who has smoke color body. Let my mouth be protected by Shoolapani. Let my tong be protected by son of lioness. Let my throat be protected by Kathinaaghrika.

Bhujamgesho Bhujou Paatu Nilamaalya Ambarha Karou,
Paatu Vaksha Sthalam Mantri Paatu Kukshi Vidhuntudaha. ॥ 4 ॥
Let my arms be protected by god of serpents. Let my hands be protected by Nilamalyambara, one who has worn blue color garlands and blue clothes. Let my breast be protected by Mantri. Let my kukshi be protected by Lord Rahu, who is also called Vidhuntuda as he is destroyer of Moon.

Katim Me Vikataha Paatu Urum Me Soor Poojitaha,
Swar Bhaanur Jaanuni Paatu Janghe Me Paatu Janghyaha. ॥ 5 ॥
Let my waist be protected by Vikata. Let my breast be protected by Surpoojita, one who is worshiped by Gods. Let my knees be protected by Svabhanu. Let my thigh be protected by Jadyaha.

Gulfou Grahapatihi Paatu Paadou Me Bhishana Krutihi,
Sarvaani Angaani Me Paatu Nilash Chandan Bhushanaha. ॥ 6 ॥
Let my ankle be protected by Grahpati. Let my feet be protected by Bhishanakruti. Let all my other parts of the body be protected by Nil, Chandan Bhushana, who has blue and sandal pest applied on his body.

Rahor Idam Kavach Amrudhidavastudam Yo,
Haktta Pathatya Nudinam Niyataha Shuchihi San,
Praapnoti Kirtim Atulaam Shrimruddhimaayu,
Raarogyam Aatmavijayam Cha Hi Tat Prasaadaat. ॥ 7 ॥
Whosoever recites this Rahu Kavacham with devotion and faith, every day will receive money, fame, long healthy life and spiritual knowledge by the blessing of Lord Rahu.

॥ Iti shri maahaabhaarate dhrutarashtra-sanjay samvaade dronparvani Raahu kavacham sampoornam ॥
Thus here completes the Rahu Kavacham. It is from Shri Mahabharat and and it has arisen through the discussion of Dhritarashtra and Sanjay at the time of Dronparvani.
