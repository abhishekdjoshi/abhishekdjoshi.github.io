Saraswati Mata Aarti
सरस्वती माता आरती


English

Asya Sri Saravanthio Kavacha Stotra Maha Manthrasya, Samyogeshwara Rishi,
Anushtup Chandha, Sri Saraswathi Devata, Sri Saraswathi Prasada Sidhyarthe Jape Viniyoga.
|| Dhyan ||

Srimad Chandana Charcithojjwala Vapu, Shuklambara Mallika
Malaa Lalitha Kunthalaa Pravilasad Mukthavali Shobhitha. ||1||
Sarva Jnana Nidhana Pusthaka Dara, Rudraksha Malangitha,
Vagdevi Vadanambhuje Vasathu May, Trilokya Mathaa Shubhaa. ||2||

|| Kavach ||

Omkaro May Sira Pathu, Imkara Pathu May Mukham,
Sakara Pathu May Nethre, Rakara Srothra Yugmakam. ||3||

Swakaro Nasikaam Pathu Traikao Ganda Yugmagam,
Nakara Pathwoshta Yugmakam, Makao Dandha Panmkthim. ||4||

Jihwam Vidhyaakalaa Pathum, Vacham Vagdevatha Mama,
Kantam May Suswara Pathu, Vaksha Karunya Manasa. ||5||

Hasthou Pusthakahastha May, Kukshim Vatadalodhara,
Katim Kanaka Vasthradya, Ooru Ramborukaa Mama. ||6||

Guhyam Sugopya Bheejadyaa, Padhou Pathu Nathamaraa,
Sarvangam Sarvadhaa Pathu Sarva Poojya Saraswathi. ||7||

Bharathi Kavacham Punyam Padathaam Sarva Sidhidham,
Sarva Vidhyapradham Sreshtam Kumathi Dwandha Bhaskaram. ||8||

|| Saraswathi Kavacham Sampoornam. ||





Devanagari Script

अस्य श्री सर्वानतो कवच स्तोत्र मन्त्रस्य, सम्योगेश्वर ऋषि,
अनुष्टुप चन्ध श्री सरस्वती देवता, श्री सरस्वती प्रशाद शिथार्थे जपे विनियोग |

|| ध्यान ||

श्रीमद चंदना चरितोज्वज्वल वपु, शुक्लाम्बरा मल्लिका,
माला ललिता, कुंथला प्रवीलासाद मुक्तावली शोभित ।।

सर्व ज्ञान निधान पूषा दारा, रुद्राक्ष मलंगित,
वाग्देवी वदनमभूजे वसु मे, त्रैलोक्य माथा शुभा ।।

|| कवच ||

ओमकारो सिरा पाथु, इमकारा पथु मूकम,
सकरा पथु मे निठरे, रकारे श्रोत्र युगमकम् ।।

स्वकारो नासिकाम् पथु त्रिकौ गन्दा युगमगम,
नाकरा पाथवोषा युगमकम, मकाओ दंड पद्मक्तिम ।।

जिह्वाम् विद्याकालं पथम्, वचम् योनिदेवता मम,
कण्ठम सुस्वरा पातु, वक्ष करुणा मनसा ।।

हस्थो पुष्पकस्थ, कुक्षिम वटदलधारा,
कटीम कनक विशारद्या, ऊरु रंबोरुका मामा ।।

गुह्यम सुपोय भजेद्य, पादहौ पथु नाथमारा,
सर्वंगम सर्वधा पथु सर्व पूजा सरस्वती ।।

भारती कवचम पुण्यम पाथम् सर्व सिद्धम्,
सर्व विद्याप्रदम् श्रीत्तम कुमति द्वंद्व भास्करम् ।।

।। सरस्वती कवचम् सम्पूर्णम् ।।





Meaning in English

Asya Sri Saravanthio Kavacha Stotra Maha Manthrasya, Samyogeshwara Rishi,
Anushtup Chandha, Sri Saraswathi Devata, Sri Saraswathi Prasada Sidhyarthe Jape Viniyoga.
For the great prayer called 'the armour of Saraswathi', the saint is Samyogeswara, the goddess addressed is Saraswathi and this is being sung for obtaining the grace of Goddess Saraswathi.

|| Dhyan ||

Srimad Chandana Charcithojjwala Vapu, Shuklambara Mallika
Malaa Lalitha Kunthalaa Pravilasad Mukthavali Shobhitha. ||1||
Sarva Jnana Nidhana Pusthaka Dara, Rudraksha Malangitha,
Vagdevi Vadanambhuje Vasathu May, Trilokya Mathaa Shubhaa. ||2||
She who is the mother of the three worlds, with a shining body coated with sandal paste. Wearing white silk, wearing the garland of jasmine, wearing a shining head gear and shining nose ring, holding a book containing all knowledge. Wearing a garland of Rudraksha is the Goddess of speech and I live in her lotus like face.

|| Kavach ||

Omkaro May Sira Pathu, Imkara Pathu May Mukham,
Sakara Pathu May Nethre, Rakara Srothra Yugmakam. ||3||
Let “Om” protect my head, “Iym” protect my face, “sa” protect my eyes and “ra” protect my two ears.

Swakaro Nasikaam Pathu Traikao Ganda Yugmagam,
Nakara Pathwoshta Yugmakam, Makao Dandha Panmkthim. ||4||
Let “Swa” protect my nose, “Trai” protect both my cheeks, “Na” protect my lips, “Ma” protect my teeth.

Jihwam Vidhyaakalaa Pathum, Vacham Vagdevatha Mama,
Kantam May Suswara Pathu, Vaksha Karunya Manasa. ||5||
Let the crescent of knowledge protect my toungue, let the goddess of word protect my words. Let she with a musical voice protect my neck, and let her with a mind of mercy protect me.

Hasthou Pusthakahastha May, Kukshim Vatadalodhara,
Katim Kanaka Vasthradya, Ooru Ramborukaa Mama. ||6||
Let my hands be protected by her who holds a book, let my abdomen be protected by her with a belly like a banyan leaf. Let my waist be protected by her who wears golden cloths, and let my thigh be protected by her who is like Rambha.

Guhyam Sugopya Bheejadyaa, Padhou Pathu Nathamaraa,
Sarvangam Sarvadhaa Pathu Sarva Poojya Saraswathi. ||7||
Let my parts be protected by her whose root chant is secret, let my feet be protected by her whose devotees are devas. And let all my limbs be always protected by her, who is the always worshipped Goddess Saraswathi.

Bharathi Kavacham Punyam Padathaam Sarva Sidhidham,
Sarva Vidhyapradham Sreshtam Kumathi Dwandha Bhaskaram. ||8||
This armour of Goddess Saraswathi is holy and by reading it everything can be achieved. And it blesses you will all knowledge and it is great and like a sun which enlightens the bad ones.

|| Saraswathi Kavacham Sampoornam. ||
Thus ends the armour of Saraswathi.
