Saraswati Mata Aarti
सरस्वती माता आरती


English

Aarti Shri Sarawati Ji

Jai Saraswati Mata, Maiya Jai Saraswati Mata,
Sadgun Vaibhav Shalini, Tribhuvan Vikhyata.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥1॥

Chandravadini Padamasini Dyuti Mangalkari,
Sohe Shubh Hans Sawari, Atul Tej Dhari.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥2॥

Bayen Kar Mein Veena, Dayen Kar Mala,
Shish Mukut Mani Sohe, Gal Motiyan Mala.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥3॥

Devi Sharan Jo Aye, Unka Uddhar Kiya,
Paithi Manthra Daasi, Raavan Sanhar Kiya.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥4॥

Vidya Gyan Pradayini, Gyan Prakash Bharo,
Moh Agyan Aur Timir Ka, Jag Se Naash Karo.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥5॥

Dhoop Deep Phal Mewa, Maa Swikaar Karo,
Gyan Chakshu De Mata, Jag Nistaar Karo.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥6॥

Maa Saraswati Ki Aarti, Jo Koi Jan Gave,
Hitkari Sukhkari Gyan Bhakti Pave
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥7॥

Jai Saraswati Mata, Jai Jai Saraswati Mata,
Sadgun Vaibhav Shalini, Tribhuvan Vikhyata.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥8॥

जय सरस्वती माता ॥1॥

चन्द्रवदनि पद्मासिनि, द्युति मंगलकारी ।

जय सरस्वती माता ॥2॥

बाएं कर में वीणा, दाएं कर माला।
शीश मुकुट मणि सोहे, गल मोतियन माला॥
जय सरस्वती माता ॥3॥

देवी शरण जो आए, उनका उद्धार किया ।

जय सरस्वती माता ॥4॥

विद्या ज्ञान प्रदायिनि, ज्ञान प्रकाश भरो ।

जय सरस्वती माता ॥5॥

जय सरस्वती माता ॥6॥

माँ सरस्वती की आरती, जो कोई जन गावे ।

जय सरस्वती माता ॥7॥

जय सरस्वती माता, जय जय सरस्वती माता ।
सदगुण वैभव शालिनी, त्रिभुवन विख्याता ॥
जय सरस्वती माता ॥8॥





Meaning in English

Aarti Shri Sarawati Ji
Prayers and bows to Goddess Saraswati mother.

Jai Saraswati Mata, Maiya Jai Saraswati Mata,
Sadgun Vaibhav Shalini, Tribhuvan Vikhyata.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥1॥
Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati, glory to you. You are the treasurer of all the goodness and virtues, you are the ruler of the three worlds. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Chandravadini Padamasini Dyuti Mangalkari,
Sohe Shubh Hans Sawari, Atul Tej Dhari.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥2॥
You radiate like the pleasant Moon and seated on a lotus, you are the epitome of auspiciousness. You are mounted on a white swan, and your grace is incomparable and so is the beauty of your swan. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Bayen Kar Mein Veena, Dayen Kar Mala,
Shish Mukut Mani Sohe, Gal Motiyan Mala.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥3॥
You hold the Veena in your left hand and a Mala in your right hand. The bejewelled crown adorns your head, and the pearl necklace shimmers around your neck. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Devi Sharan Jo Aye, Unka Uddhar Kiya,
Paithi Manthra Daasi, Raavan Sanhar Kiya.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥4॥
Whoever comes to you, gets blessed. You are the one who influenced Kaikeyi's maid Manthara to annihilate Ravana. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Vidya Gyan Pradayini, Gyan Prakash Bharo,
Moh Agyan Aur Timir Ka, Jag Se Naash Karo.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥5॥
You are the giver of knowledge, bless us with wisdom, learning and intellect. Dispel darkness, greed and ignorance by uprooting them from the world. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Dhoop Deep Phal Mewa, Maa Swikaar Karo,
Gyan Chakshu De Mata, Jag Nistaar Karo.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥6॥
Mother Goddess, please accept my humble offerings, the dhoop, deep, fruits and meva. Please bless us know the eyes that can dismiss ignorance and shower the world with your radiance. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Maa Saraswati Ki Aarti, Jo Koi Jan Gave,
Hitkari Sukhkari Gyan Bhakti Pave
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥7॥
Those who sing the Arti of Mother Goddess Saraswati. Shall get blessed and showered by wisdom, light and devotion. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.

Jai Saraswati Mata, Jai Jai Saraswati Mata,
Sadgun Vaibhav Shalini, Tribhuvan Vikhyata.
Jai Saraswati Mata ॥8॥
Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati, glory to you. You are the treasurer of all the goodness and virtues, you are the ruler of the three worlds. Glory to you mother Goddess Saraswati.
