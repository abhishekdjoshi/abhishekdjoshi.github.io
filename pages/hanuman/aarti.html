Devanagari Script

आरती कीजै हनुमान लला की।
दुष्ट दलन रघुनाथ कला की ॥1॥

जाके बल से गिरिवर कांपे।
रोग दोष जाके निकट न झांके ॥2॥

अंजनि पुत्र महाबल दाई।
सन्तन के प्रभु सदा सहाई ॥3॥

दे बीरा रघुनाथ पठाए।
लंका जारि सिया सुधि लाए ॥4॥

लंका सो कोट समुद्र-सी खाई।
जात पवनसुत बार न लाई ॥5॥

लंका जारि असुर संहारे।
सियारामजी के काज सवारे ॥6॥

लक्ष्मण मूर्छित पड़े सकारे।
आनि संजीवन प्राण उबारे ॥7॥

पैठि पाताल तो रिजम-कारे।
अहिरावण की भुजा उखारे ॥8॥

बाएं भुजा असुर दल मारे।
दाहिने भुजा संतजन तारे ॥9॥

सुर नर मुनि आरती उतारें।
जय जय जय हनुमान उचारें ॥10॥

कंचन थार कपूर लौ छाई।
आरती करत अंजना माई ॥11॥

जो हनुमानजी की आरती गावे।
बसि बैकुण्ठ परम पद पावे ॥12॥

लंका विध्वंश किये रघुराई ।
तुलसीदास स्वामी किर्ती गाई ॥13॥

आरती किजे हनुमान लला की ।
दुष्ट दलन रघुनाथ कला की ॥14॥





Meaning in English

Aarti Keejai Hanuman Lalla Ki
Dushth Dalan Raghunath Kalaa Ki ॥1॥
Sing the arati of Lord Hanuman, the destroyer of all that is wicked, an exalted hero in Sri Ram's sportive play on earth.

Jaake Bal Se Girivar Kaanpe
Rog Dosh Jaake Nikat Na Jhaanke ॥2॥
His massive power makes even mountains shake. Neither sickness nor impurities can come close to his devotees.

Anjani Putra Maha Bal Daayi
Santan Ke Prabhu Sadaa Sahai ॥3॥
This son of Mother Anjani is immensely powerful. He always provides help and protection to the good.

De Beera Raghunath Pathai
Lanka Jaari Siya Sudhi Laaye ॥4॥
Lord Ram entrusted brave Hanuman with the task of finding Sita, for which he jumped to Lanka and burnt the capital.

Lanka So Kot Samudra Si Khayee
Jaat Pavan Sut Baar Na Layee ॥5॥
The impregnable fort of Lanka and the deep moat of the surrounding sea could not hamper his progress. He succeeded in bringing news of Sita.

Lanka Jaari Asur Samhaare
Siya Raamji Ke Kaaj Savaare ॥6॥
He burned Lanka, slayed the demons, and completed the work of Lord Ram and Mother Sita.

Lakshman Murchit Padey Sakaare
Aani Sanjeevan Pran Ubaare ॥7॥
When Lakshman was lying unconscious, Hanuman went quickly for the life-saving sanjivana herb. Bringing it by early morning, he saved Lakshman's life.

Paithi Paatal Toh Rijam Kaare
Ahi Raavan Ki Bhuja Ukhaare ॥8॥
Hanuman went quickly to the realm of death and tore apart Ahiravana's arms.

Baayein Bhuja Asur Dal Maare
Daahine Bhuja Sant Jan Taare ॥9॥
Hanuman's mighty arms are ever engaged in protecting the good and slaying the demons.

Sur Nar Muni Aarti Utaare
Jaya Jaya Jaya Hanuman Ucchhaare ॥10॥
All the gods, humans, sages, and seers aver perform his arati and chant 'Glory to Hanuman' with great enthusiasm.

Kanchan Thaar Kapur Lau Chayi
Aarti Karat Anjana Maayi ॥11॥
Mother Anjani performs the arati of her beloved son, burning camphor on a golden plate.

Joh Hanuman Ji Ki Aarti Gaave
Basi Baikunt Param Pad Paave ॥12॥
Whoever sings the arati of Lord Hanuman attains the highest state and dwells in Vaikuntha, the highest heaven.

Lanka Vidhwa-vansh Kiye Raghu-rayee
Tulsidas Swami Kirti Gayee ॥13॥
Swami Tulsidas Ji sings the glory of the Hanuman, who destroyed the Lanka with the blessings of Lord Rama.

Aart Kije Hanuman Lalla Ki
Dushth Dalan Raghunath Kala Ki ॥14॥
Sing the arati of Lord Hanuman, the destroyer of all that is wicked, an exalted hero in Sri Ram's sportive play on earth.
