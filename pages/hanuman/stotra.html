Devanagari Script

भीमरूपी महारुद्रा वज्र हनुमान मारुती ।
वनारी अन्जनीसूता रामदूता प्रभंजना ॥1॥

महाबळी प्राणदाता सकळां उठवी बळें ।
सौख्यकारी दुःखहारी धूर्त वैष्णव गायका ॥2॥

दीननाथा हरीरूपा सुंदरा जगदंतरा ।
पातालदेवताहंता भव्यसिंदूरलेपना ॥3॥

लोकनाथा जगन्नाथा प्राणनाथा पुरातना ।
पुण्यवंता पुण्यशीला पावना परितोषका ॥4॥

ध्वजांगें उचली बाहो आवेशें लोटला पुढें ।
काळाग्नि काळरुद्राग्नि देखतां कांपती भयें ॥5॥

ब्रह्मांडें माइलीं नेणों आंवाळे दंतपंगती ।
नेत्राग्नी चालिल्या ज्वाळा भ्रुकुटी ताठिल्या बळें ॥6॥

पुच्छ तें मुरडिलें माथां किरीटी कुंडलें बरीं ।
सुवर्ण कटि कांसोटी घंटा किंकिणि नागरा ॥7॥

ठकारे पर्वता ऐसा नेटका सडपातळू ।
चपळांग पाहतां मोठें महाविद्युल्लतेपरी ॥8॥

कोटिच्या कोटि उड्डाणें झेंपावे उत्तरेकडे ।
मंदाद्रीसारखा द्रोणू क्रोधें उत्पाटिला बळें ॥9॥

आणिला मागुतीं नेला आला गेला मनोगती ।
मनासी टाकिलें मागें गतीसी तूळणा नसे ॥10॥

अणूपासोनि ब्रह्मांडाएवढा होत जातसे ।
तयासी तुळणा कोठें मेरु मांदार धाकुटे ॥11॥

ब्रह्मांडाभोंवते वेढे वज्रपुच्छें करूं शके ।
तयासी तुळणा कैंची ब्रह्मांडीं पाहतां नसे ॥12॥

आरक्त देखिले डोळां ग्रासिलें सूर्यमंडळा ।
वाढतां वाढतां वाढे भेदिलें शून्यमंडळा ॥13॥

धनधान्य पशुवृद्धि पुत्रपौत्र समग्रही ।
पावती रूपविद्यादि स्तोत्रपाठें करूनियां ॥14॥

भूतप्रेतसमंधादि रोगव्याधि समस्तही ।
नासती तुटती चिंता आनंदे भीमदर्शनें ॥15॥

हे धरा पंधराश्लोकी लाभली शोभली बरी ।
दृढदेहो निःसंदेहो संख्या चंद्रकला गुणें ॥16॥

रामदासीं अग्रगण्यू कपिकुळासि मंडणू ।
रामरूपी अन्तरात्मा दर्शने दोष नासती ॥17॥

॥इति श्री रामदासकृतं संकटनिरसनं नाम श्री मारुतिस्तोत्रम् सम्पूर्णम् ॥



Meaning in English

Bhimroopi Maharudra Vajra Hanuman Maruti
Vanaari Anjani-soota Ramdoota Prabhanjana ॥1॥
The large bodied, the wrathful one, diamond bodied Hanuman, the son of Maruta, who lives in the forest. The son of Anjana, the messenger of Rama, who is like a hurricane.

Mahabali Pranadata Sakalam Uthavi Bale
Saukhya-kari Dukha-hari Dhoort Vaishnav Gayaka ॥2॥
The all powerful, giver of life breaths, the one that rouses others by his strength. The bestow-er of happiness, the remover of sorrows, the shrewd one, who is the singer of Vishnu's praises.

Dina-natha Hari-roopa Sundara Jagadamtara
Pataal Devata Hanta Bhavya Sindur Lepana ॥3॥
The empower-er of the feeble, the one with an ape's face, who is beautiful like the moon, the one that can go to the ends of the world. The destroyer of the lords of Patala, whose gigantic body is smeared with Sindura.

Lokanatha Jagannatha Prana Natha Puratana
Punyavanta Punya Shila Pavna Paritoshka ॥4॥
The one who is the lord of the Lokas, who is the lord of the world, who is the lord of the life breaths, who is ancient. The one who is pure in mind, speech and body, who has pure character, son of Pavana: the pure one, The one who fulfills the desires of his devotees and bestows contentment.

Dhwajange Uchhli Baho Aaveshem Lotala Pudhen
Kala Agni, Kala Rudra Agni Dekhtam Kaampati Bhayem ॥5॥
With his arms raised like a flag staff he charges forward furiously. Even the fires of Yama, and Kaala tremble on seeing him in such a form.

Brahmandem Majhlim Nenon Aamvale Dantpangati
Netra Agni Chaleelya Jwala Bhrukuti Tatheelya Balem ॥6॥
The one in whose mouth the universe is composed when he bares(gnashes) his teeth. Whose eyes emit fire as he contracts his brows in forceful rage.

Puchchha Tem Murdilem Matham Kiriti Kundalem Barim
Suvarna Kati Kansoti Ghanta Kimkini Nagara ॥7॥
The one with his tail coiled on his head as he sports a crown with splendid earrings. The tinkling bells on his golden girdle echo(everywhere).

Thakare Parvata Aesa Netka Sadpatalu
Chaplang Pahtam Mothem Mahavidyul Tepari ॥8॥
Stationed like a gigantic mountain, of perfect limbs and slender form. He is supple and moves in the blink of an eye like a great streak of lightning.

Kotichya Koti Uddanem Zhempave Uttarekade
Mandadri Sarakha Dronu Krodhem Utpateela Balem ॥9॥
The one that flew crores of distance towards the North and violently uprooted the Dronagiri that was like Mandrachal with his strength.

Aaneela Magutim Nela Aala Gela Manogati
Manasi Takeelem Maagem,Gatisi Tulna Nase ॥10॥

Bringing the mountain (to Lanka) he returned and replaced it at the speed of the mind. Even the mind was overcome by his pace and none can match his velocity.

Anupasonee Brahmanda Aevadha Hoth Jaatse
Tayasi Tulna Kothe Meru Mandar Dhakute ॥11॥
From the size of the atom to the size of the universe, he expands. Where can one equate to his size the Meru and Mandara mountains.

Brahmanda Bhonvathe Vedhe Vajra Puchchhem Karum Shake
Tayasi Tulna Kainchi Brahmandim Pahatam Nase ॥12॥
He can siege the universe with his diamond like tail. A comparison to him the universe has never seen.

Aarakta Dekhile Dola Graseele Surya Mandala
Vadhatam Vadhatam Vadhe Bhedile Shunya Mandala ॥13॥
Whose eyes glimpsed the reddened sun and he went to swallow it. (For that) He grew to such and extent that he pierced the sunyamandala.

Dhan Dhanya Pashu Vrudhi Putra Pautra Samgrahi
Paavati Roop Vidyadi Stotra Pathem Karu-niyam ॥14॥
Wealth, foodgrains, cattle, progeny flourish and one obtains health and knowledge when one recites/masters this stotra.

Bhootpreta Samamdhadi Rogvyadhi Samast-hi
Naasti Tutati Chintha Aanande Bhimdarshane ॥15॥
Ghosts, zombies, spirits etc diseases and afflictions all worries are destroyed by the darshana of Hanuman and the devotee attains bliss.

He Dhara Pandhara Shloki Labhali Shobhali Bari
Drudh Deho Nisandeho Sankhya Chandrakala Gunnay ॥16॥
These 15 slokas, that provide gainfulness that purify the body and bestow serenity to the mind, are like the 15 digits of the moon in nature. (Moon is often said to have the quality of Amrita so these verses are like nectar).

Ramdasi Agraganyu Kapi-kulasi Mandanu
Ramroopi Antaratma Darshane Dosh Nasati ॥17॥
You are foremost among the devotees of Rama, the bringer of fame to the race of the Vanaras(Kapi). With the darshana of you, who has the form of Rama in his heart/soul, all faults vanish.

॥ Iti Sri Ramadasa-krutam Sankatanashan Namam Sri Marutistotram Sampoornam ॥
Hereby ends the Maruti Stotra which is capable of destroying all difficulties, composed by Samartha Ramadas.
