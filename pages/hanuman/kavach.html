Hanuman Kavach
हनुमान कवच


Lyrics of Hanuman Kavach in English and Hindi/Devanagari script, and the meaning of Hanuman Kavach in English. Hanuman Kavach is read in devotion to and to please Lord Hanuman.




English

Shree Ganeshay Namah
Om Asya Shri Panchamukha Hanuman,
Mantrasya Brahma Rishihi

Gaayatree Chandaha
Panchamukha Viraata Hanumaana Devataa Hreem Beejam
Shreem Shaktihi Kraum Keelakam Kroom Kavacham
Kraim Astraaya Phat Iti Digbandhah.

Shree Garuda Uvaacha
Atha Dhyaanam Pravakshyaami,
Shrunu Sarvaanga Sundari,
Yat Krutam Devedevana,
Dhyaanam Hanumanah Priyam. || 1 ||

Panchavaktram Maha Abhimam,
Tripancha Nayanairyutam,
Baahubhih Dashabhih Yuktam,
Sarvakaamaartha Siddhidam. || 2 ||

Poorvam Tu Vaanaram Vaktram,
Kotisoorya Smaprabham,
Damshtraa Karaala Vadanam,
Bhrukuti Kutilakshanam. || 3 ||

Asyaiva Dakshinam Vaktram,
Naarasimham Mahaadbhutam,
Atyugra Tejovapusham,
Bhishanam Bhayanaashanam. || 4 ||

Pashchimam Gaarudam Vaktram,
Vakratundam Mahaabalam,
Sarvanaaga Prashamanam,
Vishabhootaadi Kruntanam. || 5 ||

Uttaram Soukaram Vaktram,
Krushnam Deeptam Nabhopamam,
Paataala Simha Vetaala,
Jvara Rogaadi Kruntanam. || 6 ||

Oordhvam Hayaananam Ghoram,
Daanava Antakaram Param,
Yena Vaktrena Viprendra,
Taarakaakhyam Maha Asuram. || 7 ||

Jaghaana Sharanam Tatsyaat,
Sarvashatru Haram Param,
Dhyaatvaa Panchamukham Rudram,
Hanumantam Dayaanidhim. || 8 ||

Khadgam Trishulam Khatvaangam,
Paasham Ankusha Parvatam,
Mushtim Kaumodakeem Vruksham,
Dhaarayantam Kamandalum. || 9 ||

Bhindipaalam Gyaanamudraam,
Dashabhih Muni Pungavam,
Etaani Aayudha Jaalaani,
Dhaarayantam Bhajaamyaham. || 10 ||

Pretaasana Upavishtam Tam,
Sarvaabharana Bhooshitam,
Divya Maalya Ambara Dharam,
Divya Gandha Anulepanam,
Sarva Aashcharya Mayam Devam,
Hanuman Vishvato Mukham. || 11 ||

Panchaasyam Achyutam Aneka Vichitra Varna Vaktram,
Shashaamka Shikharam Kapiraajavaryam,
Peetaambaraadi Mukutai Roopa Shobhitaangam,
Pingaaksham Aadyam Anisham Manasaa Smaraami. || 12 ||

Markatesham Mahotsaaham,
Sarvashatruharam Param,
Shatru Samhara Maam Raksha,
Shreeman Aapadam Uddhara. || 13 ||

Om Harimarkata Markata Mamtram Idam,
Parilikhyati Likhyati Vaamatale,
Yadi Nashyati Nashyati Shatrukulam,
Yadi Mumchati Mumchati Vaamalataa. || 14 ||

Om Harimarkataaya Swahaa,
Om Namo Bhagavate,
Panchavadanaaya Poorva Kapimukhaaya,
Sakalashatru Samhaarakaaya Swahaa.

Om Namo Bhagavate Panchavadanaaya,
Dakshina Mukhaaya Karaala,
Vadanaaya Narasimhaaya Sakalabhoota,
Pramathanaaya Swahaa.

Om Namo Bhagavate Panchavadanaaya,
Pashchima Mukhaaya Garudaananaaya,
Sakala Vishaharaaya Swahaa.

Om Namo Bhagavate Panchavadanaaya,
Uttara Mukhaaya Aadi Varaahaaya,
Sakala Sampat Karaaya Swahaa.

Om Namo Bhagavate Panchavadanaaya,
Urdhva Mukhaaya Hayagreevaaya,
Sakalajana Vashankaraaya Swahaa.

Om Shri Panchavadanaaya Anjaneyaaya Namaha.

Meaning in English

Shree Ganeshay Namah

Om prayer to the armour of the great armour of the five faced Hanuman, sage is Brahma, God addressed is the regal five faced Hanuman. Hreem is the root, Shrim is the power, Kroum is the nail. Kroom is the armour and Kraim is the arrow. Thus all directions are tied.
