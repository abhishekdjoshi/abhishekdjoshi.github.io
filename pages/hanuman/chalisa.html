Meaning in English

Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj
Nij Mane Mukure Sudhar
Varnao Raghuvar Vimal Jasu
Jo Dayaku Phal Char
Cleansing the mirror in the form of my mind with the pollen of the lotus-feet of the Guru, I describe the unblemished glory of Rama, which bestows the four fruits of life.

Budhi Hin Tanu Janike
Sumirau Pavan Kumar
Bal Budhi Vidya Dehu Mohe
Harahu Kalesa Vikar
Knowing my body to be devoid of intelligence, I remember Hanuman, the son of Vāyu. Give me strength, intelligence and knowledge and remove all ailments and impurities.

Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar
Jai Kapis Tihun Lok Ujagar || 1 ||
O Hanuman, the ocean of knowledge and virtues, may you be victorious. O the chief amongst Vanaras famous across the three Lokas (Earth, Heaven and Below-the-Earth), may you be victorious.

Ram Doot Atulit Bal Dhama
Anjani-putra Pavan Sut Nama || 2 ||
You are the trusted messenger of Rama and you are the abode of incomparable strength. You are known by the names of Anjaniputra (son of Anjana) and Pavanasuta (son of Vāyu).

Mahavir Vikram Bajrangi
Kumati Nivar Sumati Ke Sangi || 3 ||
You are the great hero, you are endowed with valour, your body is as strong as Indra's Vajra. You are the destroyer of vile intellect, and you are the companion of one whose intellect is pure.

Kanchan Varan Viraj Subesa
Kanan Kundal Kunchit Kesa || 4 ||
Your complexion is that of molten gold, and you are resplendent in your handsome form. You wear Kundalas (small earrings) in your ears and your hair is curly.

Hath Vajra Aur Dhuvaje Viraje
Kandhe moonj janehu sajai|| 5 ||
You have the Vajra and the flag in your hands, and the sacred-thread (Yajnopavita) made of the Munja grass adorns your shoulder.

Sankar suvan kesri Nandan
Tej pratap maha jag vandan || 6 ||
O embodiment of Shiva (or son of Vāyu carrying the power of Shiva), the delighter of Kesari, your aura and majesty is great and is revered by the whole world.

Vidyavan guni ati chatur
Ram kaj karibe ko aatur || 7 ||
You are the praiseworthy abode of the eighteen types of Vidyā (knowledge), all virtues reside in you, and you are exceedingly clever.You are ever eager to perform tasks for Rama.

Prabu charitra sunibe ko rasiya
Ram Lakhan Sita man Basiya || 8 ||
You delight in listening to the acts of Rama (Ramayana). Rama, Lakshmana and Sita reside in your mind. Alternately, you reside in the minds of Rama, Lakshmana and Sita.

Sukshma roop dhari Siyahi dikhava
Vikat roop dhari lanka jarava || 9 ||
You assumed an extremely minute form and appeared to Sita in the Ashok Vatika. You assumed a very large and scary form and burnt the city of Lanka.

Bhima roop dhari asur sanghare
Ramachandra ke kaj sanvare || 10 ||
You assumed a frightening form and destroyed the demons [in the army of Ravana]. You carried out all the tasks of Rama.

Laye Sanjivan Lakhan Jiyaye
Shri Raghuvir Harashi ur laye || 11 ||
You brought the Sanjivini, the life saving herb from Dronagiri in Himalayas, and revitalized Lakshman. Out of elation, Rama embraced you.

Raghupati Kinhi bahut badai
Tum mam priye Bharat-hi sam bhai || 12 ||
Rama, the chief among Raghu's descendants, praised you profusely saying "You are dear to me like my brother Bharata.

Sahas badan tumharo yash gaave
Us kahi Shripati kanth lagaave || 13 ||
Rama also added that a thousand people will praise Hanuman's glory and embraced him again.

Sankadik Brahmadi Muneesa
Narad Sarad sahit Aheesa || 14 ||
When prophets like Sanka, even the Sage like Lord Brahma, the great hermit Narad himself, Goddess Saraswati and Ahisha (one of immeasurable dimensions).

Yam Kuber Digpal Jahan te
Kavi kovid kahi sake kahan te || 15 ||
Even Yamraj (God of Death) Kuber (God of Wealth) and the Digpals (deputies guarding the four corners of the Universe) have been vying with one another in offering homage to your glories. How then, can a mere poet give adequate expression of your super excellence.

Tum upkar Sugreevahin keenha
Ram milaye rajpad deenha|| 16 ||
You did Sugriva a great favour by making him meet Rama and bestowing on him the kingdom.

Tumharo mantra Vibheeshan mana
Lankeshwar Bhaye Sub jag jana || 17 ||
Your Mantra was accepted by Vibishana, as a result of which he became the king of Lanka. The whole world knows this.

Yug sahastra jojan par Bhanu
Leelyo tahi madhur phal janu || 18 ||
On your own you dashed upon the sun, Surya, which is thousands of miles away, thinking it to be a sweet fruit.

Prabhu mudrika meli mukh mahee
Jaladhi langhi gaye achraj nahee || 19 ||
O Lord, placing the ring given by Rama in your mouth, you leaped across the ocean – there is no wonder here.

Durgaam kaj jagat ke jete
Sugam anugraha tumhre tete || 20 ||
All the unattainable tasks in the world become easily attainable with your grace.

Ram dware tum rakhvare
Hoat na agya binu paisare || 21 ||
You are the sentry at the door of Shri Ram's Divine Abode. No one can enter it without your permission.

Sub sukh lahai tumhari sarna
Tum rakshak kahu ko dar na || 22 ||
All comforts of the world lie at your feet. The devotees enjoy all divine pleasures and feel fearless under your benign Protection.

Aapan tej samharo aapai
Teenhon lok hank te kanpai || 23 ||
You alone are befitted to carry your own splendid valour. All the three worlds (entire universe) tremor at your thunderous call.

Bhoot pisach Nikat nahin aavai
Mahavir jab naam sunavai || 24 ||
All the ghosts, demons and evil forces keep away, with the sheer mention of your great name, O'Mahaveer!!

Nase rog harai sab peera
Japat nirantar Hanumant beera - from here|| 25 ||
All diseases, pain and suffering disappear on reciting regularly Shri Hanuman's holy name.

Sankat se Hanuman chudavai
Man Karam Vachan dyan jo lavai || 26 ||
Those who remember Shri Hanuman in thought, words and deeds with sincerity and faith, are rescued from all crises in life.

Sub par Ram tapasvee raja
Tin ke kaj sakal Tum saja || 27 ||
Rama is the supreme God and a king with Tapas, and yet you executed all his tasks.

Aur manorath jo koi lavai
Sohi amit jeevan phal pavai || 28 ||
And whoever comes to you with any wish, that wish is fulfilled beyond limits in this very birth.

Charon Yug partap tumhara
Hai persidh jagat ujiyara || 29 ||
Your glory is famous in all the four Yugas, and illuminates the whole world.

Sadhu Sant ke tum Rakhware
Asur nikandan Ram dulhare || 30 ||
You are the protector of Sadhus (good people or ascetics) and Sants (saints). You are the destroyer of demons and dear as a son to Rama.

Ashta sidhi nav nidhi ke dhata
Us var deen Janki mata || 31 ||
You can grant to any one, any yogic power of Eight Siddhis (power to become light and heavy at will) and Nine Nidhis (Riches,comfort,power,prestige,fame,sweet relationship etc.) This boon has been conferred upon you by Mother Janki.

Ram rasayan tumhare pasa
Sada raho Raghupati ke dasa || 32 ||
You have the treasure of Rama's Bhakti (rāma rasāyana) with you. You are, respectfully, the servant of Raghupati (Shri Raam).

Tumhare bhajan Ram ko pavai
Janam janam ke dukh bisravai || 33 ||
Singing of you (Hanuman), a Bhakta obtains Rama and forgets the adversities and afflictions of many births.

Anth kaal Raghuvir pur jayee
Jahan janam Hari-Bakht Kahayee || 34 ||
As a result of devotion to you, a Bhakta goes to Sāketa Loka (raghubara pura) at the time of their end (physical death). Once the Bhakta reaches Sāketa, wherever they take birth, they are known as the Bhaktas of Hari.

Aur Devta Chit na dharehi
Hanumanth se hi sarve sukh karehi || 35 ||
Even one who does not contemplate on any other Devatas in their mind and only serves Hanuman, achieves all favourable bliss in this world and the next.

Sankat kate mite sab peera
Jo sumirai Hanumat Balbeera || 36 ||
Whoever remembers the brave and mighty Hanuman gets free of all adversities and relief from all pains.

Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Gosahin
Kripa Karahu Gurudev ki nyahin || 37 ||
O Hanuman, the master of senses, may you be victorious, may you be victorious, may you be victorious. May you shower your grace lovingly, as a Guru does, and reveal to me the knowledge of devotion to Rama.

Jo sat bar path kare kohi
Chutehi bandhi maha sukh hohi || 38 ||
One who recites Hanuman Chalisa a hundred times or for hundred days is released from bondage and obtains great bliss.

Jo yah padhe Hanuman Chalisa
Hoye siddhi sakhi Gaureesa || 39 ||
One who reads this Hanuman Chalisa obtains accomplishment or liberation. Shiva himself bears witness to this statement.

Tulsidas sada hari chera
Keejai Das Hrdaye mein dera || 40 ||
Tulsidas is always a devotee of Hari. O Lord, make my heart your abode.

Pavantnai sankat haran
Mangal murti roop
Ram Lakhan Sita sahit
Hrdaye basahu sur bhoop
O Son of Vāyu, remover of adversities, one with an auspicious form, and the chief among all Devas, may you reside in our hearts along with Rama, Lakshman and Sita.
