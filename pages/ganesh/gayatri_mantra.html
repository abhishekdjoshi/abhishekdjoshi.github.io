Ganesha Gayatri Mantra
गणेश गायत्री मंत्र


English

Om Ekadantaya Viddhamahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥1॥

Om Mahakarnaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥2॥

Om Gajananaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥3॥

Om Tatpurshaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥4॥

Om Lambodaraya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥5॥





Devanagari Script

ॐ एकदंताय विद्महे, वक्रतुण्डाय धीमहि, तन्नो दंती प्रचोदयात् ॥1॥

ॐ महाकर्णाय विद्महे, वक्रतुण्डाय धीमहि, तन्नो दंती प्रचोदयात् ॥2॥

ॐ गजाननाय विद्महे, वक्रतुण्डाय धीमहि, तन्नो दंती प्रचोदयात् ॥3॥

ॐ तत्पुरुषाय विद्महे, वक्रतुण्डाय धीमहि, तन्नो दन्ती प्रचोदयात् ॥4॥

ॐ लम्बोदराय विद्धमहे, महोदराय धीमहि, तन्नो दन्ति प्रचोदयात् ॥5॥





Meaning in English

Om Ekadantaya Viddhamahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥1॥
We pray to the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth who is omnipresent. We meditate upon and pray for greater intellect to the Lord with the curved, elephant-shaped trunk. We bow before the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth to illuminate our minds with wisdom.

Om Mahakarnaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥2॥
We pray to the doer of all great things, one who is omnipresent. We meditate upon and pray for greater intellect to the Lord with the curved, elephant-shaped trunk. We bow before the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth to illuminate our minds with wisdom.

Om Gajananaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥3॥
We pray to the elephant headed, Lord Ganesha, one who is omnipresent. We meditate upon and pray for greater intellect to the Lord with the curved, elephant-shaped trunk. We bow before the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth to illuminate our minds with wisdom.

Om Tatpurshaya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥4॥
We pray to the one who is the supreme and teh perfect male and who is omnipresent. We meditate upon and pray for greater intellect to the Lord with the curved, elephant-shaped trunk. We bow before the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth to illuminate our minds with wisdom.

Om Lambodaraya Vidmahe
Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat ॥5॥
We pray to the one with the expandable belly (lambodaraya) who is omnipresent. We meditate upon and pray for greater intellect to the Lord with the curved, elephant-shaped trunk. We bow before the one with the single-tusked elephant tooth to illuminate our minds with wisdom.
