Meaning in English

Om Gajanan-am Bhoot Ganadhi Sevitam
Kapith Jambu Phal Charu Bhakshanam
Uma Sutam Shok Vinash Karakam
Namami Vigneshwar Paad Pankajam
Salutations to Lord Ganesha who has an elephant head, who is attended by the band of his followers, who eats his favorite apple and rose apple fruits, who is the son of Goddess Uma, who is the cause of destruction of all sorrow. And I salute to his feet which are like lotus.

Gayey Ganapati Jag Bandan
Sankar Suvan Bhavani Nandan ॥ 1 ॥
Sing the praises of Lord Ganesha, who is son of Lord Shankara (Shiva) and Goddess Parvati.

Gayey Ganapati Jag Bandan
Siddhi Sadan, Gaj Badan, Vinayak
Krupa Sindhu, Sundar Sab Laayak ॥ 2 ॥
Sing the praises of Lord Ganesha, one who bestows all wishes and one who blesses us with all the successes and makes everything possible for us.

Gayey Ganapati Jag Bandan
Modak Priya, Mudh Managal Daata
Vidya Baaridhi Budhi Vidhata ॥ 3 ॥
Sing the praises of Lord Ganesha, one who loves Modakam, a kind of sweet, and one who gives joy and auspiciousness, and one who is the creator of intelligence and an ocean of skill and knowledge.

Gayey Ganapati Jag Bandan
Maangat Tulsidas Kar Zorey
Basahim Ramsiya Manas Moray ॥ 4 ॥
Sing the praises of Lord Ganesha. Tulsidas requests with hands held together, actioning like Namaste, for Lord Rama and Goddess Sita, to reside in his mind forever.

Gayey Ganapati Jag Bandan
Sing the praises of Lord Ganesha.
