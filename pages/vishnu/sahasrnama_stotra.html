Vishnu Sahasrnama Stotra | विष्णु सहस्त्रनाम स्तोत्र


English

Om Namo Bhgawate Vasudevaya Namah

Om Vishwam Vishnuh Varshakaro Bhoot Bhavya Bhavat Prabhuhu
Bhoot Krut Bhoot Bhrut Bhavo Bhutatma Bhoot Bhavanah
॥ 1 ॥

Putatma Parmatma Ch Muktanam Paramam Gatihi
Avayayah Purush Sakshi Kshetr-gyo Akshar Aev Ch
।। 2 ।।

Yogo Yog-Vidam Neta Pradhan-Pureshwarah
Narsimah-Vapuhu Shriman Keshavah Purushotamah
।। 3 ।।

Sarvah Sharvah Shivah Sthanuhu Bhootadihi Nidhihi Avayayah
Sambhavo Bhavano Bharta Prabhavah Prabhuhu Ishwarah
।। 4 ।।

Swayambhuhu Shambuhu Aaditya-ha Puskaraksho Mahaswanah
Anadi-Nidhano Dhata Vidhata Dhaturutamah
।। 5 ।।

Aprameyo Rishikeshah Padmanabho-Amarprabhu-hu
Vishwakarma Manu-satva-ashta Sthavisht-ha Sthaviro Dhruv-ha
।। 6 ।।

Agrahaha Shaswatah Krishno Lohitaksh-ha Pratardan-ha
Prabhutah Trikkub-Dham Pavitrum Mangalam Param
।। 7।।

Irshanah Pranadah Prano Jyesthah Shresthah Prajapati-hi
Hiranya-Garbho Bhu-Garbho Madhavo Madhusudanah
।। 8 ।।

Ishwaro Vikrami Dhanvi Medhavi Vikram-ha Kram-ha
Anutamo Duradarsh-ha Krutagy-ha Kruti-hi Aatmavaan
।। 9 ।।

Ahas Samvatsaro Vyala-ha Pratyaya-ha Sarva-Darshana-ha

Varshakapi-hi Ameyatma Sarva-yoga-vini-hi Sruta-ha
|| 11 ||

Amogha-ha Pundarikaksho Vrrusha-karma Vrisha-krati-hi
|| 12 ||

Amrut-ha Shaswat-ha Sthanu-hu Vararaho Mahatapa-ha
|| 13 ||

Sarvaga-ha Sarvavid-Bhanu-hu Vishwak-Seno Janardan-ha
Vedo Vedveed-Avayango Vedango Vedveet Kavi-hi
।। 14 ।।

Lokadhyaksh-ha Sueadhyaksho Dharmadhyaksh-ha Kruta-Krut-ha
Chaturatma Chaturvyaha-ha Chaturdasthra-ha Chaturbhuj-ha
।। 15 ।।

Bhrajishnu Bhojanam Bhokta Sahishnu-hu Jagdadij-ha
Anagho Vijayo Jeta Vishwayoni-hi Punarvasu-hu
।। 16 ।।

Upendro Vaman-ha Pranshu-hu Amogh-ha Shuchi-hi Urjit-ha
Atindra-ha Sangrah-ha Sargo Dhrutatma Niyamo Yamah
।। 17 ।।

Vedyo Vaidya-ha Sadayogi Veeraha Madhavo Madhu-hu
Ati-Indriyo Mahamayo Mahotsaaho Mahabala-ha
।। 18 ।।

Mahabudhi-hi Maha-Veeryo Maha-Shakti-hi Maha-Dyuti-hi
Anirdeshya-Vapu-hu Shreeman Ameyatma Mahadri-dhruk
।। 19 ।।

Maheshwaso Mahibharta Shrinivasa-ha Satam Gati-hi
Anirudh-ha Suranando Govindo Govindam-Pati-hi
।। 20 ।।

Marichi-hi Damano Hansa-ha Suparno Bhujgotam-ha
Hiranynabh-ha Sutapa-ha Padmnanabh-ha Prajapati-hi
।। 21 ।।

।। 22 ।।

Guru-hu Gurutamo Dham-ha Satya-ha Satya-Parakram-ha
Nimisho-a-Nimisha-ha Sthragavi Vachaspati-hi Udardhi-hi
।। 23 ।।

।। 25 ।।

Suprasad-ha Prasannatma Vishwa-dhrik Vishwa-bhuk Vibhu-hu
Satkarta Sakrut-ha Sadhu-hu Jahnur Narayano Nar-ha
।। 26 ।।

Asankhyeyo Aprameyatma Vishisht-ha Shisht Krit Suchi-hi
Siddharth-ha Sidhsankalp-ha Siddhidah-ha Siddhi-sadhan-ha
।। 27।।

Varsha-hi Varsha-bho Vishnu-hu Varshaparva Varshodar-ha
Vardhano Vardhmanasch Vivikat-ha Shruti Sagar-ha
।। 28 ।।

Naik Rupo Bruhad Rup-ha Shipivisht-ha Prakashan-ha
।। 29 ।।

Oja-ha Tejo Dhyutidhar-ha Prakash Aatma Pratapan-ha
Ruddah Spashtakshro Mantra-ha Chandranshu-hu Bhaskar-dhyuti-hi
।। 30 ।।

Amritanshu-dravo Bhanu-hu Shashabindu-hu Sureshwar-ha
Aushadham Jagat-ha Setu-hu Satya Dharm Parakram-ha
।। 31 ।।

Bhoot Bhavya Bhavat Nath-ha Pavan-ha Pavano Anala-ha
Kamaha Kamkrut Kant-ha Kaam-ha Kaamprad-ha Prabhu-hu
।। 32 ।।

Adrushyo Vyaktrupasch Sahasrajit-Anantjit
।। 33 ।।

Ishto Vishist-ha Shistesht-ha Shikhandi Nahusho Vrish-ha
Krodaha Krodhkrit Karta Vishwabahu-hu Mahidhar-ha
।। 34 ।।

Achyut-ha Prathit-ha Pran-ha Pranado Vasvaanuj-ha
Apaam Nidhirdhishtanam Apramat-ha Pratishthit-ha
।। 35 ।।

Skand-ha Skand-dharo Dhuryo Varado Vayu-vahan-ha
Vasudevo Brahad Bhanu-hu Adidev-ha Purandar-ha
।। 36 ।।

Ashok-ha Taaran-ha Tar-ha Shur-ha Shouri-hi Janeshwar-ha
Anukul-ha Shatavart-ha Padmi Padminibhekshan-ha
।। 37 ।।

Padmanabho Arvindaksh-ha Padmagarbh-ha Sharirbhrit
Mahardi Riddho Vridhatma Mahaksho Garud-dhwaj-ha
।। 38 ।।

Atul-ha Sharbho Bhim-ha Samyagyo Havirhari-hi
Sarvalakshan Lakshanyo Lakshmivaan Samitimjaya-ha
।। 39 ।।

Viksharo Rohito Margo Hetu-hu Damodar-ha Sah
Mahidharo Mahabhago Vegvaan Amitashan-ha
।। 40 ।।

Udbhav-ha Kshobhno Dev-ha Shrigarbh-ha Parmeshwar-ha
Karanam Kaaranam Karta Vikarta Gahano Guh-ha
।। 41 ।।

Vyavasayo Vyavasthan-ha Sansthan-ha Sthando Dhruv-ha
Parriddhi Paramspasht-ha Tusht-ha Shubhekshan-ha
।। 42 ।।

Ramo Viramo Virajo Margo Neyo Neyo Anaya-ha
Veer-ha Shaktimatam Shresht-ha Dharmo Dharm Vidumat-ha
।। 43 ।।

Vaikunth-ha Purush-ha Praann-ha Praanndah Prannvah Prathu-hu
Hiranyagharbh-ha Shatrughno Vyapto Vaayurdhokshaj-ha
।। 44।।

Rutu-hu Sudarshan-ha Kaal-ha Parameshti Parigraha
Ugra-ha Samvatsaro Daksho Vishramo Vishwa Dakshi-ha
।। 45 ।।

Vistaar-ha Sthavar-ha Sthannu-hu Pramaannam Beejam Vyayam
Artho Anartho Mahakosho Mahabhogo Mahadhan-ha
।। 46 ।।

Anirvinn-ha Sthavishto Abhurdharm Yupo Maha-Makh-ha
Nakshatranemi-hi Nakshatri Ksham-ha Kshaam-ha Samihan-ha
।। 47 ।।

Yagya Ijyo Mahejyasch Kratu-hu Satram Sataam Gati-hi
Sarvadarshi Vimuktatma Sarvgyo Gyaanmutamam
।। 48 ।।

Suvrat-ha Sumukh-ha Sukshm-ha Sughosh-ha Sukhad-ha Suhrut
Manoharo Jit Krodho Veerbaahurvidaarann-ha
।। 49 ।।

Swaapan-ha Swavasho Vyapi Naikatma Naik Karmkrut
Vatsaro Vatsalo Vatsi Ratnagarbho Dhaneshwar-ha
।। 50 ।।

Dharmgub Dharmkrudh Dharmi Sadstksharam Aksharam
Avigyata Sahstranshu-hu Vidhata Krutlakshann-ha
।। 51 ।।

Gabhastinemi-hi Stavstha-ha Simho Bhootmaheshwar-ha
Adidevo Mahadevo Devesho Devbhrud Guru-hu
।। 52 ।।

Uttaro Gopitirgopta Gyaangamya-ha Puraatan-ha
Sharir Bhootbhrudhokta Kapindro Bhuridakshinn-ha
।। 53 ।।

Somapo Amritap-ha Som-ha Purujit Purusatam-ha
Vinayo Jay-ha Satyasandho Daashar-ha Saatvataam Pati-hi
।। 54 ।।

Jivo Vinayita Sakshi Mukundo Amitvikram-ha
Ambhonidhirantatma Mahoddhishayo Antak-ha
।। 55 ।।

Ajo Maharah Swabhaavyo Jitamitra-ha Pramodan-ha
Anando Nandano Nand-ha Satyadharma Trivikram-ha
।। 56 ।।

Maharshi-hi Kapilacharya-ha Kritgyo Medinipati-hi
Tripadstridashadhyaksho Mahashring-ha Kritamatkrit
।। 57 ।।

Mahavaraho Govind-ha Sushenn-ha Kanakaangdi
Guhyo Gambhiro Gahano Guptaschakra Gadadhar-ha
।। 58 ।।

Vedha-ha Swangojit-ha Krishnno Dhrud-ha Sankarshnno Achyut-ha
Varunno Vaarunno Vriksh-ha Pushkaraksho Mahamanaa-ha
।। 59 ।।

Bhagwan Bhagahanandi Vanmaali Halaayudh-ha
Aadityo Jyotiraditya-ha Sahishnnu-hu Gatisatam-ha
।। 60 ।।

Sudhanva Khandparshurdarunno Dravinnprad-ha
Divi-hi Sprik Sarvadrik Vyaso Vaachspati-hi Ayonij-ha
।। 61 ।।

Trisama Samag-ha Saam Nirvannam Bheshajam Bhishak
Sanyaskrit Cham-ha Shanto Nishta Shanti-hi Paraayanam
।। 62 ।।

Shubhang-ha Shantid-ha Srastha Kumud-ha Kuvaleshya-ha
Gohito Gopatirgopta Vrishbhaksho Vrishpriya-ha
।। 63 ।।

Anivarti Nivratatma Sankshepta Kshemkrit Shiv-ha
Shrivatsavksh-ha Shrivas-ha Shripati-hi Shrimatam Var-ha
।। 64 ।।

Shrid-ha Shrish-ha Shrinivas-ha Shrinidhi-hi Shrivibhaavan-ha
Shridhar-ha Shrikar-ha Shrey-ha Shriman Loktrayashraya-ha
।। 65 ।।

Swaksh-ha Swvang-ha Shatanando Nandijyotirganeshwar-ha
Vijitatma Vidheyatma Stakirtischiansanshaya-ha
।। 66 ।।

Udirann-ha Sarvat-ha Chakshurnish-ha Sashwatsthir-ha
Bhushayo Bhushano Bhutirvishok-ha Shoknaashan-ha ।। 67 ।।

Archirshmanirchit-ha Kumbo Vishudhatma Vishodhan-ha
Anirudho Pratirath-ha Pradyumno Mitvikram-ha ।। 68 ।।

Kaalanayminiha Veer-ha Shauri-hi Shurajaneshwar-ha
Trilokatma Trilokesh-ha Keshav-ha Keshiha Hari-hi ।। 69 ।।

Kaamdev-ha Kaampaal-ha Kaami Kaant-ha Kritagam-ha
Anirdeshya-vapurvishnu-hu Viroananto Dhananjaya-ha ।। 70 ।।

Bramhanyo Bramhakrit Bramha Bramh Bramh-Vivardhan-ha
Bramhavid Bramhano Bramhi Bramhagyo Bramhan-Priy-ha ।। 71 ।।

Mahakarmo Mahakarma Mahateja Mahorag-ha
Mahakritur-Mahayajva Mahayagyo Mahahavi-hi ।। 72 ।।

Stavya-ha Stvapriya-ha Stotram Stuti-hi Stota Rann-Priya-ha
Purna-ha Puryita Punya-ha Punyakirtirnaamaya-ha ।। 73 ।।

Manojvasthirkaro Vasureta Vasuprad-ha
Vasuprado Vasudevo Vasurvasu-mana Havi-hi ।। 74 ।।

Sadgatihi Sakriti-hi Sata Sadbhuti-hi Satparaayan-ha
Shurseno Yadushresth-ha Sannivaas-ha Suyaamun-ha ।। 75 ।।

Bhutavaso Vasudev-ha Sarva-Sunilayo-Anal-ha
Darpaha Darpado Drupto Durdharo Athaparajit-ha ।। 76 ।।

Vishwamurti-Mahamurti-hi Diptmurti-hi Amurtimaan
Anekamurtirvyakta-ha Shatmurti-hi Shataanan-ha ।। 77 ।।

ॐ नमो भगवते वासुदेवाय नम:

ॐ विश्वं विष्णु: वषट्कारो भूत-भव्य-भवत-प्रभुः ।
भूत-कृत भूत-भृत भावो भूतात्मा भूतभावनः ।। 1 ।।

पूतात्मा परमात्मा च मुक्तानां परमं गतिः।
अव्ययः पुरुष साक्षी क्षेत्रज्ञो अक्षर एव च ।। 2 ।।

योगो योग-विदां नेता प्रधान-पुरुषेश्वरः ।
नारसिंह-वपुः श्रीमान केशवः पुरुषोत्तमः ।। 3 ।।

सर्वः शर्वः शिवः स्थाणु: भूतादि: निधि: अव्ययः ।
संभवो भावनो भर्ता प्रभवः प्रभु: ईश्वरः ।। 4 ।।

स्वयंभूः शम्भु: आदित्यः पुष्कराक्षो महास्वनः ।
अनादि-निधनो धाता विधाता धातुरुत्तमः ।। 5 ।।

अप्रमेयो हृषीकेशः पद्मनाभो-अमरप्रभुः ।
विश्वकर्मा मनुस्त्वष्टा स्थविष्ठः स्थविरो ध्रुवः ।। 6 ।।

अग्राह्यः शाश्वतः कृष्णो लोहिताक्षः प्रतर्दनः ।
प्रभूतः त्रिककुब-धाम पवित्रं मंगलं परं ।। 7।।

ईशानः प्राणदः प्राणो ज्येष्ठः श्रेष्ठः प्रजापतिः ।
हिरण्य-गर्भो भू-गर्भो माधवो मधुसूदनः ।। 8 ।।

ईश्वरो विक्रमी धन्वी मेधावी विक्रमः क्रमः ।
अनुत्तमो दुराधर्षः कृतज्ञः कृति: आत्मवान ।। 9 ।।

सुरेशः शरणं शर्म विश्व-रेताः प्रजा-भवः ।
अहः संवत्सरो व्यालः प्रत्ययः सर्वदर्शनः ।। 10 ।।

अजः सर्वेश्वरः सिद्धः सिद्धिः सर्वादि: अच्युतः ।
वृषाकपि: अमेयात्मा सर्व-योग-विनिःसृतः ।। 11 ।।

वसु:वसुमनाः सत्यः समात्मा संमितः समः ।
अमोघः पुण्डरीकाक्षो वृषकर्मा वृषाकृतिः ।। 12 ।।

रुद्रो बहु-शिरा बभ्रु: विश्वयोनिः शुचि-श्रवाः ।
अमृतः शाश्वतः स्थाणु: वरारोहो महातपाः ।। 13 ।।

सर्वगः सर्वविद्-भानु:विष्वक-सेनो जनार्दनः ।
वेदो वेदविद-अव्यंगो वेदांगो वेदवित् कविः ।। 14 ।।

लोकाध्यक्षः सुराध्यक्षो धर्माध्यक्षः कृता-कृतः ।
चतुरात्मा चतुर्व्यूह:-चतुर्दंष्ट्र:-चतुर्भुजः ।। 15 ।।

भ्राजिष्णु भोजनं भोक्ता सहिष्णु: जगदादिजः ।
अनघो विजयो जेता विश्वयोनिः पुनर्वसुः ।। 16 ।।

उपेंद्रो वामनः प्रांशु: अमोघः शुचि: ऊर्जितः ।
अतींद्रः संग्रहः सर्गो धृतात्मा नियमो यमः ।। 17 ।।

वेद्यो वैद्यः सदायोगी वीरहा माधवो मधुः।
अति-इंद्रियो महामायो महोत्साहो महाबलः ।। 18 ।।

महाबुद्धि: महा-वीर्यो महा-शक्ति: महा-द्युतिः।
अनिर्देश्य-वपुः श्रीमान अमेयात्मा महाद्रि-धृक ।। 19 ।।

महेष्वासो महीभर्ता श्रीनिवासः सतां गतिः ।
अनिरुद्धः सुरानंदो गोविंदो गोविदां-पतिः ।। 20 ।।

मरीचि:दमनो हंसः सुपर्णो भुजगोत्तमः ।
हिरण्यनाभः सुतपाः पद्मनाभः प्रजापतिः ।। 21 ।।

अमृत्युः सर्व-दृक् सिंहः सन-धाता संधिमान स्थिरः ।
अजो दुर्मर्षणः शास्ता विश्रुतात्मा सुरारिहा ।। 22 ।।

गुरुःगुरुतमो धामः सत्यः सत्य-पराक्रमः ।
निमिषो-अ-निमिषः स्रग्वी वाचस्पति: उदार-धीः ।। 23 ।।

अग्रणी: ग्रामणीः श्रीमान न्यायो नेता समीरणः ।
सहस्र-मूर्धा विश्वात्मा सहस्राक्षः सहस्रपात ।। 24 ।।

आवर्तनो निवृत्तात्मा संवृतः सं-प्रमर्दनः ।
अहः संवर्तको वह्निः अनिलो धरणीधरः ।। 25 ।।

सुप्रसादः प्रसन्नात्मा विश्वधृक्-विश्वभुक्-विभुः ।
सत्कर्ता सकृतः साधु: जह्नु:-नारायणो नरः ।। 26 ।।

असंख्येयो-अप्रमेयात्मा विशिष्टः शिष्ट-कृत्-शुचिः ।
सिद्धार्थः सिद्धसंकल्पः सिद्धिदः सिद्धिसाधनः ।। 27।।

वृषाही वृषभो विष्णु: वृषपर्वा वृषोदरः ।
वर्धनो वर्धमानश्च विविक्तः श्रुति-सागरः ।। 28 ।।

सुभुजो दुर्धरो वाग्मी महेंद्रो वसुदो वसुः ।
नैक-रूपो बृहद-रूपः शिपिविष्टः प्रकाशनः ।। 29 ।।

ओज: तेजो-द्युतिधरः प्रकाश-आत्मा प्रतापनः ।
ऋद्धः स्पष्टाक्षरो मंत्र:चंद्रांशु: भास्कर-द्युतिः ।। 30 ।।

अमृतांशूद्भवो भानुः शशबिंदुः सुरेश्वरः ।
औषधं जगतः सेतुः सत्य-धर्म-पराक्रमः ।। 31 ।।

भूत-भव्य-भवत्-नाथः पवनः पावनो-अनलः ।
कामहा कामकृत-कांतः कामः कामप्रदः प्रभुः ।। 32 ।।

युगादि-कृत युगावर्तो नैकमायो महाशनः ।
अदृश्यो व्यक्तरूपश्च सहस्रजित्-अनंतजित ।। 33 ।।

इष्टो विशिष्टः शिष्टेष्टः शिखंडी नहुषो वृषः ।
क्रोधहा क्रोधकृत कर्ता विश्वबाहु: महीधरः ।। 34 ।।

अच्युतः प्रथितः प्राणः प्राणदो वासवानुजः ।
अपाम निधिरधिष्टानम् अप्रमत्तः प्रतिष्ठितः ।। 35 ।।

स्कन्दः स्कन्द-धरो धुर्यो वरदो वायुवाहनः ।
वासुदेवो बृहद भानु: आदिदेवः पुरंदरः ।। 36 ।।

अशोक: तारण: तारः शूरः शौरि: जनेश्वर: ।
अनुकूलः शतावर्तः पद्मी पद्मनिभेक्षणः ।। 37 ।।

पद्मनाभो-अरविंदाक्षः पद्मगर्भः शरीरभृत ।
महर्धि-ऋद्धो वृद्धात्मा महाक्षो गरुड़ध्वजः ।। 38 ।।

अतुलः शरभो भीमः समयज्ञो हविर्हरिः ।
सर्वलक्षण लक्षण्यो लक्ष्मीवान समितिंजयः ।। 39 ।।

विक्षरो रोहितो मार्गो हेतु: दामोदरः सहः ।
महीधरो महाभागो वेगवान-अमिताशनः ।। 40 ।।

उद्भवः क्षोभणो देवः श्रीगर्भः परमेश्वरः ।
करणं कारणं कर्ता विकर्ता गहनो गुहः ।। 41 ।।

व्यवसायो व्यवस्थानः संस्थानः स्थानदो-ध्रुवः ।
परर्रद्वि परमस्पष्टः तुष्टः पुष्टः शुभेक्षणः ।। 42 ।।

रामो विरामो विरजो मार्गो नेयो नयो-अनयः ।
वीरः शक्तिमतां श्रेष्ठ: धर्मो धर्मविदुत्तमः ।। 43 ।।

वैकुंठः पुरुषः प्राणः प्राणदः प्रणवः पृथुः ।
हिरण्यगर्भः शत्रुघ्नो व्याप्तो वायुरधोक्षजः ।। 44।।

ऋतुः सुदर्शनः कालः परमेष्ठी परिग्रहः ।
उग्रः संवत्सरो दक्षो विश्रामो विश्व-दक्षिणः ।। 45 ।।

विस्तारः स्थावर: स्थाणुः प्रमाणं बीजमव्ययम ।
अर्थो अनर्थो महाकोशो महाभोगो महाधनः ।। 46 ।।

अनिर्विण्णः स्थविष्ठो-अभूर्धर्म-यूपो महा-मखः ।
नक्षत्रनेमि: नक्षत्री क्षमः क्षामः समीहनः ।। 47 ।।

यज्ञ इज्यो महेज्यश्च क्रतुः सत्रं सतां गतिः ।
सर्वदर्शी विमुक्तात्मा सर्वज्ञो ज्ञानमुत्तमं ।। 48 ।।

सुव्रतः सुमुखः सूक्ष्मः सुघोषः सुखदः सुहृत ।
मनोहरो जित-क्रोधो वीरबाहुर्विदारणः ।। 49 ।।

स्वापनः स्ववशो व्यापी नैकात्मा नैककर्मकृत ।
वत्सरो वत्सलो वत्सी रत्नगर्भो धनेश्वरः ।। 50 ।।

धर्मगुब धर्मकृद धर्मी सदसत्क्षरं-अक्षरं ।
अविज्ञाता सहस्त्रांशु: विधाता कृतलक्षणः ।। 51 ।।

गभस्तिनेमिः सत्त्वस्थः सिंहो भूतमहेश्वरः ।
आदिदेवो महादेवो देवेशो देवभृद गुरुः ।। 52 ।।

उत्तरो गोपतिर्गोप्ता ज्ञानगम्यः पुरातनः ।
शरीर भूतभृद्भोक्ता कपींद्रो भूरिदक्षिणः ।। 53 ।।

सोमपो-अमृतपः सोमः पुरुजित पुरुसत्तमः ।
विनयो जयः सत्यसंधो दाशार्हः सात्वतां पतिः ।। 54 ।।

जीवो विनयिता-साक्षी मुकुंदो-अमितविक्रमः ।
अम्भोनिधिरनंतात्मा महोदधिशयो-अंतकः ।। 55 ।।

अजो महार्हः स्वाभाव्यो जितामित्रः प्रमोदनः ।
आनंदो नंदनो नंदः सत्यधर्मा त्रिविक्रमः ।। 56 ।।

महर्षिः कपिलाचार्यः कृतज्ञो मेदिनीपतिः ।
त्रिपदस्त्रिदशाध्यक्षो महाश्रृंगः कृतांतकृत ।। 57 ।।

महावराहो गोविंदः सुषेणः कनकांगदी ।
गुह्यो गंभीरो गहनो गुप्तश्चक्र-गदाधरः ।। 58 ।।

वेधाः स्वांगोऽजितः कृष्णो दृढः संकर्षणो-अच्युतः ।
वरूणो वारुणो वृक्षः पुष्कराक्षो महामनाः ।। 59 ।।

भगवान भगहानंदी वनमाली हलायुधः ।
आदित्यो ज्योतिरादित्यः सहिष्णु:-गतिसत्तमः ।। 60 ।।

सुधन्वा खण्डपरशुर्दारुणो द्रविणप्रदः ।
दिवि:स्पृक् सर्वदृक व्यासो वाचस्पति:अयोनिजः ।। 61 ।।

त्रिसामा सामगः साम निर्वाणं भेषजं भिषक ।
संन्यासकृत्-छमः शांतो निष्ठा शांतिः परायणम ।। 62 ।।

शुभांगः शांतिदः स्रष्टा कुमुदः कुवलेशयः ।
गोहितो गोपतिर्गोप्ता वृषभाक्षो वृषप्रियः ।। 63 ।।

अनिवर्ती निवृत्तात्मा संक्षेप्ता क्षेमकृत्-शिवः ।
श्रीवत्सवक्षाः श्रीवासः श्रीपतिः श्रीमतां वरः ।। 64 ।।

श्रीदः श्रीशः श्रीनिवासः श्रीनिधिः श्रीविभावनः ।
श्रीधरः श्रीकरः श्रेयः श्रीमान्-लोकत्रयाश्रयः ।। 65 ।।

स्वक्षः स्वंगः शतानंदो नंदिर्ज्योतिर्गणेश्वर: ।
विजितात्मा विधेयात्मा सत्कीर्तिश्छिन्नसंशयः ।। 66 ।।

उदीर्णः सर्वत:चक्षुरनीशः शाश्वतस्थिरः ।
भूशयो भूषणो भूतिर्विशोकः शोकनाशनः ।। 67 ।।

अर्चिष्मानर्चितः कुंभो विशुद्धात्मा विशोधनः ।
अनिरुद्धोऽप्रतिरथः प्रद्युम्नोऽमितविक्रमः ।। 68 ।।

कालनेमिनिहा वीरः शौरिः शूरजनेश्वरः ।
त्रिलोकात्मा त्रिलोकेशः केशवः केशिहा हरिः ।। 69 ।।

कामदेवः कामपालः कामी कांतः कृतागमः ।
अनिर्देश्यवपुर्विष्णु: वीरोअनंतो धनंजयः ।। 70 ।।

ब्रह्मण्यो ब्रह्मकृत् ब्रह्मा ब्रह्म ब्रह्मविवर्धनः ।
ब्रह्मविद ब्राह्मणो ब्रह्मी ब्रह्मज्ञो ब्राह्मणप्रियः ।। 71 ।।

महाक्रमो महाकर्मा महातेजा महोरगः ।
महाक्रतुर्महायज्वा महायज्ञो महाहविः ।। 72 ।।

स्तव्यः स्तवप्रियः स्तोत्रं स्तुतिः स्तोता रणप्रियः ।
पूर्णः पूरयिता पुण्यः पुण्यकीर्तिरनामयः ।। 73 ।।

मनोजवस्तीर्थकरो वसुरेता वसुप्रदः ।
वसुप्रदो वासुदेवो वसुर्वसुमना हविः ।। 74 ।।

सद्गतिः सकृतिः सत्ता सद्भूतिः सत्परायणः ।
शूरसेनो यदुश्रेष्ठः सन्निवासः सुयामुनः ।। 75 ।।

भूतावासो वासुदेवः सर्वासुनिलयो-अनलः ।
दर्पहा दर्पदो दृप्तो दुर्धरो-अथापराजितः ।। 76 ।।

विश्वमूर्तिमहार्मूर्ति:दीप्तमूर्ति: अमूर्तिमान ।
अनेकमूर्तिरव्यक्तः शतमूर्तिः शताननः ।। 77 ।।

एको नैकः सवः कः किं यत-तत-पद्मनुत्तमम ।
लोकबंधु: लोकनाथो माधवो भक्तवत्सलः ।। 78 ।।

सुवर्णोवर्णो हेमांगो वरांग: चंदनांगदी ।
वीरहा विषमः शून्यो घृताशीरऽचलश्चलः ।। 79 ।।

अमानी मानदो मान्यो लोकस्वामी त्रिलोकधृक ।
सुमेधा मेधजो धन्यः सत्यमेधा धराधरः ।। 80 ।।

तेजोवृषो द्युतिधरः सर्वशस्त्रभृतां वरः ।
प्रग्रहो निग्रहो व्यग्रो नैकश्रृंगो गदाग्रजः ।। 81 ।।

चतुर्मूर्ति: चतुर्बाहु:श्चतुर्व्यूह:चतुर्गतिः ।
चतुरात्मा चतुर्भाव:चतुर्वेदविदेकपात ।। 82 ।।

समावर्तो-अनिवृत्तात्मा दुर्जयो दुरतिक्रमः ।
दुर्लभो दुर्गमो दुर्गो दुरावासो दुरारिहा ।। 83।।

शुभांगो लोकसारंगः सुतंतुस्तंतुवर्धनः ।
इंद्रकर्मा महाकर्मा कृतकर्मा कृतागमः ।। 84 ।।

उद्भवः सुंदरः सुंदो रत्ननाभः सुलोचनः ।
अर्को वाजसनः श्रृंगी जयंतः सर्वविज-जयी ।। 85 ।।

सुवर्णबिंदुरक्षोभ्यः सर्ववागीश्वरेश्वरः ।
महाह्रदो महागर्तो महाभूतो महानिधः ।। 86 ।।

कुमुदः कुंदरः कुंदः पर्जन्यः पावनो-अनिलः ।
अमृतांशो-अमृतवपुः सर्वज्ञः सर्वतोमुखः ।। 87 ।।

सुलभः सुव्रतः सिद्धः शत्रुजिच्छत्रुतापनः ।
न्यग्रोधो औदुंबरो-अश्वत्थ:चाणूरांध्रनिषूदनः ।। 88 ।।

सहस्रार्चिः सप्तजिव्हः सप्तैधाः सप्तवाहनः ।
अमूर्तिरनघो-अचिंत्यो भयकृत्-भयनाशनः ।। 89 ।।

आधारनिलयो-धाता पुष्पहासः प्रजागरः ।

भूर्भवः स्वस्तरुस्तारः सविता प्रपितामहः ।

Om Namo Bhgawate Vasudevaya Namah

5. Bhoot Krut: The creator and destroyer of all existences in the universe.
6. Bhoot Bhrut: One who supports or sustains or governs the universe.
7. Bhavo: Pure existence.

Putatma Parmatma Ch Muktanam Paramam Gatihi

।। 2 ।।
10. Putatma: One whose nature is purity/who is purity.
11. Parmatma: He who is the supreme one and the Atman.
12. Muktanam Paramam Gatihi: The highest goal of the liberated ones.
13. Avayayah: One for whom there is no decay.
14. Purush: One who abides in the body or pura.
15. Sakshi: One who witnesses everything.
16. Kshetr-gyo: The knower of the field or body.
17. Akshar: He who is without destruction.


Yogo Yog-Vidam Neta Pradhan-Pureshwarah
Narsimah-Vapuhu Shriman Keshavah Purushotamah
।। 3 ।।
18. Yogo: One attainable through Yoga.
19. Yog-Vidam Neta: The master of those who are established in the above-mentioned Yoga.
20. Pradhan-Pureshwarah: The master of pradhana or Prakriti and Purusha or Jiva.
21. Narsimah-Vapuhu: One in whom the bodies of a man and a lion are combined.
22. Shriman: One on whose chest the goddess Shri always dwells.
23. Keshavah: One whose Kesa or locks are beautiful.
24. Purushotamah: The greatest among all Purushas.

Sarvah Sharvah Shivah Sthanuhu Bhootadihi Nidhihi Avayayah
Sambhavo Bhavano Bharta Prabhavah Prabhuhu Ishwarah
।। 4 ।।
25. Sarvah: The omniscient source of all existence.
26. Sharvah: Destroyer.
27. Shivah: One pure.
28. Sthanuhu: One who is steady, immovable and changeless.
29. Bhootadihi: Source of all elements or existing things.
30. Nidhihi Avayayah: The changeless and indestructible Being in whom the whole universe becomes merged and remains in seminal condition at the time of Pralaya or cosmic dissolution.
31. Sambhavo: One born out of His own will as incarnation.
32. Bhavano: One who generates the fruits or Karmas of all Jivas for them to enjoy.
33. Bharta: One who supports the universe as its substratum.
34. Prabhavah: One from whom all the great elements have their birth. Or one who has exalted births as incarnations.
35. Prabhuhu: One who is an adept in all rites.
36. Ishwarah: One who has unlimited lordliness or power over all things.

Swayambhuhu Shambuhu Aaditya-ha Puskaraksho Mahaswanah
Anadi-Nidhano Dhata Vidhata Dhaturutamah
।। 5 ।।
37. Swayambhuhu: One who exists by Himself, uncaused by any other.
38. Shambuhu: One who bestows happiness on devotees.
39. Aaditya-ha: The golden-hued person in the sun's orb.
40. Puskaraksho: One who has eyes resembling the petals of Pushkara or lotus.
41. Mahaswanah: One from whom comes the great sound – the Veda.
42. Anadi-Nidhano: The one existence that has neither birth nor death.
43. Dhata: One who is the support of the universe.
44. Vidhata: He who generates Karmas and their fruits.
45. Dhaturutamah: The ultimate support of every thing.


Aprameyo Rishikeshah Padmanabho-Amarprabhu-hu
Vishwakarma Manu-satva-ashta Sthavisht-ha Sthaviro Dhruv-ha
।। 6 ।।
46. Aprameyo: One who is not measurable or understandable by any of the accepted means of knowledge like sense, perception, inference etc.

51. Manu: He who thinks.
52. Satva-ashta: He who makes all beings shrunken (Tanukarana) at the time of cosmic dissolution.
53. Sthavisht-ha: He who excels in everything in bulk or substantiality.
54. Sthaviro Dhruv-ha: Eternal One, being the most ancient. It is taken as a single phrase, the name along with its qualification.


Agrahaha Shaswatah Krishno Lohitaksh-ha Pratardan-ha
Prabhutah Trikkub-Dham Pavitrum Mangalam Param
।। 7।।
55. Agrahaha: One who cannot be grasped by the organs or knowledge or conceived by the mind.

62. Pavitrum: That which purifies everything.
63. Mangalam Param: Supremely auspicious.

Hiranya-Garbho Bhu-Garbho Madhavo Madhusudanah

64. Irshanah: He who controls and regulates everything.
65. Pranadah: One who bestows or activates the Prana, the vital energy.

72. Madhavo: The Consort of Ma or Mahalakshmi or one who is fit to be known through Madhu-Vidya.

74. Ishwaro: The Omnipotent Being.

81. Duradarsh-ha: One whom none (Asuras) can overcome.
82. Krutagy-ha: One who knows everything about what has been done (Kruta) by Jivas. Also one who is pleased even with those who offer such simple offerings as leaves, flowers, fruits and water.

84. Aatmavaan: One established in his own greatness i.e. requiring no other support than Himself.

93. Pratyaya-ha: One who is of the nature of Pratiti or Prajna (consciousness).
94. Sarva-Darshana-ha: One with eyes everywhere. As the Lord has assumed all forms, the eye-sight of all beings is His.

101. Varshakapi-hi: One who shwers all objects of desire.

103. Sarva-yoga-vini-hi Sruta-ha: One who stands aside completely from all bondage.

113. Vrisha-krati-hi: One who takes form for the sake of Vrushas or Dharma.

Rudro Bahu-Shira Babhru-hu Vishwayoni-hi Shuchi-Shrava-ha

120. Shaswat-ha Sthanu-hu: One who is both eternal and firmly established, unchanging.
121. Vararaho: He whose lap gives the highest blessings.
122. Mahatapa-ha: The austerity connected with creation, which is of the nature of knowledge is of great potency.

150. Punarvasu-hu: One who dwells again and again in the bodies as the Jivas

157. Atindra-ha: One who is superior to Indra by His inherent attributes like omnipotence, omniscience etc.

179. Ameyatma: The Spirit with intelligence that cannot be measured by any one.

187. Govindo: Gau means words. Thou pervadest all words, giving them power. Therefore sages call the Govinda.

913. Shishir-ha: One who is the shelter to those who are bruning in the three types of wordly fires - sufferings arising from material causes, psychological causes and spiritual causes.
914. Sharvrikar-ha: For those in bondage, the Atman is like Sarvari (night) and for an enlightened one the state of samsara is like night (Sarvari). So the Lord is called the one who generates Sarvari or night for both the enlightened and the bound ones.

Akrur-ha Peshalo Deksho Dakshin-ha Kshminnam Var-ha
Vidvatamo Vitbhaya-ha Punyashravan Kirtan-ha
।। 98 ।।
915. Akrur-ha: One who is without cruelty.
916. Peshalo: One who is handsome in regard to His actions, mind, word and body.
917. Deksho: One who is fullgrown, strong and does every thing quickly, such a person is Daksha.
918. Dakshin-ha: This word is also means the same as the above Nama.
919. Kshminnam Var-ha: The greatest among the patient ones, because He is more patient than all Yogis noted for patience.
920. Vidvatamo: He who has got the unsurpassable and allinclusive knowledge of everything.
921. Vitbhaya-ha: One who, being eternally free and the Lord of all, is free from the fear of trnsmigratory life.
922. Punyashravan Kirtan-ha: One to hear about whom and to sing of whom is meritorious.

Uttarano Dushkritaha Punyo Duswapan Nashan-ha
Veeraha Rakshan-ha Santo Jeevan-ha Paryavasthit-ha
।। 99 ।।
923. Uttarano: One who takes beings over to the other shore of the ocean of Samsara.
924. Dushkritaha: One who effaces the evil effects of evil actions. Or one who destroys those who perform evil.
925. Punyo: One who bestows holiness on those who remember and adore Him.
926. Duswapan Nashan-ha: When adored and meditated upon, He saves one from dreams foreboding danger. Hence He is called so.
927. Veeraha: One who frees Jivas from bondage and thus saves them from the various transmigratory paths by bestowing liberation on them.
928. Rakshan-ha: One who, assuming the Satvaguna, protects all the three worlds.
929. Santo: Those who adopt the virtuous path are called good men (Santah).
930. Jeevan-ha: One who supports the lives of all beings as Prana.
931. Paryavasthit-ha: One who remains pervading everywhere in this universe.

Anantrupo Anantshri-hi Jitmanyu-hu Bhayapah
Chaturshro Gambhiratma Vidisho Vyadisho Dish-ha
।। 100 ।।
932. Anantrupo: One who has innumerable forms, as He dwells in this all-comprehending universe.
933. Anantshri-hi: One whose Shri (glory) is infinite.
934. Jitmanyu-hu: One who has overcome anger.
935. Bhayapah: One who destroys the fears of beings from Samsara.
936. Chaturshro: One who is just, because He bestows on Jivas the fruits of their Karma.
937. Gambhiratma: One whose nature is unfathomable.
938. Vidisho: One who distributes various furits of actions to persons differing in their forms according to competency.
939. Vyadisho: One who gives to Indra and other deities directions according to their varied functions.
940. Dish-ha: One who in the form of the Vedas bestows the fruits of their ritualistic actions on different beings.

Anadi Bhurbhurvo Lakshmi-hi Suveero Ruchirangad-ha
Janano Janjanmadi-hi Bhimo Bhim Parakram-ha
।। 101 ।।
941. Anadi: One who has no beginning because He is the ultimate cause of all.
942. Bhurbhurvo: 'Bhu' means support. One who is the support (Bhu) of even the earth, which is known to support all things.
943. Lakshmi-hi: He who is the bestower of all that is auspicious to the earth besides being its supporter.
944. Suveero: One who has many brilliant ways of manifestation.
945. Ruchirangad-ha: One who has very attractive armlets.
946. Janano: One who gives brith to living beings.
947. Janjanmadi-hi: One who is the root cause of the origin of Jivas that come to have embodiment.
948. Bhimo: One who is the cause of fear.
949. Bhim Parakram-ha: One whose power and courage in His incarnations were a cause of fear for the Asuras.

Aadharnilayo Dhata Pushphaas-ha Prajagar-ha
Udhvarg-ha Satpthachar-ha Pranad-ha Pranav-ha Pan-ha
।। 102 ।।
950. Aadharnilayo: One who is the support of even all the basic supporting factors like the five elements - Ether, Air, Fire, Water and Earth.
951. Dhata: One who is one's own support and therefore does not require another support.
952. Pushphaas-ha: One whose manifestation as the universe resembles the Hasa or blooming of buds into flowers.
953. Prajagar-ha: One who is particularly awake, because He is eternal Awareness.
954. Udhvarg-ha: One who is above everything.
955. Satpthachar-ha: One who follows the conduct of the good.
956. Pranad-ha: One who givesback life to dead ones as in the case of Parikshit.
957. Pranav-ha: Pranava (Om) the manifesting sound symbol of Brahman. As He is inseparably related with Pranava, He is called Pranava.
958. Pan-ha: It comes from the root 'Prana' meaning transaction. So one who bestows the fruits of Karma on all according to their Karma.

Pramanam Prannilay-ha Pranbhrut Pranjivan-ha
Tatvam Tatvavid Ekatma Janma-mrityu Jaratig-ha
।। 103 ।।
959. Pramanam: One who is self-certifying, as He is Pure Consciousness.
960. Prannilay-ha: The home or dissolving ground of the Pranas.
961. Pranbhrut: One who strengthens the Pranas as food (Anna).
962. Pranjivan-ha: He who keeps alive human beings with Vayus (airs) known as Prana, Apana etc.
963. Tatvam: Means Brahman, just as words like Amruta, Satya, Paramartha, etc.
964. Tatvavid: One who knowns His own true nature.
965. Ekatma: One who is the sole being and the spirit (Atma) in all.
966. Janma-mrityu Jaratig-ha: One who subsists without being subject to the six kinds of transformations - being born, existing, temporarily, growing, transforming, decaying and dying.

Bhubharv-ha Swastarustar-ha Savita Prapitamah
Yagyo Yagyapati Yarjva Yagyango Yagyavaahan-ha
।। 104 ।।
967. Bhubharv-ha Swastarustar-ha: The three Vyahrutis Bhuh, Bhuvah, Svah are said to be the essence of the Veda.
968. Tar-ha: One who helps Jivas to go across the ocean of Samsara.
969. Savita: He who generates all the worlds.
970. Prapitamah: One who is the father of Brahma and therefore the grandfather of all.
971. Yagyo: One who is of the form of Yajna.
972. Yagyapati: One who is the protector and the master of the Yajnas.
973. Yarjva: One who manifests as the performer of a Yajna.
974. Yagyango: All the parts of His body as the incarnate Cosmic Boar are identified with the parts of a yajna.
975. Yagyavaahan-ha: One who supports the Yajna which yield various fruits.

Yagyabhrut Yagyakrut Yagyi Yagyabhuk Yagyasaadhan-ha
Yagyantkrut Yagyaguham Annam Anaad Aev Ch
।। 105 ।।
976. Yagyabhrut: He is so called, because He is the protector and supporter of all Yajna.
977. Yagyakrut: One who performs Yajna at the beginnig and end of the world.
978. Yagyi: One who is the Principal.
979. Yagyabhuk: One who is the enjoyer of Yajna or Protector of Yajna.
980. Yagyasaadhan-ha: One to whom the Yagya is the approach.
981. Yagyantkrut: One who is the end or the fruits of Yajna.
982. Yagyaguham: The Gyana Yajna or the sacrifice of knowledge, which is the esoteric (Guhyam) of all the Yajnams.
983. Annam: That which is eaten by living beings. Or He who eats all beings.
984. Anaad Aev Ch: One who is the eater of the whole world as food. The word Eva is added to show that He is also Anna, the food eaten.

Atmayoni-hi Swayamjaato Vaikhan-ha Saamgayan-ha
Devakinandan-ha Shrashta Kshitish-ha Paapnashan-ha
।। 106 ।।
985. Atmayoni-hi: One who is the source of all; that is, there is no material cause other than Himself for the universe.
986. Swayamjaato: He is also the instrumental cause.
987. Vaikhan-ha: One who excavated the earth, taking a unique form.
988. Saamgayan-ha: One who recites the Sama chants.
989. Devakinandan-ha: The Son of Devaki in the incarnation as Krishna.
990. Shrashta: The creator of all the worlds.
991. Kshitish-ha: A master of the world. Here it denotes Rama.
992. Paapnashan-ha: He who destroys the sins of those who adore Him, meditate upon Him, remember and sing hymns of praise on Him.

Shankhbhri Nandaki Chakri Shardang Dhanva Gadadhar-ha
Rath-Angpaan Nirkshobhya-ha Sarva-Praharnayudh-ha
।। 107 ।।
993. Shankhbhri: One who sports the conch known as Panchajanya, which stands for Tamasahamkara, of which the five elements are born.
994. Nandaki: One who has in His hand the sword known as Nandaka, which stands for Vidya (spiritual illumination).
995. Chakri: One who sports the discus known as Sudarshana, which stands for the Rajasahamkara, out of which the Indriyas have come.
996. Shardang Dhanva: One who aims His Sarnga bow.
997. Gadadhar-ha: One who has the mace known as the Kaumodaki, which stands for the category of Buddhi.
998. Rath-Angpaan: One in whose hand is a wheel (Chakra).
999. Nirkshobhya-ha: One who cannot be upset by anything, because He controls all the above-mentioned weapons.
1000. Sarva-Praharnayudh-ha: There is no rule that the Lord has got only the above- mentioned weapons. All things, which can be used for contacting or striking, are His weapons.

Sarvapraharnayudh Om Nam-ha It
Here I bow to the Lord. There is no rule that the Lord has got only the above- mentioned weapons. All things, which can be used for contacting or striking, are His weapons.

Vanmali Gadi Shardangi Shankhi Chakri Ch Nandaki
Shreeman Narayano Vishnu-hu Vasudevo Amirkshatu
।। 108 ।।
Protect us O' Lord Narayana, who wears the forest garland, who has the mace, conch, sword and the wheel. And who is called Vishnu and the Vasudeva.
