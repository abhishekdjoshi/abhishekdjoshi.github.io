Vishnu Gayatri Mantra
विष्णु गायत्री मंत्र


This is the lyrics of Vishnu Gayatri Mantra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the meaning of Vishnu Gayatri Mantra in English. The Vishnu Gayatri Mantra is read to please Lord Vishnu.




English

Om Shri Vishnave Cha Vidmahe Vasudevay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishno-ho Prachodayat || 1 ||

Om Trailokya Mohanay Vidmahe Aatmaramay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishnum Prachodayat || 2 ||

Om Narayanay Cha Vidmahe Vasudevay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishno-ho Prachodayat || 3 ||





Devanagari Script

ॐ श्रीविष्णवे च विद्मिहे वासुदेवाय धीमहि |
तन्नो: विष्णोः प्रचोदयात || 1 ||

ॐ त्रैलोक्यमोहनाय विद्मिहे आत्मारामाय धीमहि |
तन्नो: विष्णुं प्रचोदयात || 2 ||

ॐ नारायणाय च विद्मिहे वासुदेवाय धीमहि |
तन्नो: विष्णोः प्रचोदयात || 3 ||





Meaning in English

Om Shri Vishnave Cha Vidmahe Vasudevay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishno-ho Prachodayat || 1 ||
Om, Let me meditate on lord Vishnu, Oh, lord Vasudeva, give me higher intellect. Let lord Vishnu illuminate my mind.

Om Trailokya Mohanay Vidmahe Aatmaramay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishnum Prachodayat || 2 ||
Om, Let me meditate on lord Vishnu, the lord of all three lokas, Oh, lord Atmarama, give me higher intellect. Let lord Vishnu illuminate my mind.

Om Narayanay Cha Vidmahe Vasudevay Dhimahi.
Tanno-ho Vishno-ho Prachodayat || 3 ||
Om, Let me meditate on lord Narayana, Oh, lord Vasudeva, give me higher intellect. Let lord Vishnu illuminate my mind.
