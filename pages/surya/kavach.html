Surya Kavach Stotra
सूर्य कवच स्तोत्र


This is the lyrics of Surya Kavach Stotra in English and Devanagari script. You will also find the Surya Kavach Stotra meaning in English. The Surya Kavach Stotra is read to please Lord Surya/Sun God.




English

॥ Atha Shri Surya Kavach Stotram ॥

Shri Ganeshaya Namah. Yagya Valkya Uvach.

Shrunushwa Muni Shardhoola,
Suryasya Kavacham Shubham,
Shareer Arogyadam Divyam,
Sarva Saubaghya Dhayakam. ॥1॥

Daidipyamana Mukutam,
Sphuran Makara Kundalam,
Dhyathwaa Sahasra Kiranam,
Stotram Aetha Dudheerayeth. ॥2॥

Shiro May Bhaskara Pathu,
Lalate Mayamitha Dhyuthi,
Nethre Dhinamanihi Pathu,
Shravane Vasareshwara. ॥3॥

Ghranam Dharma Grunihi Pathu,
Vadanam Veda Vahanaha,
Jihwaa May Manadha Pathu,
Kantham May Suravandithaha. ॥4॥

Skandho Prabakaram Pathu,
Vaksha Pathu Janapriya,
Pathu Padou Dwadasha Atma,
Sarvangam Sakaleswara. ॥5॥

Surya Rakshathmakam Stotram,
Likhithwa Bhurja Pathrake,
Dadhaathi Yaha Kare Thasya,
Vasagaha Sarva Sidhaya. ॥6॥

Susnatho Ya Japeth Asmyaka,
Yodhithe Swastha Manasaha,
Sa Roga Muktho Dheergaayuhu,
Sukham Pushtim Cha Vindathi. ॥7॥

Iti Srimad Yagna Valkya Muni Virachitham,
Surya Kavacha Stotram Sampoornam.





Devanagari Script

॥ अथ श्री सूर्य कवच स्तोत्रम् ॥

श्री गणेशाय नमः । याज्ञवल्क्य उवाच ।

श्रुणुष्व मुनिशार्दूल सूर्यस्य कवचं शुभम् ।
शरीरारोग्यदं दिव्यं सर्व सौभाग्यदायकम् ॥1॥

दैदिप्यमानं मुकुटं स्फ़ुरन्मकरकुण्डलम् ।
ध्यात्वा सहस्रकिरणं स्तोत्रमेतदुदीरयेत् ॥2॥

शिरो मे भास्करः पातु ललाटे मेमितद्दुतिः ।
नेत्रे दिनमणिः पातु श्रवणे वासरेश्वरः ॥3॥

घ्राणं धर्म धृणिः पातु वदनं वेदवाहनः ।
जिह्वां मे मानदः पातु कंठं मे सुरवंदितः ॥4॥

स्कंधौ प्रभाकरं पातु वक्षः पातु जनप्रियः ।
पातु पादौ द्वादशात्मा सर्वागं सकलेश्वरः ॥5॥

सूर्यरक्षात्मकं स्तोत्रं लिखित्वा भूर्जपत्रके ।
दधाति यः करे तस्य वशगाः सर्वसिद्धयः ॥6॥

सुस्नातो यो जपेत्सम्यक् योधीते स्वस्थ मानसः ।
स रोगमुक्तो दीर्घायुः सुखं पुष्टिं च विंदति ॥7॥

॥ इति श्री माद्याज्ञवल्क्यमुनिविरचितं सूर्यकवचस्तोत्रं संपूर्णं ॥





Meaning in English

॥ Atha Shri Surya Kavach Stotram ॥
Shri Ganeshaya Namah. Yagya Valkya Uvach.
Here begins the Surya Kavach Stotra. Salutations to Lord Ganesha, who is worhsipped whenever any new thing is started. And, salutations to Sage Yagnavalkya.

Shrunushwa Muni Shardhoola,
Suryasya Kavacham Shubham,
Shareer Arogyadam Divyam,
Sarva Saubaghya Dhayakam. ॥1॥
Please hear oh most powerful among all sages, the holy armour addressed to Lord Sun or Sun God, which improves health of the body and is divine and one which grants all types of luck.

Daidipyamana Mukutam,
Sphuran Makara Kundalam,
Dhyathwaa Sahasra Kiranam,
Stotram Aetha Dudheerayeth. ॥2॥
After meditating on the shining God adorned with a crown, and shining with golden ear studs which are fish-shaped, this prayer to the God with the thousand rays should be recited.

Shiro May Bhaskara Pathu,
Lalate Mayamitha Dhyuthi,
Nethre Dhinamanihi Pathu,
Shravane Vasareshwara. ॥3॥
Let the God of light protect my head and let God with great brilliance protect my forehead. Let the God, who is the king of the day protect my eyes and let God of the day protect my ears.

Ghranam Dharma Grunihi Pathu,
Vadanam Veda Vahanaha,
Jihwaa May Manadha Pathu,
Kantham May Suravandithaha. ॥4॥
Let my nose be protected by protector of Dharma and let my face be protected by he who rides on Vedas. Let my toungue be protected by destroyer of pride and let my neck be protected by he who is saluted by other Gods.

Skandho Prabakaram Pathu,
Vaksha Pathu Janapriya,
Pathu Padou Dwadasha Atma,
Sarvangam Sakaleswara. ॥5॥
Let the maker of light protect my five object of senses, and let the darling of the people protect my chest. Let the God with twelve souls protect my feet, and let all my body parts be protected by this God of all.

Surya Rakshathmakam Stotram,
Likhithwa Bhurja Pathrake,
Dadhaathi Yaha Kare Thasya,
Vasagaha Sarva Sidhaya. ॥6॥
If this prayer of protection of Sun God is written in burja leaves, and given to someone, he would be under your control, and you would become an expert on spiritual powers.

Susnatho Ya Japeth Asmyaka,
Yodhithe Swastha Manasaha,
Sa Roga Muktho Dheergaayuhu,
Sukham Pushtim Cha Vindathi. ॥7॥
If this is read by one who is purified after performing his rituals, from the very beginning he will have a peaceful mind without worries, free of diseases, and will live long life and lead a life of immense pleasure and health.

Iti Srimad Yagna Valkya Muni Virachitham,
Surya Kavacha Stotram Sampoornam.
Thus ends the Surya Kavacham Stotra which is written by Sage Yagnavalkya.
